# 帖撒罗尼迦前书 导读 / 1 Thessalonians Guide

## 四、在末世盼望中的圣洁生活（5章）

第五章延续主再来的主题，但切入角度改变了——不再是"何时来"，而是"怎样活"。"主的日子来到，好像夜间的贼一样"（5:2），所以"我们不要睡觉，像别人一样，总要儆醒谨守"（5:6）。

"你们要常常喜乐，不住地祷告，凡事谢恩；因为这是神在基督耶稣里向你们所定的旨意。"（帖撒罗尼迦前书 5:16-18）

"Rejoice evermore. Pray without ceasing. In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you." (1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, KJV)

这三个命令是整个基督徒生活的缩影：喜乐、祷告、感谢。三者相辅相成——常常喜乐使人能感谢，不住祷告使人与神连接，凡事感谢使人保持心的敞开。保罗补充说："不要消灭圣灵的感动；不要藐视先知的讲论。但要凡事察验，善美的要持守，各等恶事要禁戒不做"（5:19-22）。全书最后以祝福作结："愿赐平安的神亲自使你们全然成圣！你们的灵与魂与体得蒙保守，在我主耶稣基督降临的时候，完全无可指摘。"（5:23）

### IV. Holy Living in Eschatological Hope (Ch. 5)

Chapter 5 continues the theme of the Lord's return but from a different angle — not "when" but "how to live." "The day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night" (5:2), so "let us not sleep, as do others; but let us watch and be sober" (5:6).

"Rejoice evermore. Pray without ceasing. In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you." (1 Thessalonians 5:16-18, KJV)

These three commands summarize the entire Christian life: joy, prayer, thanksgiving. They are mutually reinforcing — constant joy enables thanksgiving, unceasing prayer maintains connection with God, and thanksgiving in everything keeps the heart open. Paul adds: "Quench not the Spirit. Despise not prophesyings. Prove all things; hold fast that which is good. Abstain from all appearance of evil" (5:19-22, KJV). The letter ends with a blessing: "And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ." (5:23, KJV)
