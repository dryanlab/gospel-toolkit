## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。第二十五章是巴兰故事最黑暗的尾声——也是整本民数记最令人痛心的事件之一。巴兰无法用嘴巴咒诅以色列——于是他换了一种武器：摩押女子和外邦的偶像。

**什亭的淫乱（1-3节）**

> "以色列人住在什亭，百姓与摩押女子行起淫乱。因为这女子叫百姓来，一同给她们的神献祭，百姓就吃她们的祭物，跪拜她们的神。以色列人与巴力毘珥连合。"

弟兄姐妹，什亭——这是以色列渡约旦河前最后的营地。他们已经站在迦南的门口——胜利似乎近在咫尺。但就在这里——在终点线前——他们跌倒了。

注意这个过程：摩押女子→邀请以色列男人→一同吃偶像的祭物→跪拜她们的神→"与巴力毘珥连合"。这是一个渐进的堕落：从社交接触开始，到饮食交流，到宗教参与，最终到完全的偶像崇拜和淫乱。每一步看起来都不那么严重——但连锁起来就是灾难。

弟兄姐妹，民数记31:16后来揭示了这件事背后的策划者："这些妇女因巴兰的计谋，叫以色列人在毘珥的事上得罪耶和华。"巴兰的嘴被神控制了——但他的心仍然贪恋巴勒的金银。他不能直接咒诅以色列——但他可以教导巴勒用另一种方式伤害他们：用女人和偶像引诱他们离弃神。当他们离弃了神——他们就自动失去了神的保护——不需要咒诅了。

"耶和华的怒气就向以色列人发作"——二万四千人死于瘟疫。

**非尼哈的忌邪（6-13节）**

在摩西和全会众正在会幕门前哭泣、悔改的时候——一个以色列人公然将一个米甸女人带到营中、带进帐棚。弟兄姐妹，这不是私下的罪——这是公开的、挑衅性的蔑视。全会众在哭泣——他在众人面前行淫。

> "祭司亚伦的孙子，以利亚撒的儿子非尼哈看见了，就从会中起来，手里拿着枪，跟随那以色列人进亭子里去，便将以色列人和那女人由腹中刺透。这样，在以色列人中瘟疫就止住了。"

弟兄姐妹，非尼哈的行动——以今天的标准看是暴力的、极端的。但在当时的处境下——瘟疫正在蔓延、神的怒气正在燃烧、每一刻都有人在死去——非尼哈的行动是紧急的审判行为，是为了止住更大规模的死亡。

神对非尼哈的评价是什么？"祭司亚伦的孙子，以利亚撒的儿子非尼哈，使我向以色列人所发的怒消了；因他在他们中间，以我的忌邪为心。"弟兄姐妹——"以我的忌邪为心"——非尼哈不是为了自己的权力或荣誉——他是为了神的荣耀而忌邪。

神给了非尼哈一个极大的赏赐："我将我平安的约赐给他。这约要给他和他的后裔，作为永远当祭司职任的约。"弟兄姐妹，"平安的约"（brit shalom）——永远的祭司职分归给非尼哈的后裔。

**心利和哥斯比的身份（14-18节）**

圣经特别记录了这两个被杀之人的身份——以色列人心利，是"西缅一个宗族的首领"；米甸女人哥斯比，是"米甸一个宗族的首领的女儿"。弟兄姐妹，这不是小人物——是两个宗族的领袖级人物。这不是底层的混乱——是上层的蓄意安排。

然后神命令以色列人"扰害米甸人，击杀他们；因为他们用诡计扰害你们"。弟兄姐妹——这是为什么第三十一章以色列人要攻打米甸的原因。

弟兄姐妹，第二十五章给我们的警告是严肃的：以色列人可以在战场上胜过西宏和噩——但他们在试探面前跌倒了。外在的敌人可以用刀剑对付——内在的试探用什么对付？第二十一章的铜蛇告诉我们：一望基督就得救。但如果你选择不望呢？如果你转开眼睛去看摩押女子、去吃偶像的祭物呢？

弟兄姐妹，以色列最大的敌人不是摩押的军队——而是摩押的女子。不是巴兰的咒诅——而是巴兰的计谋。撒但最有效的武器从来不是正面攻击——而是侧面引诱。"因为我们并不是与属血气的争战，乃是与那些执政的、掌权的、管辖这幽暗世界的，以及天空属灵气的恶魔争战"（弗6:12）。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Chapter 25 was the Balaam story's darkest epilogue—and one of Numbers' most heartbreaking events. Balaam could not curse Israel with his mouth—so he switched weapons: Moabite women and pagan idols.

**The Immorality at Shittim (vv. 1-3)**

> "While Israel lived in Shittim, the people began to whore with the daughters of Moab. These invited the people to the sacrifices of their gods, and the people ate and bowed down to their gods. So Israel yoked himself to Baal of Peor."

Brothers and sisters, Shittim—Israel's final camp before crossing the Jordan. They stood at Canaan's doorstep—victory seemed imminent. But right here—before the finish line—they fell.

Note the progression: Moabite women → invited Israelite men → shared idol-feast food → bowed to their gods → "yoked to Baal of Peor." A gradual descent: from social contact, to dining fellowship, to religious participation, to complete idolatry and sexual immorality. Each step seemed not so serious—but chained together, disaster.

Brothers and sisters, Numbers 31:16 later revealed the mastermind: "These women...on Balaam's advice, caused the people of Israel to act treacherously against the LORD in the incident of Peor." Balaam's mouth was God-controlled—but his heart still coveted Balak's gold. He could not directly curse Israel—but he could teach Balak another way to harm them: use women and idols to entice them from God. Once they abandoned God—they automatically lost God's protection—no curse needed.

"The anger of the LORD was kindled against Israel"—twenty-four thousand died in the plague.

**Phinehas' Zeal (vv. 6-13)**

While Moses and the entire congregation wept and repented at the tabernacle entrance—one Israelite man openly brought a Midianite woman into camp, into his tent. Brothers and sisters, this was not private sin—it was public, provocative contempt. The congregation was weeping—he was fornicating before everyone.

"When Phinehas the son of Eleazar, son of Aaron the priest, saw it, he rose and left the congregation and took a spear in his hand and went after the man of Israel into the chamber and pierced both of them through." And the plague was stopped.

Brothers and sisters, Phinehas' action—by today's standards violent, extreme. But in that context—plague spreading, God's wrath burning, people dying every moment—Phinehas' act was emergency judicial action to stop greater mass death.

God's evaluation of Phinehas? "Phinehas the son of Eleazar...has turned back my wrath from the people of Israel, in that he was jealous with my jealousy among them." Brothers and sisters—"jealous with my jealousy"—Phinehas acted not for his own power or honor—but for God's glory.

God gave Phinehas an extraordinary reward: "I give to him my covenant of peace. And it shall be to him and to his descendants after him the covenant of a perpetual priesthood." Brothers and sisters, the "covenant of peace" (brit shalom)—the eternal priestly office given to Phinehas' descendants.

**Zimri and Cozbi's Identity (vv. 14-18)**

Scripture specifically recorded these two killed persons' identities—Zimri the Israelite was "a chief of a father's house in Simeon"; Cozbi the Midianite woman was "the daughter of a chief of Midian." Brothers and sisters, these were not minor figures—both were tribal-leadership level. This was not bottom-level chaos—but top-level deliberate arrangement.

Then God commanded Israel to "harass the Midianites and strike them down, for they have harassed you with their wiles." Brothers and sisters—this is why chapter 31 records Israel attacking Midian.

Brothers and sisters, chapter 25's warning is solemn: Israel could defeat Sihon and Og on battlefields—but fell before temptation. External enemies could be met with swords—what meets internal temptation? Chapter 21's bronze serpent tells us: one look at Christ saves. But what if you choose not to look? If you turn your eyes to Moabite women, to eat idol feasts?

Brothers and sisters, Israel's greatest enemy was not Moab's army—but Moab's women. Not Balaam's curse—but Balaam's counsel. Satan's most effective weapon is never frontal assault—but lateral seduction. "For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places." (Eph 6:12).

## 历史背景（中文）

巴力毘珥（Baal Peor）是毘珥山地区崇拜的迦南/摩押神祗——"巴力"意为"主人/领主"，"毘珥"是地名。古代迦南宗教中，巴力崇拜与农业丰产密切相关——包含仪式性的性行为，被认为能确保土地和人的繁衍。因此以色列人"与摩押女子行淫乱"和"跪拜她们的神"在古代异教中是一个统一的仪式行为。非尼哈的行为在犹太传统中被视为"为神而忌邪"（qana'）的最高典范——以利亚在迦密山上杀巴力先知也被归为同一传统（王上18:40）。非尼哈的平安之约——他的后裔一直担任祭司直到第二圣殿时期。列代志上6:50记载以利亚撒→非尼哈→亚比书→布基→乌西的祭司世系。"二万四千人"——保罗在哥林多前书10:8说"二万三千人"，差异可能是保罗只计算"一天之内"死的，或传统版本差异。

## Historical Context (English)

Baal of Peor was a Canaanite/Moabite deity worshipped in the Peor mountain region—"Baal" meaning "lord/master," "Peor" being the place name. In ancient Canaanite religion, Baal worship was closely linked to agricultural fertility—including ritual sexual acts believed to ensure land and human reproduction. Thus Israel "whoring with Moabite daughters" and "bowing to their gods" was in ancient paganism a unified ritual act. Phinehas' action was viewed in Jewish tradition as the supreme example of "zeal for God" (qana')—Elijah killing Baal's prophets on Carmel belonged to the same tradition (1 Kgs 18:40). Phinehas' covenant of peace—his descendants served as priests through the Second Temple period. 1 Chronicles 6:50 records the priestly lineage: Eleazar → Phinehas → Abishua → Bukki → Uzzi. "Twenty-four thousand"—Paul in 1 Corinthians 10:8 says "twenty-three thousand"; the discrepancy may reflect Paul counting only those who died "in a single day," or traditional version differences.

## Structure (English)

**Immorality and Idolatry at Shittim (vv. 1-3)**—Israel whores with Moabite women → eats idol sacrifices → bows down → yoked to Baal of Peor → LORD's anger kindled.

**God's Command and Judgment (vv. 4-5)**—Hang chiefs → judges ordered to kill those yoked to Baal of Peor.

**Phinehas' Action (vv. 6-9)**—Israelite man openly brings Midianite woman → Phinehas rises with spear → pierces both → plague stops → 24,000 dead.

**Covenant of Peace (vv. 10-13)**—God commends Phinehas "jealous with my jealousy" → grants covenant of peace → perpetual priesthood.

**Zimri/Cozbi's Identity + Command Against Midian (vv. 14-18)**—Zimri = Simeonite chief → Cozbi = Midianite chief's daughter → God commands attacking Midian for their wiles.

## 神学要点（中文）

**巴兰的计谋——诱惑比咒诅更致命**
巴兰无法咒诅就改用引诱——改革宗传统教导撒但的策略是多方面的。韦敏斯德大要理问答第105问论不可拜偶像。"人被试探，乃是被自己的私欲牵引诱惑的"（雅1:14）。以色列的防御无法被外在的咒诅攻破——但他们自己的私欲从内部瓦解了防线。

**非尼哈的忌邪——为神的荣耀而忌邪**
非尼哈"以我的忌邪为心"——改革宗传统教导"圣洁的忌邪"（holy jealousy）。韦敏斯德信条第21章论宗教崇拜。出埃及记20:5"我耶和华你的神是忌邪的神。"忌邪不是坏品质——神的忌邪是祂对盟约忠诚的热情。非尼哈的忌邪是对神忌邪的效仿。

**渐进的堕落——罪的连锁反应**
从交往到饮食到敬拜到淫乱——改革宗传统教导罪有渐进性。韦敏斯德信条第6章论罪和堕落。每一步似乎无害——连起来就是毁灭。"一点面酵能使全团都发起来"（加5:9）。

## Theology (English)

**Balaam's Counsel—Seduction More Lethal Than Cursing**
When Balaam could not curse he switched to seduction—the Reformed tradition teaches Satan's strategies are multifaceted. Westminster Larger Catechism Q105 on not worshipping idols. "But every man is tempted, when he is drawn away of his own lust, and enticed." (Jas 1:14). Israel's defense could not be breached by external curses—but their own desires dismantled the defense from within.

**Phinehas' Zeal—Jealous for God's Glory**
Phinehas was "jealous with my jealousy"—the Reformed tradition teaches "holy jealousy." Westminster Confession chapter 21 on religious worship. Exodus 20:5: "I the LORD your God am a jealous God." Jealousy is not a bad quality—God's jealousy is His passionate faithfulness to the covenant. Phinehas' jealousy imitated God's jealousy.

**Gradual Descent—Sin's Chain Reaction**
From socializing to dining to worship to immorality—the Reformed tradition teaches sin's progressive nature. Westminster Confession chapter 6 on sin and the fall. Each step seems harmless—chained together, destruction. "A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump." (Gal 5:9).

## 基督的影子（中文）

民数记第二十五章有两个指向基督的画面。

**非尼哈站在瘟疫中——基督站在审判中**
非尼哈跑进瘟疫中制止审判——如同第十六章亚伦站在活人死人中间。但非尼哈不是用香——是用枪。他直接处理了罪的源头（心利和哥斯比），瘟疫就止住了。基督也是如此——祂不只是"挡住"审判（如亚伦的香），祂直接"消灭"了罪的权势。歌罗西书2:14"又涂抹了在律例上所写攻击我们、有碍于我们的字据，把它撤去，钉在十字架上。"基督在十字架上不是暂时止住瘟疫——祂永远消灭了罪的定罪权。

**平安之约——基督是永远的大祭司**
非尼哈得到了"平安之约"——永远的祭司职分。但非尼哈是人——他的后裔最终也不能永远担任。唯独基督是永远的大祭司——希伯来书7:24"这位既是永远常存的，他祭司的职任就长久不更换。"非尼哈的"平安之约"是暂时的预表——基督的祭司职分才是真正永远的。

**巴兰的计谋——撒但的策略**
启示录2:14"然而，有几件事我要责备你：因为在你那里有人服从了巴兰的教训；这巴兰曾教导巴勒将绊脚石放在以色列人面前，叫他们吃祭偶像之物，行奸淫的事。"弟兄姐妹，在启示录给别迦摩教会的信中——基督直接引用了巴兰的教训作为教会内部腐败的警告。巴兰的策略——从外部攻击不破就从内部腐蚀——是撒但永恒的战术。教会最大的危险不是逼迫——而是妥协。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

Numbers 25 contains two images pointing to Christ.

**Phinehas Standing in the Plague—Christ Standing in Judgment**
Phinehas ran into the plague to stop judgment—like Aaron in chapter 16 standing between dead and living. But Phinehas used not incense—but a spear. He directly dealt with sin's source (Zimri and Cozbi), and the plague stopped. So with Christ—He didn't merely "block" judgment (like Aaron's incense); He directly "destroyed" sin's power. Colossians 2:14: "By canceling the record of debt that stood against us with its legal demands. This he set aside, nailing it to the cross." Christ on the cross didn't temporarily halt the plague—He permanently destroyed sin's condemning power.

**Covenant of Peace—Christ the Eternal High Priest**
Phinehas received the "covenant of peace"—a perpetual priesthood. But Phinehas was human—his descendants could not ultimately serve forever. Only Christ is the eternal High Priest—Hebrews 7:24: "He holds his priesthood permanently, because he continues forever." Phinehas' "covenant of peace" was a temporary type—Christ's priesthood is the truly eternal one.

**Balaam's Counsel—Satan's Strategy**
Revelation 2:14: "But I have a few things against you: you have some there who hold the teaching of Balaam, who taught Balak to put a stumbling block before the sons of Israel, so that they might eat food sacrificed to idols and practice sexual immorality." Brothers and sisters, in Revelation's letter to Pergamum—Christ directly cited Balaam's teaching as a warning against internal church corruption. Balaam's strategy—when external attack fails, corrode from within—is Satan's eternal tactic. The church's greatest danger is not persecution—but compromise.
