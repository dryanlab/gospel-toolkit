## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。第三十一章是民数记中最让现代读者困惑的一章——对米甸的圣战。这也是我在地上的最后一场战争——神说"你要在米甸人身上报以色列人的仇，后来要归到你列祖那里"。打完这一仗，我就要死了。

**战争的起因和使命（1-6节）**

为什么打米甸？回到第二十五章——米甸人和摩押人合谋（按巴兰的计策）用女子和偶像引诱以色列人在什亭犯罪，导致24,000人死于瘟疫。第二十五章18节明确说"因为他们用诡计扰害你们"。这不是领土战争——这是对属灵诡计的审判。

每支派出一千人，共一万二千人。弟兄姐妹——一万二千人对付米甸！这不是压倒性的军力——但这不是人的战争，是"为耶和华报仇"。

注意谁带队——不是将军，而是"祭司以利亚撒的儿子非尼哈"，手里拿着"圣所的器皿和吹大声的号筒"。弟兄姐妹，这是属灵的军事行动——祭司在前、号角领路。第二十五章非尼哈因忌邪之举止住了瘟疫——现在他领军完成审判的另一半。

**战争的结果（7-12节）**

以色列人杀了所有米甸男丁，杀了米甸五王，还杀了巴兰。弟兄姐妹——"又用刀杀了比珥的儿子巴兰"。巴兰没有回到幼发拉底河边的老家——他留在了米甸地区。为什么？因为他还在那里等着收他的酬劳。他教唆巴勒用女人引诱以色列——他在等结果、等报酬。结果等来的是以色列的刀。

弟兄姐妹，巴兰的结局——说了最伟大的祝福（"有星出于雅各"），却死在以色列的刀下。他"愿如义人之死而死"——最终死得像一个出卖灵魂的占卜师。彼得后书2:15"他们离弃正路……随从比珥之子巴兰的路，巴兰就是那贪爱不义之工价的。"

**摩西的愤怒和巴兰的诡计被揭露（13-18节）**

军队带着掳来的妇女和孩子回到营地——摩西大怒："你们要存留这一切妇女的活命吗？这些妇女因巴兰的计谋，叫以色列人在毘珥的事上得罪耶和华！"

弟兄姐妹，31:16是全民数记巴兰故事的最终注脚——"这些妇女因巴兰的计谋"——直到这里，巴兰幕后策划什亭事件的全貌才完全揭露。第二十二到二十四章巴兰无法咒诅——第二十五章以色列在什亭跌倒——第三十一章16节才把两者连起来：巴兰是什亭事件的幕后黑手。

**战利品的分配（25-47节）**

战后的分配极其有序：掳获物分成两半——一半归出战的战士，一半归留守的会众。战士的一半中，每五百取一作为贡物给祭司；会众的一半中，每五十取一给利未人。

弟兄姐妹，这个分配原则体现了几个重要的价值：

出战者和留守者共享战利品——战争的胜利不只属于前线的战士，也属于后方的支持者。神分配收获给所有参与的人，不只是那些持刀的人。

神拿走的那一份是"举祭"——从战利品中首先给祭司和利未人。即使在战争中获得的财富，也要先归给神。

**零伤亡和感恩奉献（48-54节）**

弟兄姐妹，最令人惊叹的是这个细节——"仆人权下的兵已经计算总数，并不短少一人。"一万二千人出去打仗——一万二千人回来。零伤亡。

将领们因为感恩，把从战场上获得的金饰品全部奉献给耶和华——"好在耶和华面前为我们的生命赎罪。"弟兄姐妹，他们不觉得零伤亡是他们的军事才能——他们知道这完全是神的保守。每一个活着回来的人都是恩典——所以他们用金子作感恩祭。

金子的总量是16,750舍客勒——约200公斤的黄金。弟兄姐妹，这是一笔巨大的感恩奉献。

弟兄姐妹，第三十一章的战争叙事确实让现代读者感到不安——杀戮的规模和范围令人震惊。但我们需要理解这一章在整个民数记中的位置：以色列不是为了扩张领土而打仗——他们是在执行第二十五章的神圣审判。米甸的诡计不是普通的外交冲突——它是一场蓄意设计的、以宗教引诱为手段的属灵战争。24,000以色列人因此死于瘟疫。第三十一章的战争是这场属灵战争的审判阶段。

弟兄姐妹，这是旧约以色列独特的历史处境——作为神的盟约民族，在一个具体的历史时期，执行具体的神圣命令。新约的教会不被命令进行这样的军事行动——"我们并不是与属血气的争战"（弗6:12）。但旧约的圣战叙事提醒我们：神对罪的审判是真实的、严肃的、不可逃避的。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Chapter 31 is Numbers' most troubling chapter for modern readers—the holy war against Midian. This was also my last battle on earth—God said "Avenge the people of Israel on the Midianites. Afterward you shall be gathered to your people." After this battle, I would die.

**The War's Cause and Mission (vv. 1-6)**

Why attack Midian? Return to chapter 25—Midianites and Moabites conspired (following Balaam's counsel) to use women and idols to entice Israel into sin at Shittim, causing 24,000 plague deaths. Chapter 25:18 explicitly stated "they have harassed you with their wiles." This was not territorial war—it was judgment on spiritual deception.

One thousand from each tribe, 12,000 total. Brothers and sisters—12,000 against Midian! Not overwhelming force—but this was not man's war, it was "vengeance for the LORD."

Note who led—not a general, but "Phinehas the son of Eleazar the priest" carrying "the vessels of the sanctuary and the trumpets for the alarm." Brothers and sisters, this was a spiritual military operation—priest leading, trumpets guiding. In chapter 25 Phinehas' zealous act stopped the plague—now he led the army to complete judgment's other half.

**Battle Results (vv. 7-12)**

Israel killed all Midianite males, killed five Midianite kings, and killed Balaam. Brothers and sisters—"They also killed Balaam the son of Beor with the sword." Balaam had not returned to his homeland by the Euphrates—he remained in the Midian region. Why? Because he was still there waiting for payment. He counseled Balak to use women to entice Israel—he was waiting for results and reward. What arrived instead was Israel's sword.

Brothers and sisters, Balaam's end—he spoke the greatest blessings ("a star shall come out of Jacob"), yet died by Israel's sword. He "desired to die the death of the upright"—yet ultimately died like a soul-selling diviner. 2 Peter 2:15: "Forsaking the right way, they have gone astray, following the way of Balaam, the son of Beor, who loved gain from wrongdoing."

**Moses' Anger and Balaam's Plot Revealed (vv. 13-18)**

The army returned with captured women and children—Moses was furious: "Have you let all the women live? These women, on Balaam's advice, caused the people of Israel to act treacherously against the LORD in the incident of Peor!"

Brothers and sisters, 31:16 was the entire Balaam narrative's final footnote—"on Balaam's advice"—only here was Balaam's behind-the-scenes orchestration of the Shittim incident fully revealed. Chapters 22-24: Balaam could not curse—chapter 25: Israel fell at Shittim—chapter 31:16 finally connected both: Balaam was Shittim's mastermind.

**Spoils Distribution (vv. 25-47)**

Post-war distribution was extremely orderly: spoils split in half—half to fighting warriors, half to the remaining congregation. From the warriors' half, one in 500 as tribute to priests; from the congregation's half, one in 50 to Levites.

Brothers and sisters, this distribution principle embodied several important values:

Combatants and non-combatants shared spoils equally—victory belonged not only to frontline fighters but to rear supporters. God distributed harvest to all participants, not just sword-bearers.

God's portion came first as "wave offering"—from spoils, priests and Levites received first. Even wealth gained in war must first go to God.

**Zero Casualties and Grateful Offering (vv. 48-54)**

Brothers and sisters, the most astonishing detail—"your servants have counted the men of war who are under our command, and there is not a man missing from us." Twelve thousand went to war—twelve thousand returned. Zero casualties.

The officers, in gratitude, offered all gold ornaments from the battlefield to the LORD—"to make atonement for ourselves before the LORD." Brothers and sisters, they did not attribute zero casualties to military skill—they knew it was entirely God's preservation. Every returning soldier was grace—so they offered gold as a thanksgiving sacrifice.

The gold totaled 16,750 shekels—approximately 200 kilograms. Brothers and sisters, an enormous grateful offering.

Brothers and sisters, chapter 31's war narrative indeed troubles modern readers—the killing's scale and scope are shocking. But we must understand this chapter's position within all of Numbers: Israel was not fighting for territorial expansion—they were executing chapter 25's divine judgment. Midian's deception was not ordinary diplomatic conflict—it was deliberately designed spiritual warfare using religious seduction. 24,000 Israelites died from the resulting plague. Chapter 31's war was this spiritual war's judgment phase.

Brothers and sisters, this was Old Testament Israel's unique historical situation—as God's covenant nation, in a specific historical period, executing specific divine commands. The New Testament church is not commanded to conduct such military operations—"For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places." (Eph 6:12). But the Old Testament's holy war narratives remind us: God's judgment against sin is real, serious, and inescapable.

## 历史背景（中文）

米甸人在旧约中有复杂的角色——摩西的岳父叶忒罗是米甸祭司（出18:1），摩西在米甸避难了四十年。但这里的米甸与摩西的岳父的米甸可能不完全相同——米甸是一个分布广泛的部落联盟，不同的米甸族群可能在不同地区。第三十一章的五个米甸王（以未、利金、苏珥、户珥、利巴）在约书亚记13:21被称为"西宏的族长"，暗示他们与被以色列击败的亚摩利王西宏有政治联盟。巴兰被杀——这说明巴兰虽然在第二十四章末说"回他本地去"，但实际上他仍在米甸/摩押地区活动。犹太传统认为巴兰留下来是为了从巴勒那里收取他设计什亭诡计的报酬。零伤亡（12,000人全部生还）在古代战争记录中极为罕见——这被视为神超自然保守的明证。

## Historical Context (English)

Midianites had a complex role in the Old Testament—Moses' father-in-law Jethro was a Midianite priest (Exod 18:1); Moses took refuge in Midian for forty years. But the Midian here may not be identical to Moses' father-in-law's Midian—Midian was a widely dispersed tribal confederation, with different Midianite groups in different regions. Chapter 31's five Midianite kings (Evi, Rekem, Zur, Hur, Reba) are called "chiefs of Sihon" in Joshua 13:21, suggesting political alliance with the Amorite king Sihon whom Israel had defeated. Balaam was killed—indicating that though chapter 24's end said he "went back to his place," he actually remained active in the Midian/Moab region. Jewish tradition believed Balaam stayed to collect payment from Balak for designing the Shittim scheme. Zero casualties (all 12,000 returning alive) was extremely rare in ancient war records—viewed as clear evidence of God's supernatural preservation.

## 神学要点（中文）

**圣战的神学——神的审判通过以色列执行**
米甸之战不是人的战争——改革宗传统教导旧约圣战是神审判的具体历史执行。韦敏斯德信条第5章论护理。神使用以色列作为审判的器具——但这不是以色列可以随意发动的。每一次圣战都有具体的神的命令。新约的教会不被授权进行这样的物质战争——"因为我们的争战不是肉体的"（林后10:4）。

**零伤亡——恩典的明证**
12,000人全部生还——改革宗传统教导神超自然的护理。韦敏斯德信条第5章。将领们的反应是感恩奉献，不是自我庆祝——这是正确的属灵态度：一切好结果都是恩典。

**战利品的公平分配——社区的共享**
出战者和留守者共享战果——改革宗传统教导教会是一个肢体。哥林多前书12:26"若一个肢体得荣耀，所有的肢体就一同快乐。"战争的胜利属于全以色列——不只属于前线的战士。教会的成就不属于少数领袖——而属于整个身体。

## Theology (English)

**Holy War Theology—God's Judgment Executed Through Israel**
The Midian war was not man's war—the Reformed tradition teaches Old Testament holy war was God's judgment in specific historical execution. Westminster Confession chapter 5 on providence. God used Israel as a judgment instrument—but this was not something Israel could launch at will. Every holy war had specific divine command. The New Testament church is not authorized to conduct such physical warfare—"(For the weapons of our warfare are not carnal, but mighty through God to the pulling down of strong holds;)" (2 Cor 10:4).

**Zero Casualties—Clear Evidence of Grace**
All 12,000 returned alive—the Reformed tradition teaches God's supernatural providence. Westminster Confession chapter 5. The officers' response was grateful offering, not self-celebration—the correct spiritual attitude: all good outcomes are grace.

**Fair Spoils Distribution—Community Sharing**
Combatants and non-combatants shared equally—the Reformed tradition teaches the church is one body. 1 Corinthians 12:26: "If one member is honored, all rejoice together." War's victory belonged to all Israel—not just frontline warriors. Church achievements belong not to a few leaders—but to the entire body.

## 基督的影子（中文）

民数记第三十一章有三个指向基督的画面。

**巴兰的死——假先知的结局**
巴兰说了正确的预言却死在刀下——启示录19:20"那兽被擒拿；那在兽面前曾行奇事、迷惑受兽印记和拜兽像之人的假先知，也与兽同被擒拿。他们两个就活活的被扔在烧着硫磺的火湖里。"弟兄姐妹，巴兰是假先知的原型——有正确的话语却没有正确的心。基督的审判不看你说了什么——看你的心是什么。

**非尼哈带领——祭司在属灵战争中的领导**
非尼哈——祭司——在战争中领军——希伯来书2:10"原来那为万物所属为万物所本的，要领许多的儿子进荣耀里去，使救他们的元帅，因受苦难得以完全，本是合宜的。"基督是属灵战争的大元帅——祂不是在后方指挥，祂在前线带领。非尼哈拿着圣所的器皿和号筒——基督带着十字架和复活的能力。

**零伤亡——基督保守祂的百姓**
12,000人一个不缺——约翰福音17:12"你所赐给我的人，我已护卫他们……其中除了那灭亡之子，没有一个灭亡的。"基督保守属祂的人——一个也不失落。将领们用金子感恩——信徒用生命感恩，因为我们的每一天都是基督保守的恩典。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

Numbers 31 contains three images pointing to Christ.

**Balaam's Death—False Prophets' End**
Balaam spoke correct prophecies yet died by the sword—Revelation 19:20: "The beast was captured, and with it the false prophet who in its presence had done the signs by which he deceived those who had received the mark of the beast. These two were thrown alive into the lake of fire." Brothers and sisters, Balaam was the false prophet archetype—correct words without a correct heart. Christ's judgment does not consider what you said—but what your heart was.

**Phinehas Leading—Priestly Leadership in Spiritual Warfare**
Phinehas—priest—led the army—Hebrews 2:10: "It was fitting that he, for whom and by whom all things exist, in bringing many sons to glory, should make the founder of their salvation perfect through suffering." Christ is the supreme commander of spiritual warfare—He does not command from the rear; He leads from the front. Phinehas carried sanctuary vessels and trumpets—Christ carries the cross and resurrection's power.

**Zero Casualties—Christ Preserving His People**
12,000 with none missing—John 17:12: "While I was with them, I kept them in your name, which you have given me. I have guarded them, and not one of them has been lost except the son of destruction." Christ preserves those who are His—not one lost. Officers gave gold in gratitude—believers give their lives in gratitude, because our every day is Christ's preserving grace.
