## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是所罗门。箴言第六章包含了几个独立的主题——从经济担保到懒惰、从奸诈到淫乱——但最重要的是那七样"耶和华所恨恶的"事。神的恨恶是我们最好的道德罗盘。

**经济担保的警告（第1-5节）**

> "我儿，你若为邻舍作了保，为外人击掌，你就被口中的话语缠住，被嘴里的言语捉住。我儿，你既落在邻舍手中，就当这样行：你去，要自卑，力劝你的邻舍。不要容眼睛睡觉，不要容眼皮打盹。要救自己，如鹿脱离猎人的手，如鸟脱离捉鸟人的手。"

弟兄姐妹，这个警告在今天仍然实际——不要轻易为别人的债务担保。不是不能帮助人——而是在承担财务责任之前要谨慎。弟兄姐妹，很多人出于好心为朋友担保、最后却弄得倾家荡产、朋友也散了。智慧教导我们：好心也需要智慧。

**懒惰人的教训（第6-11节）**

> "懒惰人哪，你去察看蚂蚁的动作就可得智慧！蚂蚁没有元帅，没有官长，没有君王，尚且在夏天预备食物，在收割时聚敛粮食。懒惰人哪，你要睡到几时呢？你何时睡醒呢？再睡片时，打盹片时，抱着手躺卧片时，你的贫穷就必如强盗速来，你的缺乏仿佛拿兵器的人来到。"

弟兄姐妹，去看蚂蚁——小小的昆虫——它教给你一个伟大的功课：预备。蚂蚁没有人监督它，没有人强迫它工作——但它在夏天预备冬天的食物。弟兄姐妹，你的勤劳需要内驱力——不是因为老板在看——而是因为你明白预备的重要性。

"再睡片时打盹片时"——弟兄姐妹，懒惰不是一次性的大决定——而是一连串的小"片时"。每一次的"再睡片时"都在建造懒惰的习惯。"贫穷如强盗速来"——不是慢慢来——而是"速来"。懒惰的后果往往是突然临头的。

**七样耶和华所恨恶的（第16-19节）**

> "耶和华所恨恶的有六样，连他心所憎恶的共有七样：眼目高傲，口出谎言，手流无辜人的血，心怀恶谋，脚奔跑行恶，口吐谎言的假见证，并弟兄中布散纷争的人。"

弟兄姐妹，七样——让我们逐一来看：

**眼目高傲**——骄傲是第一样。弟兄姐妹，不是"行为高傲"——而是"眼目高傲"。眼目是心里骄傲的外在表现。箴言16:18"骄傲在败坏以先。"

**口出谎言**——诚实是神的属性——谎言是撒但的属性（约8:44"撒但是说谎之人的父"）。

**手流无辜人的血**——故意伤害无辜——是对神形像的攻击。

**心怀恶谋**——不只是行恶——而是策划恶事。内心的计划。弟兄姐妹，耶稣说愤怒就是杀人（太5:21-22）——谋划恶事在心里就已经是罪了。

**脚奔跑行恶**——不是被迫做恶——而是"奔跑"。积极地、急切地去做坏事。

**口吐谎言的假见证**——法庭中的假见证。不只是私下说谎——而是在公开场合用谎言伤害人。

**弟兄中布散纷争**——最后一样——"布散纷争"。弟兄姐妹，这七样中最后一样不是杀人、不是淫乱——而是"布散纷争"。在弟兄中挑拨是非、制造分裂——这是神最深恶痛绝的事之一。弟兄姐妹，你有没有在朋友圈里成为"纷争的布散者"？

**关于淫乱的警告（第27-29节）**

> "人若怀火在怀里，衣服岂能不烧呢？人若在火炭上走，脚岂能不烫呢？亲近邻舍之妻的也是如此；凡挨近她的，不免受罚。"

弟兄姐妹，"怀火在怀里衣服岂能不烧"——这是最直白的比喻。弟兄姐妹，你以为你能"玩玩"然后停下来？你以为你能靠近火然后不被烧？智慧说：不可能。罪的吸引力是真实的——就像火的热是真实的。但烧伤的后果也是真实的。弟兄姐妹，最好的防御是远离——不是靠近了以后再抵抗。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Solomon. Proverbs 6 contains several independent themes—from financial guarantees to laziness, from deception to adultery—but most importantly, the seven things "the LORD hates."

**Warning About Financial Guarantees (vv. 1-5)**

> "My son, if you have put up security for your neighbor...you are snared in the words of your mouth...Go, hasten, and plead urgently with your neighbor. Give your eyes no sleep and your eyelids no slumber; save yourself like a gazelle from the hand of the hunter."

Brothers and sisters, this warning remains practical today—don't lightly guarantee another's debts. Not that you can't help people—but be wise before taking on financial responsibility. Many people cosigned out of goodwill, ending in ruin with friendships destroyed. Wisdom teaches: good intentions also need wisdom.

**The Ant's Lesson (vv. 6-11)**

> "Go to the ant, O sluggard; consider her ways, and be wise. Without having any chief, officer, or ruler, she prepares her bread in summer and gathers her food in harvest."

Brothers and sisters, look at the ant—a tiny insect—teaching a great lesson: preparation. No supervisor, no one forcing it to work—yet it prepares winter food in summer. Your diligence needs internal drive—not because the boss is watching—but because you understand preparation's importance.

> "A little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to rest, and poverty will come upon you like a robber."—Laziness is not one big decision—but a series of small "a little whiles."

**Seven Things the LORD Hates (vv. 16-19)**

> "There are six things that the LORD hates, seven that are an abomination to him: haughty eyes, a lying tongue, and hands that shed innocent blood, a heart that devises wicked plans, feet that make haste to run to evil, a false witness who breathes out lies, and one who sows discord among brothers."

Brothers and sisters, seven—let me examine each:

**Haughty eyes**—pride is first. Not "proud behavior" but "haughty eyes." Proverbs 16:18: "Pride goes before destruction."

**Lying tongue**—honesty is God's attribute; lying is Satan's (John 8:44 "the father of lies").

**Hands that shed innocent blood**—attacking God's image bearers.

**Heart that devises wicked plans**—not just doing evil but planning it. Jesus said anger is already murder (Matt 5:21-22)—planning evil in the heart is already sin.

**Feet that make haste to run to evil**—not forced—but "making haste." Actively, eagerly doing evil.

**False witness**—not just private lying but using lies to publicly harm people.

**One who sows discord among brothers**—the last of the seven—not murder, not adultery—but "sowing discord." Stirring strife among brothers, creating division—one of the things God most deeply hates. Have you been a "discord-sower" in your community?

**Warning About Adultery (vv. 27-29)**

> "Can a man carry fire next to his chest and his clothes not be burned? Or can one walk on hot coals and his feet not be scorched? So is he who goes in to his neighbor's wife; none who touches her will go unpunished."

Brothers and sisters, "carry fire in your chest"—the most direct metaphor. You think you can "play around" and stop? Think you can approach fire and not be burned? Wisdom says: impossible. Sin's attraction is real—like fire's heat is real. But burning's consequences are real too. The best defense is distance—not resisting after drawing near.

## 历史背景（中文）

箴言第六章将几个不相关的主题并列——这在箴言书中是常见的"格言集"形式。"七样耶和华所恨恶的"（第16-19节）是箴言书中最著名的"数字格言"（numerical saying）之一——"六样……七样"的格式在古代近东文学中很常见，表示一个完整的列表。七在圣经中是完全数。"眼目高傲"排在第一位——这在圣经中一贯的顺序：骄傲是万罪之源（参以赛亚书14:12-15撒但的骄傲，创世记3:5人想"如神"的骄傲）。蚂蚁的比喻是圣经中罕见的昆虫智慧例证——直接观察自然来学习智慧的典范。

## Historical Context (English)

Proverbs 6 juxtaposes several unrelated topics—common in the "sayings collection" format of Proverbs. The "seven things the LORD hates" (vv. 16-19) is one of the most famous "numerical sayings" in Proverbs—the "six...seven" format is common in ancient Near Eastern literature, indicating a complete list. Seven is Scripture's number of completeness. "Haughty eyes" is listed first—consistent with Scripture's pattern: pride is the root of all sin (cf. Isa 14:12-15 Satan's pride, Gen 3:5 humanity's "be like God" pride). The ant metaphor is one of Scripture's rare examples of insect wisdom—exemplifying direct nature observation for learning wisdom.

## 经文结构（中文）

**担保的危险（1-5节）**——为邻舍作保→被话缠住→要自卑力劝→如鹿脱猎人。
**懒惰的愚蒙（6-11节）**——察看蚂蚁→夏天预备→懒惰人→再睡片时→贫穷如强盗速来。
**恶人和奸诈人（12-15节）**——口出乖谬→心中策谋→必遭报应。
**七样所恨恶的（16-19节）**——眼目高傲→口出谎言→手流血→心怀恶谋→脚奔行恶→假见证→布散纷争。
**淫乱的警告（20-35节）**——遵守父母命令→不为女色心迷→怀火在怀→嫉恨不息。

## Structure (English)

**Danger of Guarantees (vv. 1-5)**—Put up security → snared in words → be humble, plead → escape like a gazelle.
**Folly of Laziness (vv. 6-11)**—Observe the ant → prepares in summer → sluggard → a little sleep → poverty comes like a robber.
**The Wicked Man (vv. 12-15)**—Crooked mouth → scheming heart → disaster suddenly.
**Seven Things Hated (vv. 16-19)**—Haughty eyes → lying tongue → shedding blood → devising evil → running to evil → false witness → sowing discord.
**Warning About Adultery (vv. 20-35)**—Keep father's commands → don't be captured by beauty → fire in chest → jealousy unrelenting.

## 神学要点（中文）

**神的属性——神的恨恶**
改革宗传统教导神有道德性的恨恶——这是神的圣洁的必然结果。韦敏斯德信条第二章论神的属性教导：祂是纯全圣洁，对罪完全厌恶。神的恨恶不是情绪失控——而是圣洁对不圣洁的必然反应。了解神"恨恶什么"——让我们更明白神是谁。

**"弟兄中布散纷争"——合一的神学**
改革宗传统教导教会合一的重要。韦敏斯德信条第25章论教会。"布散纷争"在七样恶事中排最后——但并不代表最不重要，而是强调其破坏性。基督在约翰福音17:21为信徒的合一祷告——破坏合一是抵挡基督的祷告。

**懒惰——恩赐的不良管家**
改革宗传统教导信徒要做时间和恩赐的好管家。韦敏斯德信条第16章论善行。懒惰不只是个人的问题——而是对神所赐恩赐的不良管家。"你要睡到几时"是对不善用神所赐时间的质问。

## Theology (English)

**God's Character—God's Hatred**
The Reformed tradition teaches God has moral hatred—the necessary result of His holiness. Westminster Confession chapter 2 on God's attributes: he is...most holy, hating all sin. God's hatred is not uncontrolled emotion—but holiness's necessary response to unholiness.

**"Sowing Discord Among Brothers"—Theology of Unity**
The Reformed tradition teaches the importance of church unity. Westminster Confession chapter 25 on the church.: Sowing discord is last in the seven—not least important but emphasizing its destructiveness. Christ prayed for believers' unity in John 17:21—destroying unity opposes Christ's prayer.

**Laziness—Poor Stewardship of Gifts**
The Reformed tradition teaches believers must be good stewards of time and gifts. Westminster Confession chapter 16 on good works. Laziness is not merely personal—but poor stewardship of God-given gifts.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"七样"完全应验在基督的对立面**——在七样神恨恶的事中，基督是完全相反的：谦卑（非高傲）、真理（非谎言）、为人舍命（非杀人）、专心做父的旨意（非恶谋）、奔跑行义（非行恶）、真见证（非假见证）、使人和好（非布散纷争）。约翰福音14:6"我就是道路、真理、生命"——基督的整个生命是对七样恶事的完全反面。

**蚂蚁预备指向基督降临**——哥林多前书16:13"你们要警醒，要在真道上站稳，要作大丈夫，要刚强。"如蚂蚁在夏天预备——信徒在基督再来之前要预备。"儆醒，因为你们不知道那日子，不知道那时辰"（太25:13）。预备不是被动等待——而是主动投入神的国度工作。

**"怀火在怀"——基督的圣洁**——约翰福音8:46"你们中间谁能指证我有罪呢？"基督从不"怀火在怀"——祂是唯一没有被情欲的火烧伤的人。祂的无罪使祂能作我们的赎罪祭——"就是那无瑕疵、无玷污的羔羊之宝血"（彼前1:19）。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**The "Seven" Are the Complete Opposite of Christ**—Christ is the complete opposite of each of the seven: humble (not proud), truth (not lying), giving life (not shedding blood), focused on Father's will (not devising evil), running to do good (not evil), true witness (not false), making peace (not sowing discord). John 14:6: "I am the way, and the truth, and the life"—Christ's entire life is the complete antithesis of the seven.

**The Ant's Preparation Points to Christ's Return**—1 Corinthians 16:13: "Be watchful, stand firm in the faith, act like men, be strong." Like the ant preparing in summer—believers prepare before Christ's return. "Watch therefore, for ye know neither the day nor the hour wherein the Son of man cometh." (Matt 25:13).

**"Fire in the Chest"—Christ's Holiness**—John 8:46: "Which one of you convicts me of sin?" Christ never "carried fire in his chest"—He is the only one never burned by lust. His sinlessness qualifies Him as our atoning sacrifice—"But with the precious blood of Christ, as of a lamb without blemish and without spot:" (1 Pet 1:19).
