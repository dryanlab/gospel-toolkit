## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是所罗门。箴言第十六章是整本箴言书神学密度最高的一章——它集中处理了人的筹划与神的主权之间的关系，并包含了那句令人惊叹的宣告："人心筹划自己的道路；惟耶和华指引他的脚步。"

**人心筹划，耶和华引导（第1、9节）**

> "心中的谋算在乎人；口中的答言由耶和华而来。""人心筹划自己的道路；惟耶和华指引他的脚步。"

弟兄姐妹，这是改革宗神学最核心的张力——人的责任与神的主权。"心中的谋算在乎人"——计划、策划、决策——这些是人真实的活动，神不是在控制木偶。"口中的答言由耶和华而来"——但最终什么话说出来、结果怎样——由神决定。

弟兄姐妹，这两句话不矛盾——它们描述了同一个现实的两个层面。你有真实的自由去筹划——但神有真实的主权来引导。弟兄姐妹，这意味着：你要认真筹划（不要以为"神会安排一切"就不用努力），同时你要放手结果（不要以为你的筹划就一定是神的计划）。

箴言16:3"你所做的事要交托耶和华，你所谋的就必成立。"注意"交托"在"成立"之前——不是"成立"以后才"交托"。你在谋划的时候就把它交给神——然后去做你能做的——结果交给神。

**骄傲与谦卑（第5、18节）**

> "心中骄傲的为耶和华所憎恶；他必不免受罚。""骄傲在败坏以先；狂心在跌倒之前。"

弟兄姐妹，"骄傲在败坏以先"——这是整本箴言中引用频率最高的经文之一。弟兄姐妹，历史证明了这个规律——拿破仑在莫斯科——希特勒在斯大林格勒——骄傲到了顶点，败坏就在门口了。个人的生命也是如此——当你觉得自己已经不可能跌倒的时候——跌倒往往就在那一刻。

弟兄姐妹，骄傲有一个可怕的特征——它很难被自己识别。骄傲的人通常不觉得自己骄傲——他觉得自己只是"有自信"、"知道自己的价值"。弟兄姐妹，最好的防骄傲的方式是每天早晨在神面前承认：我需要你，我离了你什么都不能做（约15:5）。

**舌头与心灵（第23-24节）**

> "智慧人的心教训他的口，又使他的嘴增长学问。良言如同蜂房，使心觉甘甜，使骨得医治。"

弟兄姐妹，"良言如同蜂房"——蜂蜜是甜的、是有营养的、是治愈的。你的"良言"对别人可以有这三种效果——甜（带来喜乐）、有营养（带来益处）、治愈（带来医治）。弟兄姐妹，想一想你最近说过的话——它们像蜂房一样吗？还是更像醋——酸的、刺人的？

"使骨得医治"——弟兄姐妹，这不是夸张。心理学研究证明，肯定和鼓励的话语对人的身心健康有真实的正面影响。圣经三千年前就告诉我们：良言可以"使骨得医治"——它是真实的医治力量。

**有一条路人以为正（第25节）**

> "有一条路，人以为正；至终成为死亡之路。"

弟兄姐妹，这句话在第14章已经出现过——现在再次出现。为什么？因为这是人类最需要被提醒的警告——我们的判断可以是错的，而且是致命的错误。弟兄姐妹，每一次你听到这句话——让它在你心里产生一点点谦卑："我有没有在走一条自以为正的死路？"

弟兄姐妹，箴言第十六章的核心是这个：神是主权者——祂引导你的脚步——不管你的筹划是什么。在祂面前谦卑，你的路会被引导。骄傲地走，败坏在等着你。把你的计划交给祂，你所谋的才能成立。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Solomon. Proverbs 16 is the most theologically dense chapter in all Proverbs—concentrating on the relationship between human planning and divine sovereignty, containing that astounding declaration: "The heart of man plans his way, but the LORD establishes his steps."

**Man Plans, the LORD Directs (vv. 1, 9)**

> "The plans of the heart belong to man, but the answer of the tongue is from the LORD." "The heart of man plans his way, but the LORD establishes his steps."

Brothers and sisters, Reformed theology's most central tension—human responsibility and divine sovereignty. "Plans of the heart belong to man"—planning, strategizing, deciding—genuine human activity; God is not controlling puppets. "The answer of the tongue is from the LORD"—but what ultimately comes out, how things turn out—God determines.

These two statements don't contradict—they describe two levels of the same reality. You have genuine freedom to plan—but God has genuine sovereignty to direct. This means: plan seriously (don't think "God will arrange everything" so no effort needed), while releasing the outcome (don't think your plan is necessarily God's plan).

Verse 3: "Commit your work to the LORD, and your plans will be established." Note "commit" before "established"—not "commit" after things work out. While strategizing, give it to God—then do what you can—give results to God.

**Pride and Humility (vv. 5, 18)**

> "Everyone who is arrogant in heart is an abomination to the LORD; be assured, he will not go unpunished." "Pride goes before destruction, and a haughty spirit before a fall."

Brothers and sisters, "pride goes before destruction"—one of Proverbs' most quoted verses. History proves this pattern—Napoleon in Moscow, Hitler at Stalingrad—pride at its peak, destruction at the door. Personal life too—when you feel you can't possibly fall—that's often the exact moment of falling.

**Good Words Like Honeycomb (vv. 23-24)**

> "The heart of the wise makes his speech judicious and adds persuasiveness to his lips. Gracious words are like a honeycomb, sweetness to the soul and health to the body."

Brothers and sisters, "like a honeycomb"—honey is sweet, nourishing, healing. Your "gracious words" can have these three effects—sweet (bringing joy), nourishing (bringing benefit), healing (bringing restoration). Think about what you've said recently—are they like honeycomb? Or more like vinegar?

Brothers and sisters, Proverbs 16's core: God is sovereign—He establishes your steps—regardless of your plans. Humble yourself before Him, your path will be directed. Walk in pride, destruction waits. Commit your plans to Him, and what you plan will be established.

## 历史背景（中文）

箴言第十六章在希伯来文中形成了一个对称的结构——前十一节几乎全部提到"耶和华"或"神"，是箴言书中"耶和华格言"最集中的段落。"骄傲在败坏以先狂心在跌倒之前"（第18节）是英文世界最广为人知的箴言格言之一（"Pride goes before a fall"谚语即源于此）。第9节"人心筹划自己的道路惟耶和华指引他的脚步"是改革宗传统中论"护理"（providence）和"人的责任"（human responsibility）关系的关键经文，被加尔文、清教徒神学家和韦敏斯德信条广泛引用。

## Historical Context (English)

Proverbs 16 forms a symmetric structure in Hebrew—the first eleven verses almost all mention "the LORD" or "God," the most concentrated "LORD-sayings" passage in Proverbs. "Pride goes before destruction, a haughty spirit before a fall" (v. 18) is one of the most widely known Proverbs sayings in the English-speaking world (the proverb "Pride goes before a fall" derives from this). Verse 9 "the heart of man plans his way, but the LORD establishes his steps" is a key text in the Reformed tradition for discussing the relationship between "providence" and "human responsibility," widely cited by Calvin, Puritan theologians, and the Westminster Confession.

## 经文结构（中文）

**神的主权（1-9节）**——谋算在乎人→口中答言由耶和华→交托耶和华→人心筹划→惟耶和华指引。
**骄傲的危险（10-20节）**——公平的天平→骄傲为耶和华憎恶→骄傲在败坏以先。
**口舌与心灵（21-30节）**——智慧心使口有辨别→良言如蜂房→有一条路人以为正。
**白发与谦卑（31-33节）**——白发是荣耀的冠冕→不轻易发怒→人拈阄决定事情耶和华掌权。

## Structure (English)

**God's Sovereignty (vv. 1-9)**—Plans belong to man → answer from LORD → commit to LORD → man plans → LORD establishes.
**Pride's Danger (vv. 10-20)**—Just balance → arrogant heart abomination → pride before destruction.
**Tongue and Heart (vv. 21-30)**—Wise heart makes speech judicious → gracious words like honeycomb → way seems right but leads to death.
**Gray Hair and Humility (vv. 31-33)**—Gray hair is crown of glory → slow to anger → lot is cast but decision is LORD's.

## 神学要点（中文）

**神的护理与人的责任——改革宗神学核心**
改革宗传统教导神完全的主权和人完全的责任并行不悖。韦敏斯德信条第5章论护理：神……普遍的护理……不取消受造之物的本性，也不束缚二因的自由。箴言16:9是这个神学最精炼的圣经表达——"人心筹划"（人的责任）"耶和华指引"（神的主权）——两者都是真实的。

**"骄傲在败坏以先"——人类的基本罪**
改革宗传统教导骄傲是所有罪的根源。奥古斯丁："一切的罪之母是骄傲。"韦敏斯德信条第6章论原罪——始祖的罪是想"如神"（创3:5）——这是骄傲的原型。"骄傲在败坏以先"不只是人生格言，而是神学律则——骄傲是靠自己代替神的尝试，必然以失败告终。

**"把你的工作交托耶和华"——祷告与信靠**
改革宗传统教导信靠神的主权是信仰生活的核心。韦敏斯德小要理问答第98-107问论主祷文。"交托"（galal，字面意思是"滚到神那里"）——是把重担完全转移给神的行动。这与菲律比书4:6"应当一无挂虑，只要凡事藉着祷告……将你们所要的告诉神"相呼应。

## Theology (English)

**God's Providence and Human Responsibility—Reformed Theology's Core**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's complete sovereignty and human complete responsibility coexist without contradiction. Westminster Confession chapter 5: God's providence...does not take away or constrain the nature of second causes, or their liberty. Proverbs 16:9 is this theology's most refined biblical expression—"man plans" (human responsibility) "LORD establishes" (divine sovereignty)—both genuinely real.

**"Pride Before Destruction"—Humanity's Fundamental Sin**
The Reformed tradition teaches pride is all sin's root. Augustine: "pride is the mother of all sins." Westminster Confession chapter 6—the first parents' sin was wanting to "For God doth know that in the day ye eat thereof, then your eyes shall be opened, and ye shall be as gods, knowing good and evil." (Gen 3:5)—pride's archetype. "Pride before destruction" is not just life wisdom but a theological law—pride is the attempt to replace God with self, inevitably ending in failure.

**"Commit Your Work to the LORD"—Prayer and Trust**
The Reformed tradition teaches trusting God's sovereignty is faith life's core. "Commit" (galal, literally "roll onto God")—the action of completely transferring burdens to God. Echoing Philippians 4:6 "do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer...let your requests be made known to God."

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"惟耶和华指引他的脚步"——基督完全的顺服**——约翰福音5:19"子凭着自己不能做什么，惟有看见父所做的，子才能做。"基督是"神指引脚步"的完全体现——祂不自主行事，而是完全顺服父神的引导。这是"人心筹划耶和华指引"在圣子身上的完美实现。

**"骄傲在败坏以先"——基督的谦卑拯救骄傲的世界**——腓立比书2:3-8"不要从自私自利……只要存心谦卑……基督耶稣……将自己倒空……存心顺服以至于死。"基督以最深的谦卑进入世界——以最深的谦卑死在十字架——对抗了人类骄傲的原罪，开了谦卑得救的道路。

**"良言如蜂房"——基督是生命的粮**——约翰福音6:35"我就是生命的粮；到我这里来的必定不饿。"基督的每一句话都是"良言"——对尼哥德慕是"你必须重生"、对撒玛利亚妇人是"活水"、对门徒是"我是葡萄树你们是枝子"——每一句都像蜂房一样，甜的、有益的、医治的。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"The LORD Establishes His Steps"—Christ's Perfect Obedience**—John 5:19: "the Son can do nothing of his own accord, but only what he sees the Father doing." Christ is the complete embodiment of "God directing steps"—He doesn't act independently but completely submits to the Father's leading. This is "man plans, LORD establishes" perfectly realized in the Son.

**"Pride Before Destruction"—Christ's Humility Saves Proud World**—Philippians 2:3-8: "Do nothing from selfish ambition...in humility count others more significant...Christ Jesus...emptied himself...obedient to the point of death." Christ entered the world in deepest humility—died on the cross in deepest humility—countering humanity's original sin of pride, opening the path of salvation through humility.

**"Gracious Words Like Honeycomb"—Christ Is the Bread of Life**—John 6:35: "I am the bread of life; whoever comes to me shall not hunger." Every word Christ speaks is "gracious words"—to Nicodemus "you must be born again," to the Samaritan woman "living water," to disciples "I am the vine, you are the branches"—each like honeycomb: sweet, nourishing, healing.
