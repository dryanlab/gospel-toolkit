## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是所罗门。箴言第十四章包含了箴言中最可怕的一句警告——"有一条路人以为正其终端乃死亡之路"——以及关于贫富、智愚、喜忧的深刻观察。

**智慧妇人与愚妄妇人（第1节）**

> "智慧妇人建立家室；愚妄妇人亲手拆毁。"

弟兄姐妹，"建立"和"拆毁"——两个完全相反的动作——而工具是同一双手。弟兄姐妹，你的手在"建立"还是"拆毁"你的家？你的话在巩固还是瓦解你的家庭关系？弟兄姐妹，家庭不是自然就会好的——需要智慧来"建立"。而拆毁一个家——只需要愚妄就够了。

**最可怕的警告（第12节）**

> "有一条路，人以为正，至终成为死亡之路。"

弟兄姐妹，这句话如此重要，以至于在箴言16:25又重复了一次。"人以为正"——弟兄姐妹，问题不在于你走了一条明显错误的路——而在于你走了一条"你以为正确"的路。弟兄姐妹，最危险的不是你知道自己走错了——最危险的是你坚信自己走对了。

弟兄姐妹，这是为什么我们需要圣经、需要教会、需要弟兄姐妹的提醒——因为我们自己的判断是不可靠的。耶利米书17:9"人心比万物都诡诈，坏到极处，谁能识透呢？"你不能信任你自己的心——因为你的心会骗你。弟兄姐妹，那条"你以为正"的路——可能正在带你走向死亡——而你还以为在走向成功。

**笑中有忧（第13节）**

> "人在喜笑中，心也忧愁；快乐至极就生愁苦。"

弟兄姐妹，"笑中有忧"——这是对人类情感最深刻的观察之一。弟兄姐妹，在最热闹的派对上你有没有突然感到空虚？在笑声最大的时候心里突然涌上忧愁？弟兄姐妹，世界的快乐是掺杂忧愁的——因为世界的快乐是暂时的。"快乐至极就生愁苦"——欢乐达到顶点的那一刻——你已经知道它要结束了——愁苦就从那一刻开始。弟兄姐妹，唯一不会"至极就生愁苦"的快乐是在神里面的喜乐——因为那喜乐是永恒的。

**贫富与邻舍（第20-21节）**

> "贫穷人连邻舍也恨他；富足人朋友最多。藐视邻舍的，这人有罪；怜悯贫穷的，这人有福。"

弟兄姐妹，"贫穷人连邻舍也恨他"——弟兄姐妹，这不是说贫穷是罪——而是描述一个残酷的社会现实。穷的时候朋友都走了——富的时候朋友都来了。弟兄姐妹，你的"朋友"是因为你这个人而来的——还是因为你的钱而来的？箴言让你看清现实——然后作出智慧的选择。

"藐视邻舍的这人有罪"——弟兄姐妹，看不起穷人不只是不好——而是"有罪"。因为穷人也是按神的形像造的。弟兄姐妹，"怜悯贫穷的这人有福"——你怎样对待最弱小的人——决定了你是有福还是有罪。

**敬畏耶和华是生命的泉源（第26-27节）**

> "敬畏耶和华的，大有倚靠；他的儿女也有避难所。敬畏耶和华就是生命的泉源，可以使人离开死亡的网罗。"

弟兄姐妹，"大有倚靠"——弟兄姐妹，在不确定的世界里，你最需要的就是一个可以"倚靠"的。钱可以贬值——健康可以失去——朋友可以离开——但"敬畏耶和华的大有倚靠"。

"他的儿女也有避难所"——弟兄姐妹，你的敬畏不只保护你自己——还保护你的下一代。弟兄姐妹，你今天的信仰选择在为你孩子的明天建造"避难所"。

弟兄姐妹，箴言十四章最核心的信息是这个：不要相信自己的判断——"有一条路人以为正至终成为死亡之路"。你需要一个比自己更可靠的标准——那就是敬畏耶和华。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Solomon. Proverbs 14 contains Proverbs' most terrifying warning—"there is a way that seems right to a man, but its end is the way to death"—along with profound observations about rich and poor, wise and foolish, joy and sorrow.

**The Wise Woman and the Foolish Woman (v. 1)**

> "The wisest of women builds her house, but folly with her own hands tears it down."

Brothers and sisters, "builds" and "tears down"—opposite actions—using the same hands. Are your hands "building" or "tearing down" your home? A family doesn't naturally go well—it needs wisdom to "build." But tearing down a home—foolishness alone suffices.

**The Most Terrifying Warning (v. 12)**

> "There is a way that seems right to a man, but its end is the way to death."

Brothers and sisters, so important it's repeated in Proverbs 16:25. "Seems right"—the problem isn't walking an obviously wrong path—but walking one "you think is right." The most dangerous state isn't knowing you're wrong—it's being convinced you're right.

This is why we need Scripture, church, brothers and sisters' reminders—because our own judgment is unreliable. Jeremiah 17:9: "The heart is deceitful above all things, and desperately sick; who can understand it?" That path "seeming right" to you—may be leading you to death—while you think you're heading toward success.

**Sorrow in Laughter (v. 13)**

> "Even in laughter the heart may ache, and the end of joy may be grief."

Brothers and sisters, "sorrow in laughter"—one of the most profound observations of human emotion. At the loudest laughter, have you suddenly felt emptiness? The world's joy is mixed with sorrow—because it's temporary. "The end of joy may be grief"—at joy's peak, you already know it's ending—grief begins from that moment. The only joy that doesn't end in grief is joy in God—because that joy is eternal.

**Fear of the LORD Is Life's Fountain (vv. 26-27)**

> "In the fear of the LORD one has strong confidence, and his children will have a refuge. The fear of the LORD is a fountain of life, that one may turn away from the snares of death."

Brothers and sisters, "strong confidence"—in an uncertain world, you most need something to "rely on." Money devalues—health fails—friends leave—but "in the fear of the LORD one has strong confidence." "His children will have a refuge"—your fear doesn't only protect you—but your next generation.

Brothers and sisters, Proverbs 14's core message: don't trust your own judgment—"there is a way that seems right but its end is death." You need a more reliable standard than yourself—that is fearing the LORD.

## 历史背景（中文）

箴言14:12"有一条路人以为正至终成为死亡之路"在箴言16:25几乎一字不差地重复——这在箴言书中很少见，显示了这句格言的极端重要性。古代以色列社会中的"道路"比喻在智慧文学中非常常见——"两条道路"的主题贯穿诗篇1篇、箴言全书和福音书。第13节"人在喜笑中心也忧愁"被传道书的作者扩展为整本书的主题——人间一切的欢乐都掺杂着虚空。"敬畏耶和华是生命的泉源"（第27节）与箴言13:14"智慧人的法则是生命的泉源"形成对照——说明真正的智慧就是敬畏耶和华。

## Historical Context (English)

Proverbs 14:12 "there is a way that seems right to a man, but its end is the way to death" is repeated almost verbatim in Proverbs 16:25—rare in Proverbs, showing this saying's extreme importance. The "way/path" metaphor was very common in ancient Israelite wisdom literature—the "two ways" theme runs through Psalm 1, all of Proverbs, and the Gospels. Verse 13 "even in laughter the heart may ache" was expanded by Ecclesiastes' author into the entire book's theme. "Fear of the LORD is a fountain of life" (v. 27) parallels Proverbs 13:14 "the teaching of the wise is a fountain of life"—showing true wisdom is fearing the LORD.

## 经文结构（中文）

**智愚对比（1-7节）**——智慧妇人建立vs愚妄拆毁→正直人敬畏→愚妄人轻狂自恃。
**死亡之路（8-15节）**——通达人明白道路→有一条路人以为正→笑中有忧→愚蒙人信一切话。
**贫富与邻舍（16-24节）**——谨慎人惧怕远离→贫穷人被恨→藐视邻舍有罪→怜悯贫穷有福。
**敬畏耶和华的福（25-35节）**——敬畏耶和华大有倚靠→生命的泉源→公义使邦国高举。

## Structure (English)

**Wisdom vs. Folly (vv. 1-7)**—Wise woman builds vs. folly tears down → upright fears → fool is reckless.
**The Way to Death (vv. 8-15)**—Prudent understands way → way seems right, ends in death → sorrow in laughter → simple believes everything.
**Rich, Poor, and Neighbor (vv. 16-24)**—Cautious man fears → poor is hated → despising neighbor is sin → merciful to poor is blessed.
**Blessings of Fearing the LORD (vv. 25-35)**—Fear of LORD gives confidence → fountain of life → righteousness exalts a nation.

## 神学要点（中文）

**人心的诡诈——全然败坏**
改革宗传统教导人的全然败坏。韦敏斯德信条第6章论人的堕落。"有一条路人以为正至终成为死亡之路"正是全然败坏的表现——人的理性已经被罪扭曲，不再能可靠地判断善恶。不是说人无法做任何好事——而是说人的判断不能被信任。这是为什么需要圣经作为外在客观的标准。

**"敬畏耶和华是生命的泉源"——归正信仰的中心**
韦敏斯德小要理问答第1问"（一）荣耀神；（二）以祂为乐，直到永远"。敬畏耶和华不是恐惧——而是以神为中心的生活，它产生"生命"而不是死亡。诗36:9"在你那里有生命的源头。"

**"怜悯贫穷的这人有福"——社会公义**
改革宗传统教导信仰有社会伦理的要求。韦敏斯德大要理问答第141问论第八诫的义务包括公义慷慨地借贷给有需要的人。藐视穷人是"有罪"的——这不只是社会问题，而是神学问题。

## Theology (English)

**The Heart's Deceit—Total Depravity**
The Reformed tradition teaches total depravity. Westminster Confession chapter 6.: A way that seems right but ends in death precisely demonstrates total depravity—human reason, twisted by sin, can no longer reliably judge good and evil. This is why Scripture is needed as an external objective standard.

**"Fear of the LORD Is Life's Fountain"—The Center of Reformed Faith**
WSC Q1: "Man's chief end is to glorify God and enjoy him forever." Fearing the LORD is not terror—but a God-centered life producing "life" not death.

**"Merciful to the Poor Is Blessed"—Social Justice**
The Reformed tradition teaches faith has social-ethical demands. WLC Q141 on eighth commandment obligations includes "just and liberal lending to those in need." Despising the poor is "sinful"—not merely a social problem but a theological one.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"有一条路人以为正"——基督是唯一的道路**——约翰福音14:6"我就是道路、真理、生命；若不藉着我，没有人能到父那里去。"人以为正的路千万条——但基督宣告只有一条真路。一切"人以为正"的路最终都是死路——唯一通向生命的路是基督自己。

**"人在喜笑中心也忧愁"——基督赐永恒的喜乐**——约翰福音16:22"你们现在也是忧愁，但我要再见你们，你们的心就喜乐了；这喜乐也没有人能夺去。"世界的喜乐必然会终结——但基督应许的喜乐"没有人能夺去"。

**"他的儿女也有避难所"——基督是我们的避难所**——希伯来书6:18"我们……好像逃往避难所、持定摆在我们前头指望的人可以大得勉励。"箴言14:26的"避难所"在基督里得到了终极实现——基督是信徒和他们儿女永远的避难所。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"A Way That Seems Right"—Christ Is the Only Way**—John 14:6: "I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me." A thousand paths "seem right" to humans—but Christ declares only one true way. All "seemingly right" paths ultimately lead to death—the only path to life is Christ Himself.

**"Sorrow in Laughter"—Christ Gives Eternal Joy**—John 16:22: "you have sorrow now, but I will see you again, and your hearts will rejoice, and no one will take your joy from you." The world's joy inevitably ends—but Christ's promised joy "no one can take away."

**"His Children Have a Refuge"—Christ Is Our Refuge**—Hebrews 6:18: "we who have fled for refuge might have strong encouragement to hold fast to the hope set before us." Proverbs 14:26's "refuge" finds ultimate realization in Christ—Christ is believers' and their children's eternal refuge.
