## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是所罗门。箴言第八章是整本箴言书最壮丽的一章——智慧不再只是一种美德，她在这里以一个位格出现，宣告自己在创造天地之前就已经存在。

**智慧的公开呼唤（第1-5节）**

> "智慧岂不呼叫？聪明岂不发声？她在道旁高处的顶上，在十字路口站立。在城门旁，在城门口，在城门洞，大声说：众人哪，我呼叫你们，向世人发声。"

弟兄姐妹，智慧站在最公开的地方呼叫——道旁、十字路口、城门口。不是在密室里、不是在精英圈子里——而是对"众人"和"世人"。弟兄姐妹，神的智慧从不自私保留——她向所有人公开呼唤。你听不到不是因为她声音小——而是因为你在忙别的事。

**智慧的属性（第6-11节）**

> "你们当听，因我要说极美的话；我张嘴要论正直的事。我的口要发出真理；我的嘴憎恶邪恶。我口中的言语都是公义，并无弯曲乖僻。有聪明的人以为明显，得知识的人以为正直。你们当受我的教训，不受白银；宁得知识，胜过黄金。"

弟兄姐妹，"我的口要发出真理"——智慧的核心是真理。不是实用技巧——不是生存策略——而是"真理"。弟兄姐妹，世界上有很多聪明人——但聪明不等于有智慧。聪明人可以用技巧操纵别人——但智慧人说真理。聪明可以没有道德——智慧不能离开真理。

"宁得知识胜过黄金"——弟兄姐妹，这是价值观的对决。你选什么？黄金还是知识？世界说黄金——智慧说知识。弟兄姐妹，你用一生追求的东西定义了你是谁。

**智慧的果子（第12-21节）**

> "我——智慧以灵明为居所，又寻得知识和谋略。敬畏耶和华在乎恨恶邪恶；骄傲、狂妄、恶道和乖谬的口，都为我所恨恶。我有谋略和真智慧，我乃聪明，我有能力。帝王藉我坐国位；君王藉我定公平。王子和首领、世上一切的审判官都是藉我掌权。"

弟兄姐妹，"帝王藉我坐国位"——不是帝王创造了智慧——而是智慧使帝王能治理。弟兄姐妹，任何不以神的智慧为根基的权力最终都会崩溃。历史上一切暴君的下场都证明了这一点——因为他们的权力不是"藉智慧"而来的。

**智慧在创造之先（第22-31节）**

> "在耶和华造化的起头，在太初创造万物之先，就有了我。从亘古，从太初，未有世界以前，我已被立。没有深渊，没有大水的泉源，我已生出。大山未曾奠定，小山未有之先，我已生出。耶和华还没有创造大地和田野，并世上的土质，我已生出。他立高天，我在那里；他在渊面的周围画出圆圈……那时，我在他那里为工师，日日为他所喜爱，常常在他面前踊跃，踊跃在他为人预备可住之地，也喜悦住在世人之间。"

弟兄姐妹，这是箴言书的巅峰——也是整本旧约最深奥的经文之一。"在太初创造万物之先就有了我"——智慧不是被造物——她在创造之前就存在。"我在他那里为工师"——智慧参与了创造。弟兄姐妹，约翰福音1:1-3"太初有道……万物是藉着他造的"——使徒约翰读到箴言8章的时候一定心领神会：这位在创造之先就存在的"智慧"就是那位"道"——就是基督。

"日日为他所喜爱常常在他面前踊跃"——弟兄姐妹，创造不是一件冷冰冰的工程——而是充满喜乐的。智慧在创造中"踊跃"——在为人预备住所时"喜悦"。弟兄姐妹，你是在喜悦中被创造的——你不是意外的产物，而是智慧"喜悦"中的杰作。

**最后的呼唤（第32-36节）**

> "众子啊，现在要听从我。因为谨守我道的，便为有福。要听教训就得智慧，不可弃绝。听从我、日日在我门口仰望、在我门框旁等候的，那人便为有福。因为寻得我的，就寻得生命，也必蒙耶和华的恩惠。得罪我的，却害了自己的性命；恨恶我的，都喜爱死亡。"

弟兄姐妹，"寻得我的就寻得生命"——约翰福音14:6"我就是道路、真理、生命。"弟兄姐妹，寻得智慧就是寻得基督——寻得基督就是寻得生命。而"恨恶我的都喜爱死亡"——拒绝智慧就是选择死亡。没有中间地带——不是生命就是死亡。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Solomon. Proverbs 8 is Proverbs' most magnificent chapter—wisdom is no longer merely a virtue; she appears here as a person, declaring her existence before the creation of heaven and earth.

**Wisdom's Public Call (vv. 1-5)**

> "Does not wisdom call? Does not understanding raise her voice? On the heights beside the way, at the crossroads she takes her stand; beside the gates in front of the town, at the entrance of the portals she cries aloud."

Brothers and sisters, wisdom stands in the most public places—roadsides, crossroads, city gates. Not in private chambers—but to "all people." God's wisdom is never selfishly withheld—she publicly calls to all. You can't hear not because she's quiet—but because you're busy with other things.

**Wisdom's Character (vv. 6-11)**

> "Hear, for I will speak noble things...my mouth will utter truth...Take my instruction instead of silver, and knowledge rather than choice gold."

Brothers and sisters, "my mouth will utter truth"—wisdom's core is truth. Not practical tricks—not survival strategies—but truth. The world has many clever people—but cleverness isn't wisdom. Clever people can manipulate—but wise people speak truth.

**Wisdom Before Creation (vv. 22-31)**

> "The LORD possessed me at the beginning of his work, the first of his acts of old. Ages ago I was set up, at the first, before the beginning of the earth...when he marked out the foundations of the earth, then I was beside him, like a master workman, and I was daily his delight, rejoicing before him always, rejoicing in his inhabited world and delighting in the children of man."

Brothers and sisters, this is Proverbs' summit—and one of the Old Testament's most profound passages. "Before the beginning of the earth"—wisdom is not created; she existed before creation. "I was beside him, like a master workman"—wisdom participated in creation. John 1:1-3: "In the beginning was the Word...All things were made through him"—when the apostle John read Proverbs 8, he must have understood: this "Wisdom" existing before creation is the "Word"—is Christ.

"I was daily his delight, rejoicing before him always"—creation was not cold engineering—but full of joy. Wisdom "rejoiced" in creation—"delighted" in preparing humanity's dwelling. You were created in delight—not an accident but wisdom's joyful masterpiece.

**The Final Call (vv. 32-36)**

> "For whoever finds me finds life and obtains favor from the LORD, but he who fails to find me injures himself; all who hate me love death."

Brothers and sisters, "whoever finds me finds life"—John 14:6: "I am the way, and the truth, and the life." Finding wisdom is finding Christ—finding Christ is finding life. "All who hate me love death"—rejecting wisdom is choosing death. No middle ground—either life or death.

## 历史背景（中文）

箴言8章是旧约智慧文学的巅峰。第22-31节中"智慧"的身份在教会历史上引发了重大争议。亚流（Arius，约256-336年）用第22节"在耶和华造化的起头就有了我"来论证基督是被造的——"有了我"意味着有一个开始。尼西亚大公会议（325年）对此作出了回应，亚他那修等教父论证这里的"有"（qanah）应理解为"拥有/设立"而非"创造"——智慧是永恒的，不是被造的。歌罗西书1:15-17"他是那不能看见之神的像，是首生的"也呼应了这个教义。犹太传统（如智慧书/便西拉智训）将此章理解为拟人化的智慧属性；基督教传统从使徒时代起就将其理解为基督的预表（前身/预言）。

## Historical Context (English)

Proverbs 8 is wisdom literature's summit. The identity of "Wisdom" in vv. 22-31 sparked major church history controversy. Arius (c. 256-336) used v. 22 "the LORD possessed me at the beginning" to argue Christ was created—"possessed" implies a beginning. The Council of Nicaea (325) responded; Athanasius and other church fathers argued "possessed" (qanah) should be understood as "possessed/established" not "created"—wisdom is eternal, not created. Colossians 1:15-17 "He is the image of the invisible God, the firstborn of all creation" echoes this doctrine. Jewish tradition (Wisdom of Solomon, Sirach) understands this chapter as personified wisdom; Christian tradition from the apostolic age understands it as a type/prophecy of Christ.

## Structure (English)

**Wisdom's Public Call (vv. 1-5)**—Crying aloud → heights, crossroads → city gates → to all people.
**Wisdom's Character (vv. 6-11)**—Noble things → truth → righteousness → better than silver and gold.
**Wisdom's Fruit (vv. 12-21)**—Prudence and counsel → hating evil → kings reign by me → those who love me find wealth.
**Wisdom Before Creation (vv. 22-31)**—Before the beginning → no depths → no mountains → heavens established → master workman → daily delight → rejoicing.
**Final Call (vv. 32-36)**—Listen → wait daily → find me find life → hate me love death.

## 神学要点（中文）

**基督的先在性（Pre-existence of Christ）**
改革宗传统教导基督的永恒先在。韦敏斯德信条第八章论中保基督教导：神的儿子从永远被父所生。箴言8:22-31的"在太初之先就有了我"是基督永恒先在的旧约根据之一——祂不是被造的，而是在创造之先就已经存在。

**创造中的三一论**
改革宗传统教导三一神共同参与创造。韦敏斯德信条第4章论创造。箴言8的"工师"暗示圣子参与创造——与创世记1:2"神的灵运行在水面上"一起，构成了三一创造的旧约基础（圣父命令、圣子执行、圣灵运行）。

**智慧与敬畏的关系**
箴言8:13"敬畏耶和华在乎恨恶邪恶"——敬畏不只是崇拜，还包括道德行为。韦敏斯德小要理问答第1问"荣耀神，以祂为乐"——这包括恨恶祂所恨恶的。真正的敬畏产生道德上的分辨和拒绝。

## Theology (English)

**Pre-existence of Christ**
The Reformed tradition teaches Christ's eternal pre-existence. Westminster Confession chapter 8 on Christ the mediator: "The Son of God...from all eternity...begotten of the Father." Proverbs 8:22-31 is one of the OT foundations for Christ's eternal pre-existence—He is not created but existed before creation.

**Trinitarian Creation**
The Reformed tradition teaches the triune God participated together in creation. Westminster Confession chapter 4. Proverbs 8's: master workman implies the Son's participation—together with Genesis 1:2 "the Spirit of God was hovering over the face of the waters," forming the OT foundation for Trinitarian creation (Father commands, Son executes, Spirit hovers).

**Wisdom and Fear's Relationship**
Proverbs 8:13: "The fear of the LORD is hatred of evil"—fear is not just worship but includes moral action. True fear produces moral discernment and rejection.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**基督是智慧的本体**——哥林多前书1:24"基督总为神的能力，神的智慧。"歌罗西书2:3"所积蓄的一切智慧知识，都在他里面藏着。"箴言8章的智慧在基督里完全成就——祂不只是有智慧，祂就是智慧本身。

**"太初之先就有了我"——约翰福音1:1**——"太初有道，道与神同在，道就是神。"箴言8:22-23与约翰福音1:1-3精确对应——两处都宣告智慧/道在创造之先就存在，两处都宣告智慧/道参与了创造。早期教父们（如奥利金、亚他那修）都看到了这个联系。

**"为工师"——基督是创造的中介**——希伯来书1:2"就在这末世藉着他儿子晓谕我们……也曾藉着他创造诸世界。"歌罗西书1:16"万有都是靠他造的……一概都是藉着他造的，又是为他造的。"箴言8:30的"工师"在新约中得到了最完整的神学阐释——基督是创造的执行者。

**"寻得我的就寻得生命"——基督是生命**——约翰福音11:25"复活在我，生命也在我。"14:6"我就是道路、真理、生命。"箴言8:35的应许在基督里达到终极应验——不只是得到更好的生活，而是得到永恒的生命本身。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**Christ Is Wisdom's Substance**—1 Corinthians 1:24: "Christ the power of God and the wisdom of God." Colossians 2:3: "in whom are hidden all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge." Proverbs 8's wisdom is fully realized in Christ—He doesn't merely have wisdom; He is wisdom itself.

**"Before the Beginning"—John 1:1**—"In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God." Proverbs 8:22-23 precisely corresponds with John 1:1-3—both declare wisdom/Word existed before creation; both declare wisdom/Word participated in creation. Early church fathers (Origen, Athanasius) recognized this connection.

**"Master Workman"—Christ as Creation's Agent**—Hebrews 1:2: "through whom also he created the world." Colossians 1:16: "all things were created through him and for him." Proverbs 8:30's "master workman" receives its fullest theological exposition in the NT—Christ is creation's executor.

**"Whoever Finds Me Finds Life"—Christ Is Life**—John 11:25: "I am the resurrection and the life." John 14:6: "I am the way, and the truth, and the life." Proverbs 8:35's promise reaches ultimate fulfillment in Christ—not just a better life, but eternal life itself.
