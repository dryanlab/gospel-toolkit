## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第三十一章——我即将交出领导权的一章。弟兄姐妹，我一百二十岁了——我对以色列人说"我现在一百二十岁了不能照常出入——耶和华也曾对我说你必不得过这约旦河。"弟兄姐妹，这是我最后的日子。

**权力的交接——约书亚（第1-8节）**

> "摩西去告诉以色列众人说……耶和华你们的神必引导你们过去……耶和华必照他从前对待西宏与噩一样对待他们的。你们当刚强壮胆不要害怕也不要畏惧他们因为耶和华你的神和你同去他必不撇下你也不丢弃你。"

弟兄姐妹，"刚强壮胆"——弟兄姐妹，这句话在这一章中出现了三次。弟兄姐妹，三次——对百姓说一次（第6节）——对约书亚说两次（第7节、第23节）。弟兄姐妹，为什么需要反复说"刚强壮胆"？因为他们害怕。弟兄姐妹，摩西要离开了——四十年的领袖即将去世——迦南的敌人就在前面——他们当然害怕。弟兄姐妹，但信心的勇气不来自对困难的轻视——而来自"耶和华你的神和你同去"这个事实。

弟兄姐妹，"摩西召了约书亚来在以色列众人眼前对他说你当刚强壮胆因为你要和这百姓一同进入耶和华向他们列祖起誓应许所赐之地你也要使他们承受那地为业。"弟兄姐妹，这是公开的权力交接——不是密室里的阴谋——而是在"以色列众人眼前"。弟兄姐妹，合法的领袖交替需要公开性和透明度。

**律法的保存（第9-13节）**

> "摩西将这律法写出来交给抬耶和华约柜的祭司利未的子孙和以色列的众长老。"

弟兄姐妹，摩西不只是口头传讲——他把律法"写出来"。弟兄姐妹，为什么要写？因为口头传承可能走样——但书面文字是确定的。弟兄姐妹，每逢七年的豁免年——在住棚节的时候——要在全民面前宣读律法——"使他们听也使他们学习好敬畏耶和华你们的神谨守遵行这律法的一切话。"弟兄姐妹，律法不只是祭司知道的——全民都要听——包括"他们未曾晓得的儿女"。弟兄姐妹，信仰教育是跨代的——每一代都需要重新听见、重新学习。

**预言以色列的背叛（第14-22节）**

弟兄姐妹，耶和华对我说："你必和你列祖同睡。这百姓要起来在他们所要去的地上在那地的人中随从外邦神行邪淫离弃我违背我与他们所立的约。那时我的怒气必向他们发作——我也必掩面不顾他们。"

弟兄姐妹，"你必和你列祖同睡"——神用最温柔的方式告诉我死亡的消息。弟兄姐妹，但更令我心碎的是接下来的话——"这百姓要起来……离弃我。"弟兄姐妹，我带了他们四十年——教了他们律法——警告了他们一切的后果——但神告诉我他们终究会背叛。弟兄姐妹，作为领袖这是最痛苦的认知——你知道你的劳苦会被辜负。

弟兄姐妹，但神没有因此取消进入应许之地的计划。弟兄姐妹，神知道以色列会悖逆——但祂仍然领他们进去。弟兄姐妹，这就是恩典——不是因为你配得——而是因为祂的信实。

**摩西的歌（第30节）**

"摩西将这一篇歌的话都说与以色列全会众听。"弟兄姐妹，神命令我写一首歌——作为见证——当以色列人在繁华中忘记神的时候——这首歌要做他们的警告和控诉。弟兄姐妹，第三十二章就是这首歌。弟兄姐妹，诗歌比律法条文更容易被记住——旋律会留在心里——即使文字被忘记。弟兄姐妹，神用歌曲来见证——因为歌曲比法律更能穿透人心。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 31—the chapter where I hand over leadership. I am one hundred twenty years old. These are my final days.

**Leadership Transition—Joshua (vv. 1-8)**

> "Be strong and courageous"—appearing three times in this chapter. Why repeat it? Because they were afraid. Moses leaving—forty years' leader dying—Canaan's enemies ahead. But faith's courage comes not from minimizing difficulty—but from "the LORD your God goes with you."

The transition was public—"in the sight of all Israel"—not a back-room deal. Legitimate leadership transition requires openness and transparency.

**Preserving the Law (vv. 9-13)**

Moses wrote the law down and gave it to the Levitical priests and Israel's elders. Why write? Oral tradition can distort—written text is fixed. Every seven years at the Feast of Booths—read the law before all people—including "their children, who have not known it." Faith education is cross-generational—every generation needs to hear and learn anew.

**Predicting Israel's Betrayal (vv. 14-22)**

"This people will rise and whore after the foreign gods...and forsake me and break my covenant." God told me in the gentlest way that I would die—but what broke my heart more was: "this people will...forsake me." As a leader, the most painful knowledge—knowing your labor will be betrayed.

But God didn't cancel the plan. He knew Israel would rebel—yet still led them in. This is grace—not because you deserve it—but because of His faithfulness.

**Moses' Song (v. 30)**

God commanded me to write a song—as a witness—when Israel forgets God in prosperity—this song warns and testifies. Chapter 32 is that song. Songs are remembered longer than legal codes—melodies linger in hearts—even when words are forgotten. God uses song as witness—because song penetrates hearts more than law.

## 历史背景（中文）

每七年在住棚节公开宣读律法的规定（hakhel，意为"聚集"）在犹太传统中是一个重大的公共仪式——密西拿（Sotah 7:8）记载由王在圣殿院子的木台上宣读。犹太传统认为亡约柜放在至圣所中——律法书放在约柜旁边（不是里面——里面是石版）。约书亚被按手立为领袖的仪式与后来犹太拉比的"按手礼"（semikhah）有密切关系——表示权柄和智慧的传递。

## Historical Context (English)

The seven-year public law reading (hakhel, "assembly") was a major public ceremony in Jewish tradition—Mishnah (Sotah 7:8) records the king reading on a wooden platform in the temple court. The law scroll was placed beside the ark (not inside—the stone tablets were inside). Joshua's ordination by laying on of hands relates to later rabbinic semikhah—transmitting authority and wisdom.

## 经文结构（中文）

**摩西的告别（1-6节）**——一百二十岁不能出入→耶和华不许过河→耶和华必引导你们→对待敌人如西宏噩→刚强壮胆→不要害怕。
**约书亚的任命（7-8节）**——在众人面前召约书亚→刚强壮胆→你要领百姓进入→耶和华与你同去→不撇下不丢弃。
**律法的保存（9-13节）**——摩西写律法→交给祭司和长老→每七年住棚节→在全民面前宣读→使儿女也听见学习。
**神预言背叛（14-22节）**——摩西将死→百姓将随从外邦神→离弃我的约→我掩面不顾→多灾多难→写一篇歌作见证。
**吩咐约书亚（23节）**——刚强壮胆→你必领以色列人进地。
**律法书放在约柜旁（24-29节）**——写在书上→交给利未人→放在约柜旁→作见证攻击你们→因为我知道你们的悖逆。
**摩西之歌的开始（30节）**——将歌的话说给全会众。

## Structure (English)

**Moses' Farewell (vv. 1-6)**—120 years old → can't go over → LORD will lead → treat enemies like Sihon/Og → be strong/courageous → don't fear.
**Joshua's Commission (vv. 7-8)**—Called publicly → be strong → you will lead the people → LORD goes with you → won't leave/forsake.
**Law Preservation (vv. 9-13)**—Moses wrote law → gave to priests/elders → every seven years at Booths → read before all → children hear and learn.
**God Predicts Betrayal (vv. 14-22)**—Moses will die → people will follow foreign gods → forsake covenant → God hides face → many disasters → write a song as witness.
**Joshua Charged (v. 23)**—Be strong → you will bring Israel into the land.
**Law Book Beside the Ark (vv. 24-29)**—Written in book → given to Levites → placed beside ark → as witness against you → for I know your rebellion.
**Moses' Song Begins (v. 30)**—Spoke song's words to all assembly.

## 神学要点（中文）

**"刚强壮胆"——信心的勇气来自神的同在**
改革宗传统教导信心的勇气基于神的应许而非人的能力。韦敏斯德信条第5章论护理。"耶和华必不撇下你也不丢弃你"——勇气的根基是神的同在和信实——不是你的力量或才能。希伯来书13:5-6引用了这段话："主曾说我总不撇下你也不丢弃你。所以我们可以放胆说主是帮助我的。"

**律法的书面保存——圣经的权威和保存**
改革宗传统教导圣经是神话语的最终权威形式。韦敏斯德信条第1章。摩西把律法写下来——不是留给口头传统——而是固定在文字中。这奠定了"唯独圣经"的原则——神的话语以书面形式保存——不受人的更改和扭曲。

**神预知背叛仍然施恩——无条件拣选**
改革宗传统教导神的拣选不基于人的行为。韦敏斯德信条第3章。神明知以色列会背叛——但仍然领他们进应许之地。这是无条件恩典的最清晰展示——恩典不基于你的表现——而基于神自己的信实和目的。

## Theology (English)

**"Be Strong and Courageous"—Faith's Courage from God's Presence**
The Reformed tradition teaches faith's courage is based on God's promises, not human ability. Westminster Confession chapter 5. Hebrews 13:5-6 quotes this: I will never leave you nor forsake you.

**Written Law Preservation—Scripture's Authority**
The Reformed tradition teaches Scripture is God's word's final authoritative form. Westminster Confession chapter 1. Moses wrote the law down—establishing "sola scriptura"—God's word preserved in writing—protected from human alteration.

**God Foreknows Betrayal Yet Shows Grace—Unconditional Election**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's election isn't based on human behavior. Westminster Confession chapter 3. God knew Israel would rebel—yet still led them into the promised land. Grace isn't based on your performance—but on God's own faithfulness and purpose.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"耶和华必不撇下你也不丢弃你"——基督永远不离弃我们**——希伯来书13:5"因为主曾说我总不撇下你也不丢弃你。"摩西对约书亚说"不撇下不丢弃"——希伯来书作者把这句话给了每一个基督徒。弟兄姐妹，摩西会死——约书亚会死——但基督"昨日今日一直到永远是一样的"（来13:8）——祂永远不离弃。

**摩西的交接——基督是更伟大的约书亚**——弟兄姐妹，"约书亚"和"耶稣"是同一个名字——希伯来文Yehoshua（耶和华是拯救）。摩西不能带以色列人过约旦河——约书亚可以。律法不能带我们进入安息——但耶稣可以。希伯来书4:8"若是约书亚已叫他们享了安息后来神就不再提别的日子了。"弟兄姐妹，摩西（律法）把你带到门口——但约书亚（耶稣/恩典）带你进去。

**律法书放在约柜旁——基督成全了律法**——马太福音5:17"莫想我来要废掉律法和先知。我来不是要废掉乃是要成全。"律法书放在约柜旁边——基督把律法放在心里面——不再是外面的见证——而是里面的生命。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Will Not Leave or Forsake"—Christ Never Abandons**—Hebrews 13:5 quotes this for every Christian. Moses dies—Joshua dies—but Christ "Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever." (Heb 13:8)—He never forsakes.

**Moses' Transition—Christ the Greater Joshua**—"Joshua" and "Jesus" are the same name—Hebrew Yehoshua (the LORD saves). Moses couldn't bring Israel across Jordan—Joshua could. Law can't bring us to rest—but Jesus can. Hebrews 4:8. Moses (law) brings you to the door—Joshua (Jesus/grace) brings you in.

**Law Beside the Ark—Christ Fulfilled the Law**—Matthew 5:17: "I have not come to abolish them but to fulfill them." The law scroll beside the ark—Christ puts the law inside the heart—no longer external witness—but internal life.
