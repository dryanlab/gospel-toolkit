## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第十一章是摩西第二篇讲论的结语——把选择摆在以色列人面前：祝福或咒诅。这一章把应许之地描绘为一块需要信心的土地——不像埃及靠尼罗河灌溉——迦南要靠天上降的雨。

**记念神的大能作为（第1-7节）**

> "你要爱耶和华你的神，常守他的吩咐、律例、典章、诫命。"

弟兄姐妹，"你要爱耶和华"——第六章已经说过——第十一章再说一次。弟兄姐妹，为什么反复说？因为爱是律法的总纲——如果你真的爱神——遵守诫命就不是负担而是喜乐。弟兄姐妹，摩西列举了神的大能作为——出埃及、红海、旷野的供应——每一件都是爱的证据。弟兄姐妹，你为什么要爱神？不是因为"应该"——而是因为祂先爱了你——祂的大能作为就是祂爱你的证据。

**不像埃及——需要信心的土地（第10-15节）**

> "你要进去得为业的那地，本不像你出来的埃及地。你在那里撒种，用脚浇灌，像浇灌菜园一样。你们要过去得为业的那地乃是有山有谷、雨水滋润之地，是耶和华你神所眷顾的，从岁首到年终耶和华你神的眼目时常看顾那地。"

弟兄姐妹，这是一个非常深刻的对比。埃及——靠尼罗河灌溉——你用脚踩水车就能浇灌——一切在你的掌控中。但迦南——"雨水滋润之地"——你得靠天上降雨——降不降雨不在你的控制中。弟兄姐妹，从人的角度看——埃及更"安全"——水源可控。但神把以色列人从可控的埃及带到了不可控的迦南——为什么？弟兄姐妹，因为"不可控"迫使你信靠神。

弟兄姐妹，"从岁首到年终耶和华你神的眼目时常看顾那地"——弟兄姐妹，雨水的来源虽然"不可控"——但看顾那地的是耶和华的"眼目"——"时常"看顾。弟兄姐妹，你觉得你的生活"不可控"——但看顾你的那位"时常"在看着。你不能控制明天——但能控制明天的那位在看顾你。

**如果你们听从（第13-17节）**

> "你们若留意听从我今日所吩咐的诫命，爱耶和华你们的神，尽心尽性侍奉他，他必按时降秋雨春雨在你们的地上，使你们可以收藏五谷、新酒和油。"

弟兄姐妹，"秋雨春雨"——秋雨在十月至十一月（播种后滋润土地）——春雨在三月至四月（收割前最后的滋润）。弟兄姐妹，农业社会中没有什么比雨水更关键了——雨水决定了你这一年是丰收还是饥荒。弟兄姐妹，神把以色列的经济命脉——雨水——直接跟他们的顺服挂钩。弟兄姐妹，这不是"成功神学"——这是约的条款——旧约的约包含了物质层面的祝福和咒诅——新约的祝福更侧重属灵的层面。

"你们要谨慎，免得心中受迷惑，就偏离正路，去侍奉敬拜别神。耶和华的怒气向你们发作，就使天闭塞不下雨。"弟兄姐妹，不下雨——在古代就是死刑判决。弟兄姐妹，以利亚时代就经历了这个——三年半不降雨（王上17-18章）。

**基利心山与以巴路山（第26-32节）**

> "看哪，我今日将祝福与咒诅的话都陈明在你们面前。你们若听从耶和华你们神的诫命就必蒙福；你们若不听从耶和华你们神的诫命就必受咒诅。你就要将祝福的话陈明在基利心山上，将咒诅的话陈明在以巴路山上。"

弟兄姐妹，基利心山和以巴路山——两座山面对面——中间是示剑谷。后来在约书亚记8:30-35中，以色列人真的在这两座山上举行了这个仪式——六个支派站在基利心山宣读祝福——六个支派站在以巴路山宣读咒诅。弟兄姐妹，两座山、两条路——你选哪一边？弟兄姐妹，生命就是选择——而选择的后果是真实的。

弟兄姐妹，申命记第十一章告诉我们：应许之地是需要信心的土地——你必须信靠降雨的那位。祝福与咒诅摆在你面前——选择在你。但愿你选择祝福的路。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 11 concludes my second address—placing the choice before Israel: blessing or curse. This chapter portrays the Promised Land as a land requiring faith—unlike Egypt with its Nile irrigation, Canaan depends on rain from heaven.

**Remember God's Mighty Acts (vv. 1-7)**

"You shall therefore love the LORD your God and keep his charge." Why repeat? Because love is the law's summary—if you truly love God—obedience isn't burden but joy.

**Not Like Egypt—A Land Requiring Faith (vv. 10-15)**

> "For the land that you are entering to take possession of it is not like the land of Egypt...It is a land of hills and valleys, which drinks water by the rain from heaven, a land that the LORD your God cares for. The eyes of the LORD your God are always upon it."

Brothers and sisters, a profound contrast. Egypt—Nile irrigation—you step on the water wheel—everything under your control. But Canaan—"drinks water by rain from heaven"—rain isn't under your control. Humanly speaking, Egypt is "safer"—controllable water source. But God brought Israel from controllable Egypt to uncontrollable Canaan—why? Because "uncontrollable" forces you to trust God.

"The eyes of the LORD your God are always upon it"—though rain is "uncontrollable"—the One watching the land is the LORD—"always" watching. You can't control tomorrow—but the One who controls tomorrow is watching over you.

**If You Obey (vv. 13-17)**

"He will give the rain for your land in its season, the early rain and the later rain." Autumn rain (October-November, moistening soil after sowing) and spring rain (March-April, final moisture before harvest). God tied Israel's economic lifeline—rain—directly to their obedience.

**Mount Gerizim and Mount Ebal (vv. 26-32)**

"See, I am setting before you today a blessing and a curse: the blessing, if you obey...the curse, if you do not obey." Two mountains facing each other—Shechem valley between. Later in Joshua 8:30-35, Israel actually performed this ceremony. Two mountains, two paths—which side do you choose? Life is choice—and choices have real consequences.

## 历史背景（中文）

基利心山和以巴路山位于示剑（今纳布卢斯）附近——基利心山在南，以巴路山在北，中间的示剑谷约有600米宽。这个地理位置有深厚的历史意义——亚伯拉罕最初进入迦南时就在示剑停留（创12:6）。约书亚记8:30-35记载了约书亚在这两座山上执行摩西的命令。申命记的"秋雨春雨"反映了巴勒斯坦地区独特的雨季模式——"早雨"（yoreh）约在十至十一月，软化夏季干燥的土壤以便播种——"晚雨"（malqosh）约在三至四月，在收割前给庄稼最后的滋润。五月到九月几乎完全无雨——这使得以色列人对雨水的依赖和对神的信靠成为日常生活的核心。

## Historical Context (English)

Mount Gerizim and Mount Ebal are near Shechem (modern Nablus)—Gerizim to the south, Ebal to the north, Shechem valley about 600 meters wide between them. This location has deep historical significance—Abraham first stopped at Shechem entering Canaan (Gen 12:6). Joshua 8:30-35 records Joshua executing Moses' command at these mountains. Deuteronomy's "early and later rain" reflects Palestine's unique rain pattern—"early rain" (yoreh) in October-November softens summer-dried soil for sowing—"later rain" (malqosh) in March-April gives crops final moisture before harvest. May through September is almost completely rainless.

## 经文结构（中文）

**爱与记念（1-7节）**——爱耶和华守诫命→记念大能作为→出埃及红海旷野→你们亲眼看见。
**不像埃及的土地（8-12节）**——遵守诫命好进美地→不像埃及用脚浇灌→有山有谷雨水滋润→耶和华眼目看顾。
**顺从与悖逆的后果（13-21节）**——听从就降秋雨春雨→谨慎免得迷惑→不下雨就灭亡→将话记在心上→教训儿女。
**应许之地的疆界（22-25节）**——谨守遵行→从旷野到利巴嫩→无人能在你面前站立。
**祝福与咒诅（26-32节）**——今日陈明祝福与咒诅→听从就蒙福→不听就受咒诅→基利心山祝福以巴路山咒诅。

## Structure (English)

**Love and Remember (vv. 1-7)**—Love LORD, keep commands → remember mighty acts → Exodus, Red Sea, wilderness → your eyes saw.
**Not Like Egypt (vv. 8-12)**—Keep commands to enter good land → not like Egypt's foot-irrigation → hills and valleys, rain-watered → LORD's eyes always upon it.
**Consequences of Obedience/Rebellion (vv. 13-21)**—Obey: early/later rain → beware lest deceived → no rain: perish → keep words in heart → teach children.
**Land's Boundaries (vv. 22-25)**—Carefully obey → from wilderness to Lebanon → none shall stand against you.
**Blessing and Curse (vv. 26-32)**—Today set before you blessing and curse → obey: blessed → disobey: cursed → Gerizim blessing, Ebal curse.

## 神学要点（中文）

**约的祝福与咒诅——圣约神学**
改革宗圣约神学教导神与人的关系以约为框架。韦敏斯德信条第7章论神与人所立的约。祝福与咒诅是约的两面——顺服带来祝福，悖逆带来咒诅。这不是"交易"——而是约的条款。新约的祝福不再以物质丰富为主——而是属灵的福分（弗1:3"在基督里赐给我们天上各样属灵的福气"）。

**信心的土地——从可控到信靠**
改革宗传统教导信心是信靠神的应许。韦敏斯德信条第14章论得救的信心。埃及代表人的掌控——迦南代表对神的信靠。神把我们从"可控"带到"不可控"——不是要让我们无助——而是要让我们学会信靠。很多时候神拿走我们的"安全网"——正是为了教我们依靠祂。

**选择的真实性**
改革宗传统教导神的主权与人的责任并行不悖。韦敏斯德信条第3章与第9章。"看哪我今日将祝福与咒诅陈明在你们面前"——选择是真实的——后果也是真实的。神的预定不排除人的选择——而是通过人的选择来实现。

## Theology (English)

**Covenant Blessings and Curses—Covenant Theology**
Reformed covenant theology teaches God's relationship with humanity is framed by covenant. Westminster Confession chapter 7. Blessings and curses are covenant's two sides—obedience brings blessing, rebellion brings curse. New covenant blessings are primarily spiritual (Eph 1:3).

**A Land of Faith—From Control to Trust**
The Reformed tradition teaches faith is trusting God's promises. Westminster Confession chapter 14. Egypt represents human control—Canaan represents trusting God. God brings us from "controllable" to "uncontrollable"—not to leave us helpless—but to teach us trust.

**The Reality of Choice**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's sovereignty and human responsibility run parallel. Westminster Confession chapters 3 and 9.: See, I set before you blessing and curse—choices are real—consequences are real. God's predestination doesn't exclude human choice—but works through it.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**秋雨春雨——圣灵的浇灌**——约珥书2:23,28"他必为你们降秋雨，使你们的场上满了麦子……以后我要将我的灵浇灌凡有血气的。"旧约的秋雨春雨预表了圣灵的浇灌——五旬节是属灵的"春雨"——圣灵大能降临——使教会得着能力。

**基利心山与以巴路山——律法的咒诅与基督的代赎**——加拉太书3:13"基督既为我们受了咒诅，就赎出我们脱离律法的咒诅；因为经上记着'凡挂在木头上都是被咒诅的。'"以巴路山上的咒诅最终落在了基督身上——祂代替我们承受了律法的咒诅——使我们可以得到基利心山上的祝福。

**不像埃及——基督带我们进入信心的生活**——哥林多后书5:7"因我们行事为人是凭着信心，不是凭着眼见。"从可控的埃及到信心的迦南——基督呼召我们离开"凭眼见"的生活——进入"凭信心"的生活。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**Early and Later Rain—The Spirit's Outpouring**—Joel 2:23,28: "He has poured down for you abundant rain...And it shall come to pass afterward, that I will pour out my Spirit on all flesh." OT early/later rain foreshadows the Spirit's outpouring—Pentecost was the spiritual "spring rain."

**Gerizim and Ebal—Law's Curse and Christ's Substitution**—Galatians 3:13: "Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law by becoming a curse for us—for it is written, 'Cursed is everyone who is hanged on a tree.'" Ebal's curse ultimately fell on Christ—He bore the law's curse in our place—so we can receive Gerizim's blessing.

**Not Like Egypt—Christ Leads Into Faith's Life**—2 Corinthians 5:7: "For we walk by faith, not by sight." From controllable Egypt to faith's Canaan—Christ calls us from "by sight" living into "by faith" living.
