## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第二十五章涵盖了打罚的限制、弟兄的义务、诚实的度量衡，以及消灭亚玛力人的命令。这一章展示了神律法的精准——即使在惩罚中也有节制，即使在交易中也有公义。

**打罚的限制（第1-3节）**

> "人若有争讼来听审判，审判官就要定义人有理定恶人有罪。恶人若该受责打，审判官就要叫他当面伏在地上，按着他的罪照数责打。只可打他四十下不可过数，恐怕过数使你的弟兄受辱太甚你就把他看轻了。"

弟兄姐妹，"四十下不可过数"——弟兄姐妹，惩罚是合法的——但惩罚有上限。弟兄姐妹，"恐怕过数使你的弟兄受辱太甚"——注意——即使他是"恶人"——摩西仍然称他为"你的弟兄"！弟兄姐妹，犯了罪的人仍然是你的弟兄——惩罚的目的是纠正——不是摧毁。弟兄姐妹，过度的惩罚使人"受辱太甚"——就把人当成了畜生而非弟兄。弟兄姐妹，犹太传统为了绝对避免超过四十——定为三十九下——这就是保罗说的"犹太人打了我五次每次四十减去一下"（林后11:24）。

> **"不可笼住牛的嘴"（第4节）**

> "牛在场上踹谷的时候，不可笼住它的嘴。"

弟兄姐妹，这条律法看似是关于牛的——但保罗在哥林多前书9:9-10引用它来论传道人该得工价："难道神所挂念的是牛吗？不全是为我们说的吗？"弟兄姐妹，如果连牛在劳作的时候都有权吃——何况为神做工的人呢？弟兄姐妹，每一个劳动者都有权分享他劳动的成果——这是神律法的基本公义。

**弟兄的义务——娶兄嫂之律（第5-10节）**

> "弟兄同居若其中一个死了没有儿子，死人的妻不可出嫁外人；她丈夫的兄弟当尽弟兄的本分娶她为妻，与她同房。她所生的长子必归死兄的名下，免得他的名在以色列中涂抹了。"

弟兄姐妹，这就是"利未拉特婚姻"（levirate marriage）——"利未拉特"来自拉丁文"levir"即"丈夫的兄弟"。弟兄姐妹，这个制度的目的不是让寡妇"有人养"——虽然也有这个功能——但核心目的是"免得他的名在以色列中涂抹"——就是为死者延续后裔和名字。弟兄姐妹，在旧约中，没有后裔意味着你的"名"从以色列中被除去——这是比死亡更可怕的命运。弟兄姐妹，路得记中波阿斯娶路得就是行使了这个义务（得4:5-10）。

弟兄姐妹，如果弟兄不肯——嫂子可以在长老面前脱他的鞋吐唾沫在他脸上——"那人的家在以色列中必称为'脱鞋之家'"——弟兄姐妹，这是羞辱——因为拒绝延续弟兄的后裔是不义的行为。

**诚实的度量衡（第13-16节）**

> "你囊中不可有一大一小两样的法码。你家里不可有一大一小两样的升斗。当用对准公平的法码公平的升斗。"

弟兄姐妹，"一大一小两样的法码"——买东西用大秤（多量给自己）卖东西用小秤（少量给别人）——这是商业欺诈最古老的形式。弟兄姐妹，箴言11:1"诡诈的天平为耶和华所憎恶；公平的法码为他所喜悦。"弟兄姐妹，你在别人看不见的地方是否也使用"公平的秤"？弟兄姐妹，诚实不只是大事上的——更是"法码"和"升斗"这些日常小事上的。

**记念亚玛力人（第17-19节）**

> "你要记念你们出埃及的时候亚玛力人在路上怎样待你。他们在路上遇见你就击杀你尽后边软弱的人，那时你又疲乏又困倦。你要将亚玛力的名号从天下涂抹了，不可忘记。"

弟兄姐妹，亚玛力人攻击的是"尽后边软弱的人"——老人、妇女、儿童、疲乏的人。弟兄姐妹，欺负弱者——这是亚玛力人的罪。弟兄姐妹，在战争中攻击强者是可以理解的——但专门攻击软弱的人——这是卑鄙。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 25 covers punishment limits, fraternal obligations, honest weights, and Amalek's elimination. This chapter shows the law's precision—restraint even in punishment, justice even in commerce.

**Punishment Limits (vv. 1-3)**

"Forty stripes may be given him, but not more, lest...your brother be degraded in your sight." Even the "wicked" person is still called "your brother"! Punishment's purpose is correction—not destruction. Excessive punishment degrades a person from brother to beast. Jewish tradition set thirty-nine stripes to absolutely avoid exceeding forty—hence Paul's "Of the Jews five times received I forty stripes save one." (2 Cor 11:24).

> **"Don't Muzzle the Ox" (v. 4)**

Paul quoted this in 1 Corinthians 9:9-10 for preachers' wages: "Is it for oxen that God is concerned? Or does he speak entirely for our sake?" If even an ox working may eat—how much more those working for God? Every laborer deserves to share in their labor's fruit.

**Levirate Marriage (vv. 5-10)**

"If brothers dwell together, and one dies having no son...her husband's brother shall...take her as his wife." The levirate marriage's core purpose: "that his name may not be blotted out of Israel"—continuing the deceased's lineage. Boaz married Ruth exercising this duty (Ruth 4:5-10).

**Honest Weights (vv. 13-16)**

> "You shall not have two differing weights in your bag...You shall have a full and fair weight." The oldest commercial fraud: large measures for buying, small for selling. Proverbs 11:1: "A false balance is an abomination to the LORD; but a just weight is his delight." Honesty isn't just in big matters—but in daily "weights and measures."

**Remember Amalek (vv. 17-19)**

"He attacked you...all who were lagging behind you, when you were faint and weary." Amalek attacked the weakest—elderly, women, children, exhausted. Bullying the vulnerable—that's Amalek's sin.

## 历史背景（中文）

"四十减去一下"（39下）的做法在犹太传统中有明确记载——密西拿（Makkot 3:10）规定用三条皮带的鞭子打十三下（胸部一下、背部两下，共13轮=39下）。利未拉特婚姻（levirate）在创世记38章犹大和他玛的故事中已有先例——他玛因为犹大没有让第三个儿子示拉娶她而假装妓女引诱犹大——犹大最终承认"她比我更有义"。撒都该人用利未拉特婚姻来质问耶稣关于复活的问题（太22:23-33）。亚玛力人是以扫的后裔（创36:12）——他们在以色列出埃及后最脆弱的时候发动攻击（出17:8-16）。扫罗因为没有彻底执行灭绝亚玛力人的命令而失去王位（撒上15章）。

## Historical Context (English)

"Forty less one" (39 stripes) is documented in the Mishnah (Makkot 3:10)—thirteen rounds of three-strap whip. Levirate marriage precedent in Genesis 38 (Judah and Tamar). Sadducees used levirate marriage to question Jesus about resurrection (Matt 22:23-33). Amalekites descended from Esau (Gen 36:12)—attacked Israel at their most vulnerable after Egypt (Ex 17:8-16). Saul lost his throne for not fully executing Amalek's destruction (1 Sam 15).

## 经文结构（中文）

**打罚限制（1-3节）**——审判定义人有理→恶人当面受罚→四十下不可过数→免得受辱太甚。
**牛在场上（4节）**——不可笼住踹谷之牛的嘴。
**弟兄的义务（5-10节）**——弟兄死无子→兄弟当娶嫂→长子归死兄名下→若不肯→脱鞋吐唾沫→称为脱鞋之家。
**打斗中的不端（11-12节）**——妻子帮丈夫打斗时不可抓对方的下体→砍断她的手不可怜恤。
**诚实度量衡（13-16节）**——不可有大小两样法码→公平法码公平升斗→诡诈者为神所憎恶。
**消灭亚玛力（17-19节）**——记念亚玛力在路上的恶行→击杀后边软弱的人→要涂抹他的名号→不可忘记。

## Structure (English)

**Punishment Limits (vv. 1-3)**—Judge determines right/wrong → punish in presence → forty stripes maximum → lest degraded.
**Ox Threshing (v. 4)**—Don't muzzle ox treading grain.
**Levirate Duty (vv. 5-10)**—Brother dies without son → brother marries widow → firstborn carries dead brother's name → if refuses → sandal removal, spit → "house of unsandaled."
**Fighting Indecency (vv. 11-12)**—Wife grabs opponent's genitals while helping husband → cut off hand.
**Honest Weights (vv. 13-16)**—No two different weights → full and fair weight → dishonesty: abomination.
**Destroy Amalek (vv. 17-19)**—Remember Amalek's ambush → attacked weak stragglers → blot out name → don't forget.

## 神学要点（中文）

**惩罚的节制——人的尊严**
改革宗传统教导人是照神形像所造的即使犯罪后仍保留尊严。韦敏斯德信条第4章。"恐怕受辱太甚"——惩罚有上限——因为即使是罪犯也有作为"弟兄"和神的形像承载者的尊严。这个原则是现代反对酷刑和非人道惩罚的圣经根基之一。

**"牛在场上"——劳动者配得报酬**
改革宗传统教导传道人应当被供养。韦敏斯德信条第30章。保罗引用这节经文（林前9:9-10）为传道人得工价辩护——如果连牛都不可被笼住嘴——何况为福音劳苦的人？这个原则扩展到所有劳动者——每个工人都配得他的工价（路10:7）。

**公平的法码——日常生活中的公义**
改革宗传统教导第八诫不仅禁止偷窃还要求公平交易。韦敏斯德大要理问答第141-142问。"诡诈的天平为耶和华所憎恶"——神关心你在商业交易中的诚实——大事上的公义始于小事上的公平。

## Theology (English)

**Restrained Punishment—Human Dignity**
The Reformed tradition teaches humans are made in God's image—retaining dignity even after sinning. Westminster Confession chapter 4. Punishment has limits—because even criminals have dignity as "brothers" and God's image-bearers. This principle underlies modern opposition to torture.

**"Don't Muzzle the Ox"—Workers Deserve Compensation**
The Reformed tradition teaches ministers should be supported. Westminster Confession chapter 30. Paul cited this (1 Cor 9:9-10). Extends to all workers—"And in the same house remain, eating and drinking such things as they give: for the labourer is worthy of his hire. Go not from house to house." (Luke 10:7).

**Fair Weights—Daily Life Justice**
The Reformed tradition teaches the eighth commandment requires fair trade beyond prohibiting theft. WLC Q141-142. "False balance is abomination"—God cares about commercial honesty.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"四十下不可过数"——基督承受了无限的刑罚**——以赛亚书53:5"哪知他为我们的过犯受害，为我们的罪孽压伤。因他受的刑罚我们得平安，因他受的鞭伤我们得医治。"律法限制了人对人的惩罚在四十下——但基督在十字架上承受的刑罚是没有上限的——因为祂承担的是全人类的罪。四十下的限制显示了神对人尊严的保护——基督自愿超越这个限制代替我们承受无限的审判。

**"牛在场上不可笼嘴"——基督配得一切荣耀**——启示录5:12"曾被杀的羔羊是配得权柄、丰富、智慧、能力、尊贵、荣耀、颂赞的。"如果踹谷的牛配得吃——那为我们的救赎献上自己的羔羊配得什么？基督配得一切的荣耀和赞美。

**消灭亚玛力——基督消灭一切恶的权势**——希伯来书2:14"儿女既同有血肉之体，他也照样亲自成了血肉之体，特要藉着死败坏那掌死权的就是魔鬼。"亚玛力人专门攻击软弱的人——魔鬼也是如此——但基督来"败坏那掌死权的"——保护一切软弱的人。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Forty Stripes Maximum"—Christ Bore Unlimited Punishment**—Isaiah 53:5: "He was pierced for our transgressions...by his wounds we are healed." Law limited human punishment to forty—Christ on the cross bore unlimited punishment—carrying all humanity's sin. Forty's limit shows God's protection of dignity—Christ voluntarily exceeded this limit, bearing infinite judgment for us.

**"Don't Muzzle the Ox"—Christ Deserves All Glory**—Revelation 5:12: "Worthy is the Lamb who was slain, to receive power and wealth and wisdom and might and honor and glory and blessing!" If the threshing ox deserves to eat—what does the Lamb sacrificed for our redemption deserve? Christ deserves all glory and praise.

**Destroy Amalek—Christ Destroys All Evil Powers**—Hebrews 2:14: "That through death he might destroy the one who has the power of death, that is, the devil." Amalek specifically attacked the weak—the devil does likewise—but Christ came to "destroy the one who has the power of death"—protecting all the vulnerable.
