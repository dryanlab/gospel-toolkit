## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第十七章涵盖了三个重要主题：献祭的完全性、司法的严肃性、以及对将来王权的规范。其中最引人注目的是第14-20节——神预先为以色列的王立下了规则——这些规则后来被大多数王所忽视，导致了王国的衰败。

**完全的献祭（第1节）**

> "凡有残疾或有什么恶病的牛羊，你都不可献给耶和华你的神，因为这是耶和华你神所憎恶的。"

弟兄姐妹，"残疾或有恶病的"——弟兄姐妹，神只接受"完全"的祭物。弟兄姐妹，玛拉基书中神责备以色列人献瞎眼的、残缺的、患病的牲畜——说"你们把瞎眼的牲畜献上为祭，这不为恶吗"（玛1:8）。弟兄姐妹，献给神最差的——以为神不介意——这是极大的不敬。弟兄姐妹，你对神的态度直接反映在你给神的最好还是最差。

**司法的严肃性（第2-13节）**

> "在耶和华你神所赐你各城中，若有人男或女行耶和华你神眼中看为恶的事违背了他的约，去侍奉别神敬拜……你就要将那男人或女人拉到城门，用石头将他打死。只是死刑必凭两个或三个见证人的口作证，不可凭一个见证人的口作证将人治死。"

弟兄姐妹，"两个或三个见证人"——弟兄姐妹，这是圣经中最重要的司法原则之一。一个人的话不足以定罪——必须有两到三个独立的证人证词相符。弟兄姐妹，这个原则后来被耶稣在马太福音18:16引用——"要凭两三个人的口作见证，句句都可以定准"。使徒保罗也在哥林多后书13:1引用它。弟兄姐妹，神的公义包括程序公义——不能因为"听说"就定人的罪。

**王权的规范（第14-20节）**

> "到了耶和华你神所赐你的地，得了那地居住的时候，若说'我要立王治理我，像四围的国一样'，你总要立耶和华你神所拣选的人为王。必从你弟兄中立一人；不可立你弟兄以外的人为王。只是王不可为自己多添马匹……也不可为自己多立妃嫔……也不可为自己多积金银。"

弟兄姐妹，神在以色列尚未有王的时候就立下了王的规矩——这是多么有远见的立法！弟兄姐妹，三条禁令：不可多马、不可多妃、不可多金银。弟兄姐妹，这三条正好是所罗门王所犯的三条罪——他拥有大量战马（王上10:26）、七百妃嫔（王上11:3）、大量金银（王上10:14-21）——于是"他的心偏邪不专诚"（王上11:4）。

> "他登了王位，就要将祭司利未人面前的这律法书，为自己抄录一本。要平生诵读，学习敬畏耶和华他的神，谨守遵行这律法书上的一切言语和这些律例。"

弟兄姐妹，"要为自己抄录一本"——王亲自抄写律法书——不只是读——是抄写。弟兄姐妹，用手把每一个字都写一遍——这不只是信息的获取——而是把神的话语刻在心里的过程。弟兄姐妹，王的权力越大——越需要神话语的约束。弟兄姐妹，真正的领袖不是那些最不受约束的人——而是最甘心受神话语约束的人。

弟兄姐妹，申命记第十七章告诉我们：给神的必须是最好的、司法必须有证人保证公平、权力必须在神话语下运行。这三个原则在今天都适用。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 17 covers three important themes: the perfection of sacrifice, the seriousness of justice, and regulations for future kingship. Most striking is verses 14-20—God pre-established rules for Israel's kings—rules most kings later ignored, causing the kingdom's downfall.

**Perfect Sacrifice (v. 1)**

"You shall not sacrifice to the LORD your God an ox or a sheep in which is a blemish, any defect whatever." God only accepts "complete" offerings. Malachi rebukes offering blind and sick animals—"And if ye offer the blind for sacrifice, is it not evil? and if ye offer the lame and sick, is it not evil? offer it now unto thy governor; will he be pleased with thee, or accept thy person? saith the LORD of hosts." (Mal 1:8). Giving God the worst—thinking He doesn't mind—is profound disrespect. Your attitude toward God is directly reflected in whether you give Him your best or worst.

**Judicial Seriousness (vv. 2-13)**

"On the evidence of two witnesses or of three witnesses the one who is to die shall be put to death; a person shall not be put to death on the evidence of one witness." One person's word is insufficient for conviction—must have two or three independent witnesses. Jesus quoted this in Matthew 18:16; Paul in 2 Corinthians 13:1. God's justice includes procedural justice—can't condemn someone based on "hearsay."

**Law of the King (vv. 14-20)**

> "When you come to the land...and say, 'I will set a king over me'...he shall not acquire many horses...he shall not acquire many wives...he shall not acquire excessive silver and gold."

God established kingship rules before Israel even had a king—remarkable foresight! Three prohibitions: no many horses, no many wives, no much gold. These are exactly Solomon's three sins—vast horses (1 Kings 10:26), 700 wives (1 Kings 11:3), enormous gold (1 Kings 10:14-21)—then "For it came to pass, when Solomon was old, that his wives turned away his heart after other gods: and his heart was not perfect with the LORD his God, as was the heart of David his father." (1 Kings 11:4).

"He shall write for himself in a book a copy of this law...and he shall read in it all the days of his life." The king personally copies the law—not just reads—writes. Writing every word isn't just information acquisition—it's the process of engraving God's word on the heart. The greater the power—the more you need Scripture's restraint. True leaders aren't those least restrained—but those most willingly under God's word.

## 历史背景（中文）

申命记17章的王权法是旧约中独一无二的宪政性文件——在君主制建立之前就预先限制了王权。撒母耳记上8章记载当以色列人要求立王时，撒母耳警告了王的"规矩"——这与申命记17章形成对应。"不可多马"——古代近东战马代表军事力量，也象征与埃及的联盟（以色列从埃及购买战马）。"不可多妃"——外交婚姻是古代王国外交政策的常见手段——所罗门的外邦妻子带来了偶像崇拜。"自己抄录一本"——犹太传统认为每位国王必须携带两本妥拉：一本放在宫殿，一本随身携带。

## Historical Context (English)

Deuteronomy 17's kingship law is the OT's unique constitutional document—pre-limiting royal power before monarchy was established. 1 Samuel 8 records when Israel demanded a king, Samuel warned of the king's "ways"—corresponding to Deuteronomy 17. "No many horses"—ancient Near Eastern warhorses represented military power and Egyptian alliance. "No many wives"—diplomatic marriage was common foreign policy. Jewish tradition holds each king must carry two Torah scrolls: one in the palace, one personally.

## 经文结构（中文）

**完全的献祭（1节）**——不可献有残疾恶病的牲畜→这是神所憎恶的。
**司法审判（2-13节）**——若有人违约拜别神→凭两三个见证人→见证人先下手→除灭恶人→疑难案件送祭司审判→不可偏左偏右。
**王权规范（14-20节）**——将来若要立王→立神所拣选的→不可多马→不可与埃及交通→不可多妃→不可多金银→要为自己抄录律法书→平生诵读→不可心高气傲→遵守律例典章。

## Structure (English)

**Perfect Sacrifice (v. 1)**—Don't offer blemished animals → abomination to LORD.
**Judicial Matters (vv. 2-13)**—If anyone violates covenant, worships other gods → two or three witnesses → witnesses strike first → purge evil → difficult cases to priests → don't deviate.
**Law of the King (vv. 14-20)**—When wanting a king → LORD's chosen → no many horses → no Egyptian trade → no many wives → no much gold → copy law personally → read all life → not heart-proud → keep statutes.

## 神学要点（中文）

**王权在神话语之下——政治神学**
改革宗传统教导世俗权力必须在神的话语下运行。韦敏斯德信条第23章论民事长官。申命记17章的王权法是圣经宪政主义的基础——没有人——即使是王——可以凌驾于神的话语之上。宗教改革时期的改革宗政治思想家（加尔文、诺克斯等）引用这个原则来限制王权。

**两三个见证人——圣经的司法原则**
改革宗传统教导程序公义的重要性。韦敏斯德信条第23章。这个原则从申命记延伸到马太福音18:16（教会纪律）和哥林多后书13:1（使徒权柄），显示神的公义原则跨越旧约新约。

**"为自己抄录律法书"——话语的权威性**
改革宗传统教导圣经对每个人——包括领袖——的权威。韦敏斯德信条第1章。领袖越有权力——越需要被神的话语约束。权力与谦卑是成比例的——越高位越需要在神面前俯伏。

## Theology (English)

**Kingship Under God's Word—Political Theology**
The Reformed tradition teaches secular power must operate under God's word. Westminster Confession chapter 23. Deuteronomy 17's kingship law is Scripture's constitutional foundation—no one—even a king—is above God's word. Reformed political thinkers (Calvin, Knox) cited this to limit royal power.

**Two or Three Witnesses—Scripture's Judicial Principle**
The Reformed tradition teaches procedural justice's importance. Westminster Confession chapter 23. This principle extends from Deuteronomy to Matthew 18:16 and 2 Corinthians 13:1—God's justice principles span both testaments.

**"Copy the Law Personally"—Scripture's Authority**
The Reformed tradition teaches Scripture's authority over everyone—including leaders. Westminster Confession chapter 1. The more power, the more need to be constrained by God's word.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**王必须亲自抄录律法——基督完全成全律法**——马太福音5:17"莫想我来要废掉律法；我来不是要废掉，乃是要成全。"以色列的王要抄写律法——基督是神的道成肉身——律法的完全实现者。他不只是抄写律法——祂自己就是律法的本体。

**"不可多妃"——基督只有一个新妇**——以弗所书5:25-27"你们作丈夫的要爱你们的妻子，正如基督爱教会，为教会舍己……为要献给自己，作个荣耀的教会。"申命记禁止王多妃——预表基督与教会专一的爱。基督只有一个新妇——教会——祂为她舍命、圣化她、使她荣耀。

**完全的祭物——基督是无瑕疵的羔羊**——希伯来书9:14"何况基督藉着永远的灵，将自己无瑕无疵献给神，他的血岂不更能洗净你们的心，除去你们的死行，使你们侍奉那永生神吗？"旧约要求完全无瑕的祭物——基督是那终极的"无瑕无疵"的献祭——神所接受的完全之祭。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**King Must Copy the Law—Christ Perfectly Fulfilled the Law**—Matthew 5:17: "I have not come to abolish them but to fulfill them." Israel's kings copied the law—Christ is the Word incarnate—the law's perfect fulfillment. He didn't just copy the law—He is the law's substance.

**"No Many Wives"—Christ Has One Bride**—Ephesians 5:25-27: "Husbands, love your wives, as Christ loved the church and gave himself up for her." Deuteronomy forbids kings many wives—foreshadowing Christ's exclusive love for the church. Christ has one bride—the church—He died for her, sanctifies her, glorifies her.

**Complete Sacrifice—Christ Is the Spotless Lamb**—Hebrews 9:14: "How much more will the blood of Christ, who through the eternal Spirit offered himself without blemish to God, purify our conscience." OT required perfect unblemished sacrifices—Christ is the ultimate "without blemish" offering.
