## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第三十三章是我在地上说的最后一段话——我临死前为十二个支派所作的祝福。弟兄姐妹，这让我想起了雅各——我的先祖——他在临终前也为十二个儿子祝福（创49章）。弟兄姐妹，伟大的领袖临别时不留遗嘱——而留祝福。

**开篇：神在西奈显现（第1-5节）**

> "耶和华从西奈而来，从西珥向他们显现，从巴兰山发出光辉，从万万圣者中来临，从他右手为百姓传出烈火的律法。"

弟兄姐妹，"从万万圣者中来临"——弟兄姐妹，在西奈山神的显现是何等壮观。弟兄姐妹，万万天使——烈火——圣者云集——为了给一个民族颁布律法。弟兄姐妹，神如此看重这个民族——以至于动用了天上一切的力量在西奈相遇。弟兄姐妹，这个神就是你的神——同样以无限的荣耀和爱看顾你。

**各支派的祝福（第6-25节）**

弟兄姐妹，我为十二个支派一一祝福——每个支派都有它独特的呼召和恩赐：

流便：虽然是长子，却因他的罪（创35:22）只得到"愿他存活——人数不至稀少"的简短祝福。弟兄姐妹，罪的阴影长久跟随一个人——但即使是流便也得到了祝福。神的恩典没有放弃任何一个支派。

犹大：弟兄姐妹，犹大的祝福是"愿你求助于耶和华——耶和华必使你回到你的民那里"——弟兄姐妹，这是出发和回归的祝福——犹大将来要成为以色列的领袖支派——从这个支派要出来大卫——出来弥赛亚。

利未：弟兄姐妹，"他对父母说我未曾看见他；他也不承认弟兄，也不认识自己的儿女——这等人遵行了你的话，谨守了你的约。"弟兄姐妹，利未人在金牛犊事件（出32章）中站在神这边——拿刀砍杀悖逆的弟兄——这是何等艰难的忠诚。弟兄姐妹，神就把祭坛交给了他们——"他们要将香烟献在你的面前，将全然的燔祭献在你的坛上。"弟兄姐妹，对神绝对的忠诚使他们得着了事奉神的特权。

约瑟（以法莲和玛拿西）：弟兄姐妹，约瑟得到了最长最丰富的祝福——天上的恩惠、深藏的泉水、太阳月亮的美物、大山的宝产——"愿这些福，落在约瑟的头上——落在那与弟兄迥别之人的顶上。"弟兄姐妹，"与弟兄迥别之人"——约瑟经历了最多的苦难和背叛——但他也得到了最多的祝福。弟兄姐妹，神的恩典与苦难成正比。

以萨迦：将来居住在山中，以商业著称，"因为他们靠海吸取丰富的财物，靠沙取藏隐的珍宝。"

西布伦：弟兄姐妹，西布伦和以萨迦合在一起——这是两个商业性支派——靠近地中海——将来要在贸易中繁荣。但更重要的是第19节："他们要召百姓上山，在那里献公义的祭。"弟兄姐妹，商业的成功要用来奉献给神——财富的目的是事奉。

迦得：弟兄姐妹，迦得"如母狮蹲伏"——是勇猛的战士。第21节有一句神秘的话："他选择头一段为自己的分……行耶和华所断定的公义，行以色列所定的典章。"弟兄姐妹，迦得选择了约旦河东岸——在其他支派进迦南之前他们先完成了国家的义务。弟兄姐妹，这是先尽义务、后享权利的模范。

**结语：神是以色列的福分（第26-29节）**

> "以色列啊，没有能与神相比的，他乘坐诸天——乘坐穹苍——来帮助你。永世的神是你的居所；他永久的膀臂在你以下。"

弟兄姐妹，"永世的神是你的居所"——弟兄姐妹，神不只是给你一个住所——神自己就是你的住所。弟兄姐妹，"他永久的膀臂在你以下"——不是你靠自己的力量站立——而是神的膀臂托住你。弟兄姐妹，你永远不会跌落在神的膀臂之外——因为祂的膀臂是"永久的"。

弟兄姐妹，我摩西要死了——但我为以色列说的最后一句话是："以色列啊，你是有福的！谁像你这蒙耶和华所拯救的百姓呢？"弟兄姐妹，在我一生所有的话语中——这是最真实的一句——被神拣选、被神拯救——这是人所能得到的最大的福。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 33 is my last words on earth—blessings for the twelve tribes before death. This recalls Jacob—my ancestor—who also blessed his twelve sons before dying (Gen 49). Great leaders' final words aren't wills—but blessings.

**Opening: God Appeared at Sinai (vv. 1-5)**

"The LORD came from Sinai and dawned from Seir upon us...he came from the ten thousands of holy ones, with flaming fire at his right hand." Ten thousands of angels—fire—holy ones assembled—to give one people the law. God valued this people so highly—He deployed heaven's full glory to meet them at Sinai.

**Each Tribe's Blessing (vv. 6-25)**

Reuben—brief blessing despite being firstborn (his sin casts a long shadow). Even Reuben received blessing—grace didn't abandon any tribe.

Judah—"Bring him to his people"—the departure and return blessing. Judah becomes Israel's leader tribe—David comes from here—and the Messiah.

Levi—"He said of his father...I do not know him...for they observed your word"—Levites stood with God during the golden calf incident (Ex 32). God entrusted them with the altar—absolute loyalty earned the privilege of serving God.

Joseph (Ephraim and Manasseh)—received the longest, richest blessing. "May these rest on the head of Joseph...the crown of the head of him who is prince among his brothers." Joseph suffered most—but received most blessing. God's grace is proportional to suffering.

**Conclusion: God Is Israel's Blessing (vv. 26-29)**

> "There is none like God, O Jeshurun, who rides through the heavens to your help...The eternal God is your dwelling place, and underneath are the everlasting arms." Not God giving you a home—God IS your home. "Underneath are the everlasting arms"—not standing on your own strength—God's arms upholding you. You can never fall outside God's arms—for they are "everlasting."

"Happy are you, O Israel! Who is like you, a people saved by the LORD?" My last true words—chosen and saved by God—the greatest blessing humanity can receive.

## 历史背景（中文）

摩西之福（申命记33章）与雅各之福（创世记49章）的对比是旧约研究的重要课题。两段都是临终祝福——但主题有所不同：雅各的祝福更多揭示各支派的特性和命运（如"犹大是只幼狮"），摩西的祝福更多是为各支派的繁荣和保护祈祷。"西缅"在摩西的祝福中缺席——可能是因为西缅人在什亭事件（民25章）中的罪行，或者西缅后来被并入犹大支派。"永世的神是你的居所"（第27节）的希伯来文原文有学术争议——部分版本翻译为"他用古时的膀臂驱逐你面前的敌人"。

## Historical Context (English)

Moses' Blessing (Deut 33) vs. Jacob's Blessing (Gen 49) is an important OT research topic. Both are deathbed blessings—but thematically different: Jacob's reveals each tribe's character and destiny; Moses' prays for each tribe's prosperity and protection. "Simeon" is absent from Moses' blessing—possibly due to Simeon's sin at Shittim (Num 25) or later absorption into Judah. "The eternal God is your dwelling place" (v. 27) has scholarly textual debate—some versions translate as "He drives out the enemy before you."

## 神学要点（中文）

**临终的祝福——领袖对群体的最终奉献**
改革宗传统教导牧师的呼召是持续到生命最后的。韦敏斯德信条第30章。摩西在死前不是安排个人事务——而是为每一个支派祈祷祝福。弟兄姐妹，这是什么样的心——临死前不想自己而想他人。弟兄姐妹，这是基督的心——"他鞠躬尽瘁，舍命服事"（可10:45）。

**"永久的膀臂"——神的绝对托持**
改革宗传统教导信徒的保守是神的工作。韦敏斯德信条第17章论圣徒蒙保守。"永久的膀臂在你以下"——弟兄姐妹，你不是靠自己的能力站立的——神的膀臂托着你。诗篇37:24"他虽失脚也不至全倒，因为耶和华用手搀扶他。"圣徒不是不会跌倒——而是跌倒也跌不出神的手。

**各支派的多样性——教会的多元合一**
改革宗传统教导教会是由不同恩赐的人组成的合一整体。韦敏斯德信条第25章。十二支派各有不同的特质和呼召——但都属于以色列。弟兄姐妹，教会不是模子印出来的——每个人都有独特的恩赐——但都在同一个约里。

## Theology (English)

**Deathbed Blessings—Leader's Final Gift to the Community**
The Reformed tradition teaches a pastor's calling extends to life's end. Westminster Confession chapter 30. Moses before death doesn't arrange personal affairs—but prays blessings for each tribe. What kind of heart—dying yet thinking of others rather than self. This is Christ's heart—"For even the Son of man came not to be ministered unto, but to minister, and to give his life a ransom for many." (Mark 10:45).

**"Everlasting Arms"—God's Absolute Upholding**
The Reformed tradition teaches the saints' preservation is God's work. Westminster Confession chapter 17.: Everlasting arms beneath—you don't stand by your own strength—God's arms uphold you. Psalm 37:24. Saints don't never stumble—but stumbling can't fall outside God's hands.

**Tribes' Diversity—Church's Unity in Diversity**
The Reformed tradition teaches the church is a unified whole of diverse gifts. Westminster Confession chapter 25. Twelve tribes with different qualities—but all part of Israel. The church isn't cookie-cutter—each person has unique gifts—but all within the same covenant.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"犹大啊，求告耶和华"——基督出自犹大**——希伯来书7:14"我们的主分明是从犹大出来的。"摩西祝福犹大"求告耶和华"——基督就是从犹大支派出来的那位——祂的祷告是"父啊，如果可以，让这杯离我而去——然而不要照我的意思，只要照你的意思"——是绝对顺服的"求告"。

**"利未人的忠诚"——基督是大祭司**——希伯来书7:26"像这样圣洁、无邪恶、无玷污、远离罪人、高过诸天的大祭司，正合我们的需要。"利未人因绝对忠诚而得祭司职分——基督是更伟大的大祭司——以祂完全的顺服和生命换取了我们的祭司资格。

**"永久的膀臂"——基督是我们永恒的根基**——哥林多前书3:11"因为那已经立好的根基就是耶稣基督，此外没有人能立别的根基。"神"永久的膀臂"在以色列以下——基督是教会不可动摇的根基——那在我们以下的"永久的膀臂"在基督里成为了"磐石般的根基"。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Judah, Hear the LORD's Voice"—Christ from Judah**—Hebrews 7:14: "It is evident that our Lord was descended from Judah." Moses blessed Judah "to hear the LORD"—Christ came from Judah's tribe—His prayer: "Father, if possible, let this cup pass—yet not my will, but yours"—the absolute submission of "hearing."

**"Levites' Loyalty"—Christ the High Priest**—Hebrews 7:26: "For it was indeed fitting that we should have such a high priest, holy, innocent, unstained, separated from sinners, exalted above the heavens." Levites gained priesthood through absolute loyalty—Christ is the greater High Priest—His perfect obedience earned our priestly standing.

**"Everlasting Arms"—Christ Is Our Eternal Foundation**—1 Corinthians 3:11: "For no one can lay a foundation other than that which is laid, which is Jesus Christ." God's "everlasting arms" beneath Israel—Christ is the Church's unshakeable foundation—that which is "beneath us" becomes in Christ a "rock-solid foundation."
