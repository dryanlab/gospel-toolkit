## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第二十七章是一个庄严的预备仪式——以色列过约旦河以后要做的第一件事：把律法刻在石头上，在基利心山和以巴路山宣告祝福和咒诅。这一章标志着律法讲论（12-26章）的结束和约的正式确认。

**把律法写在石头上（第1-8节）**

> "你们过约旦河到了耶和华你神所赐给你的地，当天要立起几块大石头，墁上石灰，把这律法的一切话写在石头上。"

弟兄姐妹，"当天"——不是到了以后"找时间"——而是过河的当天就要做。弟兄姐妹，为什么这么急？因为进入应许之地的第一件事不是征服、不是分地、不是建房子——而是确认你与神的约。弟兄姐妹，在你开始"享受"恩典之前——先确认你对恩典之主的忠诚。

弟兄姐妹，"墁上石灰把律法写在石头上"——弟兄姐妹，为什么用石头而不是羊皮卷？因为石头是永久的——羊皮卷会腐烂——但刻在石头上的律法要长久存留。弟兄姐妹，神的话语不是一时的指导——而是永恒的标准。弟兄姐妹，同时石头立在大路旁边——所有人都能看到——律法不是祭司的专利——而是全民的公开文件。

弟兄姐妹，"要用没有凿过的石头筑耶和华你神的坛"——弟兄姐妹，"没有凿过的"——不加人工修饰。弟兄姐妹，为什么？出埃及记20:25说"你若在上头动铁器就把坛污秽了"。弟兄姐妹，祭坛代表神的圣洁——不需要人的"改进"。弟兄姐妹，人总想在神的事上加上自己的东西——但神说"保持原样"。

**两座山的宣告（第11-13节）**

> "当日摩西嘱咐百姓说：你们过了约旦河——西缅、利未、犹大、以萨迦、约瑟、便雅悯六个支派的人都要站在基利心山上为百姓祝福。流便、迦得、亚设、西布伦、但、拿弗他利六个支派的人都要站在以巴路山上宣布咒诅。"

弟兄姐妹，两座山——一座祝福——一座咒诅——中间是示剑谷。弟兄姐妹，以色列全民被分成两半——六个支派这边——六个支派那边——面对面站着。弟兄姐妹，利未人在中间高声宣读。弟兄姐妹，这是一个巨大的露天剧场——声音在山谷中回荡——几百万人同时参与这个庄严的仪式。

弟兄姐妹，注意站在祝福山上的支派——犹大（大卫和弥赛亚的支派）、约瑟（以法莲和玛拿西）、便雅悯（后来圣殿所在的支派）——都是以色列历史中最重要的支派。弟兄姐妹，神的安排不是随意的。

**十二个咒诅（第15-26节）**

弟兄姐妹，利未人宣告了十二个"受咒诅"的条款——每一条百姓都要回应"阿们"。弟兄姐妹，"阿们"的意思是"实实在在"——就是"我同意——我接受——如果我违反——愿咒诅临到我"。弟兄姐妹，这不是被动的听讲——而是主动的参与和承诺。

弟兄姐妹，这十二个咒诅涵盖了：拜偶像（暗中设立偶像）、不孝（轻慢父母）、欺骗邻居（挪移地界）、欺负弱者（使瞎子走差路）、屈枉寄居的孤儿寡妇的正直、各种性犯罪（乱伦）、暗杀、受贿、以及总结性的"不坚守遵行这律法一切话的"。

弟兄姐妹，最后一条是最可怕的——"不坚守遵行这律法一切话的必受咒诅"。弟兄姐妹，保罗在加拉太书3:10引用了这句话："凡以行律法为本的都是被咒诅的，因为经上记着'凡不常照律法书上所记一切之事去行的就被咒诅。'"弟兄姐妹，没有人能完全遵守律法——所以每个人都在这个咒诅之下——除非有人代替我们承受这个咒诅。弟兄姐妹，这正是基督所做的。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 27 is a solemn preparatory ceremony—the first thing Israel must do after crossing the Jordan: inscribe the law on stones and pronounce blessings and curses on Mount Gerizim and Mount Ebal. This chapter marks the conclusion of the law speeches (chs. 12-26) and the formal covenant confirmation.

**Writing the Law on Stones (vv. 1-8)**

"On the day you cross over the Jordan...you shall set up large stones and plaster them with plaster. And you shall write on them all the words of this law." "On the day"—not "when you get around to it"—but the very day of crossing. The first act in the promised land isn't conquest or settlement—but confirming your covenant with God.

Why stones instead of scrolls? Stones are permanent—scrolls decay. God's word isn't temporary guidance—but eternal standard. The stones stood by the road—visible to all—the law isn't priests' monopoly—but a public document for all people.

> "You shall build an altar of uncut stones"—no human modification. Exodus 20:25: "If you wield your tool on it you profane it." The altar represents God's holiness—needing no human "improvement."

**Two Mountains (vv. 11-13)**

Two mountains—one blessing—one curse—Shechem valley between them. All Israel divided in half—six tribes each side—face to face. A massive open-air amphitheater—sound echoing in the valley—millions participating in this solemn ceremony.

**Twelve Curses (vv. 15-26)**

Twelve curses—each answered by the people: "Amen." "Amen" means "truly"—"I agree—I accept—if I violate, let the curse fall on me." Active participation and commitment—not passive listening.

The final curse is most terrifying—"Cursed be anyone who does not confirm the words of this law by doing them." Paul quoted this in Galatians 3:10. No one can perfectly keep the law—so everyone is under this curse—unless someone bears it for us. This is exactly what Christ did.

## 历史背景（中文）

示剑（Shechem）是以色列历史中极其重要的地点——亚伯拉罕第一次到迦南时在这里筑坛（创12:6-7），雅各从巴旦亚兰回来时在这里买地（创33:18-19），约瑟的骸骨最终埋葬在这里（书24:32）。基利心山和以巴路山之间的谷地有天然的声学效果——声音可以在山谷中清晰传播，使大规模的公开仪式成为可能。约书亚记8:30-35记载了约书亚确实执行了这个仪式——"约书亚在以巴路山上为耶和华以色列的神筑一座坛……将摩西所写的律法抄写在石头上。"十二个咒诅的数目对应十二支派——但内容与十诫有重叠又不完全相同。

## Historical Context (English)

Shechem is crucial in Israel's history—Abraham first built an altar here (Gen 12:6-7), Jacob bought land here (Gen 33:18-19), Joseph's bones were buried here (Josh 24:32). The valley between Gerizim and Ebal has natural acoustic properties enabling large-scale public ceremonies. Joshua 8:30-35 records Joshua actually performing this ceremony. The twelve curses correspond to twelve tribes but overlap with yet differ from the Ten Commandments.

## 经文结构（中文）

**石头上的律法（1-8节）**——过河当天立大石头→墁上石灰写律法→在以巴路山筑坛→用没有凿过的石头→献燔祭和平安祭→在神面前欢乐→明明地写在石头上。
**以色列今日成为神的百姓（9-10节）**——要默默静听→今日成为耶和华的百姓→要听从遵行。
**两山的分配（11-13节）**——基利心山：西缅利未犹大以萨迦约瑟便雅悯（祝福）→以巴路山：流便迦得亚设西布伦但拿弗他利（咒诅）。
**十二个咒诅（14-26节）**——暗中设偶像→轻慢父母→挪移地界→使瞎子走差路→屈枉寄居的孤儿寡妇→与继母同寝→与兽淫合→与异母姐妹同寝→与岳母同寝→暗中杀人→受贿杀人→不坚守律法→百姓回应阿们。

## Structure (English)

**Law on Stones (vv. 1-8)**—Set up stones on crossing day → plaster and write law → build altar on Ebal → uncut stones → burnt/peace offerings → rejoice before God.
**Israel Becomes God's People (vv. 9-10)**—Keep silence and hear → today you have become the LORD's people → obey and do.
**Mountain Assignment (vv. 11-13)**—Gerizim: Simeon/Levi/Judah/Issachar/Joseph/Benjamin (blessing) → Ebal: Reuben/Gad/Asher/Zebulun/Dan/Naphtali (curse).
**Twelve Curses (vv. 14-26)**—Idol worship → dishonoring parents → moving boundary → misleading blind → perverting justice → various sexual sins → secret murder → bribery → not upholding law → people answer Amen.

## 神学要点（中文）

**律法刻在石头上——神话语的永恒性**
改革宗传统教导圣经是神永恒不变的话语。韦敏斯德信条第1章。律法写在石头上而非羊皮卷——象征神话语的永恒性和不可更改性。太24:35"天地要废去，我的话却不能废去。"弟兄姐妹，时代变迁但真理不变。

**咒诅与"阿们"——约的自觉承担**
改革宗传统教导约的关系需要自觉的回应。韦敏斯德信条第7章论神的约。"阿们"不是形式——而是以色列人自愿承担约的义务和后果。约不是强加的——而是双方同意的。弟兄姐妹，信仰需要你的"阿们"——你的主动回应。

**"不坚守律法一切话的必受咒诅"——律法的绝对标准**
改革宗传统教导律法要求完全的顺服。韦敏斯德信条第19章。雅各书2:10"因为凡遵守全律法的只在一条上跌倒他就是犯了众条。"律法的标准是全部——不是部分。这让每个人都绝望——但这正是恩典的入口。

## Theology (English)

**Law on Stones—God's Word's Eternality**
The Reformed tradition teaches Scripture is God's eternal, unchanging word. Westminster Confession chapter 1. Law on stone, not parchment—symbolizing God's word's permanence. Matt 24:35: "Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will not."

**Curses and "Amen"—Voluntary Covenant Commitment**
The Reformed tradition teaches covenant requires conscious response. Westminster Confession chapter 7. "Amen" isn't formality—but Israel voluntarily assuming covenant obligations and consequences.

**"Cursed Be Anyone Who Does Not Confirm"—Law's Absolute Standard**
The Reformed tradition teaches law demands total obedience. Westminster Confession chapter 19. James 2:10. Law's standard is all—not partial. This makes everyone despair—but despair is grace's entrance.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"凡不常照律法书上所记一切之事去行的就被咒诅"——基督承受了咒诅**——加拉太书3:13"基督既为我们受了咒诅，就赎出我们脱离律法的咒诅，因为经上记着'凡挂在木头上都是被咒诅的。'"弟兄姐妹，以色列人在以巴路山上回应"阿们"的每一个咒诅——基督在十字架上全部承受了。祂没有犯任何一条——但祂承受了违反每一条的咒诅。弟兄姐妹，十字架就是那座"以巴路山"——咒诅山——基督站在那里——为我们承受了一切。

**律法写在石头上——基督把律法写在心版上**——耶利米书31:33"我要将我的律法放在他们里面，写在他们心上。"摩西把律法写在外面的石头上——基督把律法写在里面的心上。外面的石头可以被打碎——但写在心上的律法成为你生命的一部分。

**"没有凿过的石头"——基督是未经人手凿出的石头**——但以理书2:34"你观看，见有一块非人手凿出来的石头打在这像半铁半泥的脚上。"筑坛用没有凿过的石头——基督就是那块"非人手凿出来的石头"——不是人工制造的救主——而是神亲自差来的。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Cursed Be Anyone Who Does Not Confirm"—Christ Bore the Curse**—Galatians 3:13: "Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law by becoming a curse for us—for it is written, 'Cursed is everyone who is hanged on a tree.'" Every curse Israel answered "Amen" to on Ebal—Christ bore on the cross. He violated none—but bore the curse for violating all. The cross is that "Mount Ebal"—the curse mountain—Christ stood there for us.

**Law on Stones—Christ Writes Law on Hearts**—Jeremiah 31:33: "I will put my law within them, and I will write it on their hearts." Moses wrote law on external stones—Christ writes it on internal hearts. External stones can be shattered—but law written on hearts becomes part of your life.

**"Uncut Stones"—Christ the Uncut Stone**—Daniel 2:34: "A stone was cut out by no human hand." The altar uses uncut stones—Christ is that "stone cut out by no human hand"—not a man-made savior—but God's own.
