## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第二十二章是一章涵盖日常生活细节的律法——从归还迷路的牲畜到婚姻的圣洁。这些法规看似零散，但都有一个中心主题：在每一件日常小事中维护爱人如己和圣洁分别的原则。

**归还迷路的财物（第1-4节）**

> "你的弟兄的牛或羊若走迷了路，你看见了，不可转脸不顾，总要带回给你的弟兄。你弟兄若离你甚远，或是你不认识他，就要牵到你家里，留在你那里，等你弟兄来寻找再给他。"

弟兄姐妹，"不可转脸不顾"——弟兄姐妹，发现了迷路的牛羊而装作没看见——这在神的律法中是罪。弟兄姐妹，这个原则的背后是"爱人如己"——你的牛丢了你希望别人怎么对你——你就怎么对你的弟兄。弟兄姐妹，"即使是你仇敌的"——出埃及记23:4说即使是仇敌的驴走迷了也要领回——爱包括了爱仇敌。弟兄姐妹，律法不允许你因为"不认识他"或"离得很远"就不管——麻烦的慷慨。

**不可混淆（第5-12节）**

> "女人不可穿戴男人所穿戴的，男人也不可穿女人的衣服。因为这样行都是耶和华你神所憎恶的。"

弟兄姐妹，这个命令历来被解读为维护性别区分的命令——神创造了男女的区别，不可混淆。弟兄姐妹，这节经文在现代文化中争议很大——但改革宗传统坚持：神设立的男女性别区分是有意义的、是美好的——不是任意的文化建构。

> "你若路上遇见鸟巢，或在树上，或在地上，里头有雏或有蛋，母鸟伏在雏上或在蛋上，你不可连母鸟一同取走，总要放走母鸟，只可取雏和蛋——这样你就可以得福，并使你的日子延长。"

弟兄姐妹，这是旧约中最温柔的一条律法。弟兄姐妹，神关心一只鸟！你不可以把母鸟和雏鸟一起带走——要放走母鸟。弟兄姐妹，为什么？因为带走雏鸟你已经破坏了这个家庭——如果连母鸟一起带走——这个物种的繁衍能力就被彻底破坏了。弟兄姐妹，这是对自然界可持续性的关怀——神关心祂所创造的物种的延续。弟兄姐妹，"这样就可以得福并使你的日子延长"——保护创造物的繁殖能力与人类的福祉是连在一起的。

> "你建造房屋，要在房顶上加上女墙，免得有人从房上掉下来，流血的罪就归你家了。"

弟兄姐妹，"女墙"——古代以色列的房屋是平顶的——可以在上面生活、吃饭、睡觉。没有护栏是危险的。弟兄姐妹，"免得流血的罪归你家"——如果你的疏忽导致别人跌倒死亡——那血归你。弟兄姐妹，这是旧约中的"职业安全"法规——建筑者有责任保证建筑的安全——这个责任比建筑的经济价值更重要。

**婚姻的圣洁（第13-30节）**

弟兄姐妹，本章后半部分涉及婚姻的圣洁——包括对贞洁诬告的处理、奸淫的惩罚、强奸的处理等。弟兄姐妹，这些规定在今天读来令人不安——但必须在古代背景下理解。弟兄姐妹，当时的文化中女性几乎没有任何法律保护——而申命记这些规定实际上给了女性在当时来说是革命性的法律保护。

弟兄姐妹，核心神学是清楚的：婚姻是神所设立的圣约——它的圣洁必须被维护。弟兄姐妹，"淫乱"（porneia）在圣经中始终是严重的罪——不是因为神要限制人的快乐——而是因为性的结合在神的设计中是婚约的印证——在婚约之外的性行为是对神设计的扭曲和破坏。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 22 covers daily life details—from returning stray animals to marriage holiness. Seemingly scattered, but with one central theme: maintaining the principle of loving one's neighbor and holy separation in every daily detail.

**Returning Lost Property (vv. 1-4)**

"You shall not see your brother's ox or his sheep going astray and ignore them." "Don't ignore"—seeing stray animals and pretending not to see is sin. The principle behind this: "love your neighbor as yourself"—how you wish others would treat you, treat your brother. Even if you don't know him or he's far away—you can't ignore it. Loving inconveniently.

**Not Mixing What Should Be Separate (vv. 5-12)**

"A woman shall not wear a man's garment, nor shall a man put on a woman's cloak, for whoever does these things is an abomination to the LORD." This command maintains gender distinction—God created male and female differences, not to be confused.

> "If you come across a bird's nest in any tree or on the ground...you shall not take the mother with the young. You shall let the mother go, but the young you may take for yourself."

One of the OT's most tender laws. God cares about one bird! Taking both mother and young—completely destroys the species' reproductive capacity. This is care for creation's sustainability—God cares about continuation of what He created. "So that it may go well with you and you may live long"—protecting creation's reproductive capacity is connected to human wellbeing.

"When you build a new house, you shall make a parapet for your roof, that you may not bring the guilt of blood upon your house." Ancient Israel's flat roofs were used for living. Without railings, dangerous. If your negligence causes someone's death—that blood is yours. OT "workplace safety" law—builders responsible for safety.

**Marriage's Holiness (vv. 13-30)**

The chapter's second half addresses marriage holiness—handling false virginity accusations, adultery punishment, rape. Disturbing to modern readers—must understand in ancient context. Contemporary culture gave women almost no legal protection—Deuteronomy's regulations gave women revolutionary-for-their-time legal protection. The core theology: marriage is God's established covenant—its holiness must be maintained.

## 历史背景（中文）

"女人不可穿男人的衣服"在古代近东背景中可能还有防止欺骗（女人伪装成男人参战或进入禁地）和防止异教崇拜中的异装仪式（迦南宗教中有男人穿女人衣服事奉神明的习俗）的含义。"母鸟的律法"（shiluach ha-ken）在犹太传统中被视为"最轻"的诫命之一——但其报偿却是"长寿"——与"最重"的诫命（孝敬父母）的报偿相同（利19:3，弗6:2-3）——说明"轻重"不是人所能判断的。"房顶女墙"的规定显示了古代以色列社会的建筑特点——平顶房屋是地中海地区的常见建筑形式。

## Historical Context (English)

"Women shall not wear men's garments" in ANE context may also prevent deception (women disguising as men to enter battle or restricted areas) and Canaanite cultic transvestism. The "mother bird law" (shiluach ha-ken) is considered one of the "lightest" commandments in Jewish tradition—yet its reward is "long life"—the same as the "heaviest" commandment (honor parents) (Lev 19:3, Eph 6:2-3)—showing "light vs. heavy" can't be humanly judged. The parapet regulation reflects ancient Israelite building—flat-roofed houses common in the Mediterranean region.

## 经文结构（中文）

**爱邻居的实践（1-4节）**——归还迷路牛羊驴→看见不可转脸不顾→弟兄远可先带回家。
**维护神创造的秩序（5-12节）**——不可异性穿戴→路遇鸟巢放走母鸟→建房要加女墙→不可将不同种子同种→不可以两种牲畜拉犁→不可穿两种原料织的衣服→衣服四角加络腮。
**婚姻的圣洁（13-30节）**——诬告妻无贞洁→若诬告：打他罚款→若妻确实不贞洁：用石头打死→行奸淫男女都要治死→城里强奸：双方治死→旷野强奸：只治死男的→强奸未订婚的：娶她并付父亲罚款。

## Structure (English)

**Loving Neighbor in Practice (vv. 1-4)**—Return stray ox/sheep/donkey → can't ignore → if brother far, keep temporarily.
**Maintaining God's Created Order (vv. 5-12)**—No cross-dressing → release mother bird → house parapets → don't mix seeds → don't plow with mixed animals → no mixed-material clothing → tassels on garment corners.
**Marriage's Holiness (vv. 13-30)**—False accusation of wife's virginity → if false: flog, fine → if true: stone her → adultery: stone both → city rape: stone both → wilderness rape: only punish man → rape of unbetrothed: must marry, pay father.

## 神学要点（中文）

**爱人如己的具体实践**
改革宗传统教导爱人如己是第二大诫命。韦敏斯德大要理问答第141-148问论第八诫。归还迷路的牲畜、给房子加女墙、放走母鸟——这些都是爱人如己在日常生活中的具体表达。改革宗传统强调：道德原则必须落实在生活的具体细节中——不只是抽象的"爱"。

**神创造秩序的维护**
改革宗传统教导神在创造中建立了秩序——包括性别秩序、物种秩序、社会秩序。韦敏斯德信条第4章。混淆神所区分的——无论是性别（异装）、物种（混种）还是社会关系——都是对神创造秩序的破坏。

**婚姻的神圣约性**
改革宗传统教导婚姻是神所设立的约。韦敏斯德信条第24章论婚姻。婚姻中的忠贞不只是道德要求——而是约的要求。违背婚姻之约是破约——在旧约中受到严厉对待——因为婚姻预表了基督与教会的关系（弗5:32）。

## Theology (English)

**Love Your Neighbor in Concrete Practice**
The Reformed tradition teaches loving one's neighbor is the second great commandment. WLC Q141-148 on the eighth commandment. Returning stray animals, adding parapets, releasing mother birds—all concrete expressions of neighbor-love. Reformed tradition emphasizes: moral principles must be implemented in life's specific details.

**Maintaining God's Created Order**
The Reformed tradition teaches God established order in creation—including gender order, species order, social order. Westminster Confession chapter 4. Confusing what God separated—gender, species, social relations—disrupts God's created order.

**Marriage's Sacred Covenantal Nature**
The Reformed tradition teaches marriage is God's established covenant. Westminster Confession chapter 24. Marriage faithfulness isn't just moral requirement—but covenantal requirement. Violating the marriage covenant is breaking covenant—severely treated in the OT—because marriage foreshadows Christ and the church (Eph 5:32).

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"不可转脸不顾"——基督的好撒玛利亚人**——路加福音10:30-37的好撒玛利亚人比喻是申命记22:1-4的最完美诠释——那个被打劫受伤的人躺在路边——祭司和利未人"转脸不顾"——但好撒玛利亚人"动了慈心"去帮助他。基督就是那位"不转脸不顾"的终极好撒玛利亚人——祂看见了我们的境况——没有装作看不见。

**放走母鸟——基督保护教会的未来**——马太福音23:37"耶路撒冷啊，耶路撒冷啊……我多次愿意聚集你的儿女，好像母鸡把小鸡聚集在翅膀底下，只是你们不愿意。"申命记要放走母鸟保护繁殖能力——基督像母鸡保护小鸡——祂不是要毁灭，而是要聚集和保护。

**房顶加女墙——基督是我们的护卫**——诗篇121:5"保护你的是耶和华；耶和华在你右边荫庇你。"建筑者有责任给房子加护栏——基督是我们生命的护卫——祂在我们"房顶"四周加上了护栏——使我们不坠落。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Don't Ignore"—Christ the Good Samaritan**—Luke 10:30-37: The Good Samaritan parable is Deuteronomy 22:1-4's perfect interpretation—the beaten man lying roadside—priest and Levite "passed by on the other side"—but the Samaritan "had compassion." Christ is the ultimate Good Samaritan who doesn't ignore—He saw our condition and didn't pretend not to see.

**Releasing Mother Bird—Christ Protects the Church's Future**—Matthew 23:37: "Jerusalem, Jerusalem...How often would I have gathered your children together as a hen gathers her brood under her wings, but you were not willing!" Deuteronomy releases mother birds to protect reproductive capacity—Christ like a hen protecting chicks—not to destroy but to gather and protect.

**House Parapet—Christ Is Our Guard**—Psalm 121:5: "The LORD is your keeper; the LORD is your shade on your right hand." Builders must provide guardrails—Christ guards our lives—He placed guardrails around our "rooftop"—keeping us from falling.
