## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第三十二章是我一生中写过的最重要的文字——摩西之歌。这不是一首抒情诗——而是一份预言性的法庭文件。神命令我写这首歌作为见证，当以色列人在应许之地享福然后背叛的时候——这首歌要在他们口中，控诉他们、提醒他们、呼唤他们回转。

**歌的开篇——呼天唤地（第1-4节）**

> "诸天哪，侧耳，我要说话；愿地也听我口中的言语。我的训诲要淋漓如雨；我的言语要滴落如露，如细雨降在嫩草上，如甘霖降在蔬菜中。我要宣告耶和华的名；你们要将大德归与我们的神。他是磐石，他的作为完全；他所行的无不公平，是诚实无伪的神，又公义又正直。"

弟兄姐妹，"他是磐石"——弟兄姐妹，这是整首歌的主题。全诗中"磐石"出现了六次——作为神不变信实的象征。弟兄姐妹，在旷野四十年——我们的脚踩着磐石——但那磐石指向一位更大的磐石。"他的作为完全——他所行的无不公平"——弟兄姐妹，神不会犯错——即使祂的道路我们不明白。

**以色列的悖逆（第5-18节）**

> "他们行事邪僻，不是他的儿女，有了污点，乃是弯曲悖谬的一代。愚昧无知的民哪，你们这样报答耶和华吗？他是你父，造就你的，创造你，建立你。"

弟兄姐妹，"愚昧无知的民"——弟兄姐妹，这是指责——但也是父亲的心痛。弟兄姐妹，"他是你父——造就你的——创造你——建立你"——神是创造者和父亲——以色列却"行事邪僻"。弟兄姐妹，这是最深的背叛——儿女背叛父亲。

弟兄姐妹，第13节描述了神给以色列的丰富："又使他乘驾地的高处，得吃田间的土产，又从磐石中吸蜂蜜，从坚石中吸油——"弟兄姐妹，但第15节："耶书仑渐渐肥胖，踢跳了——你肥胖、粗壮、油腻——他就离弃创造他的神，轻看救他的磐石。"弟兄姐妹，"耶书仑"是以色列的诗意名称——意思是"正直的"——但肥胖之后的"正直者"踢跳离弃神了。弟兄姐妹，丰盛是信仰最大的危险。当你什么都有——你可能忘记你的来源。

**神的审判与最终的怜恤（第19-43节）**

弟兄姐妹，神的审判是可怕的——但第36节出现了转折："耶和华见他的百姓毫无能力，无论困住的自由的都没有了，那时他必为他的百姓伸冤，为他的仆人后悔。"弟兄姐妹，"为他的百姓伸冤"——当以色列被仇敌彻底打败——神要"后悔"——要"伸冤"——要拯救他们。弟兄姐妹，这不是说神会在最后放弃审判——而是说神的目的不是彻底消灭以色列——而是通过审判使他们回转。

弟兄姐妹，第39节是这首歌的神学高峰："你们现在要知道、我、唯有我、是神；在我以外并无别神。我使人死，我使人活；我打伤，我也医治，并无人能从我手中救出来。"弟兄姐妹，"我、唯有我、是神"——绝对的唯一神论——神的主权涵盖生死——涵盖审判和拯救。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 32 is the most important text I ever wrote—the Song of Moses. Not lyric poetry—but a prophetic legal document. God commanded me to write this song as a witness—when Israel enjoys the promised land then betrays—this song in their mouths convicts, reminds, and calls them to return.

**Opening—Heaven and Earth Called as Witnesses (vv. 1-4)**

> "Give ear, O heavens...Hear, O earth...For I will proclaim the name of the LORD; ascribe greatness to our God! The Rock, his work is perfect, for all his ways are justice. A God of faithfulness and without iniquity, just and upright is he."

"The Rock"—this is the whole song's theme. "Rock" appears six times—symbol of God's unchanging faithfulness. "His work is perfect—all his ways are justice"—God makes no mistakes—even when His ways are incomprehensible.

**Israel's Rebellion (vv. 5-18)**

"Jeshurun grew fat, and kicked...then he forsook God who made him and scoffed at the Rock of his salvation." "Jeshurun"—Israel's poetic name meaning "upright"—but the fat "upright one" kicked away from God. Abundance is faith's greatest danger. When you have everything—you may forget your source.

**God's Judgment and Final Mercy (vv. 19-43)**

God's judgment is terrifying—but verse 36 turns: "For the LORD will vindicate his people and have compassion on his servants." When Israel is completely defeated—God vindicates and has compassion. Not abandoning judgment—but using judgment to bring return.

Verse 39: "See now that I, even I, am he, and there is no god beside me; I kill and I make alive; I wound and I heal; and there is none that can deliver out of my hand." Absolute monotheism—God's sovereignty covers life and death—judgment and salvation.

## 历史背景（中文）

摩西之歌（哈阿济努，Ha'azinu，意为"侧耳"）是犹太传统中极为重要的文本。密西拿（Sanhedrin 10:1）提到有些拉比认为否认哈阿济努歌是神的话语的人在来世没有份。这首歌的诗体结构（与散文律法不同）被学者认为是可能是以色列最古老的诗歌之一，与士师记5章底波拉之歌、撒母耳记下22章大卫之歌属于同类型的古代诗歌。"耶书仑"（Jeshurun）这个名称在圣经中只出现三次——申命记32:15、33:5、33:26和以赛亚书44:2——在以赛亚书中神用这个名称称呼以色列表示亲昵和赞许。

## Historical Context (English)

The Song of Moses (Ha'azinu, "give ear") is critically important in Jewish tradition. The Mishnah (Sanhedrin 10:1) says some rabbis believed denying Ha'azinu's divine authorship excluded from the world to come. Its poetic structure is considered one of Israel's most ancient texts—related to Judges 5 (Deborah's Song) and 2 Samuel 22 (David's Song). "Jeshurun" appears only three times in Scripture—Deut 32:15, 33:5, 33:26 and Isaiah 44:2—where God uses it as a term of endearment.

## 经文结构（中文）

**序言（1-4节）**——呼唤天地→我的训诲如雨如露→宣告耶和华名→他是磐石→作为完全→公义正直。
**以色列的背叛（5-18节）**——弯曲悖谬→愚昧无知→耶和华是你父→领你进肥美之地→耶书仑渐渐肥胖→离弃神→轻看磐石→祭祀鬼魔。
**神的审判（19-33节）**——我掩面不顾→借没有国的民激动他们→我要降灾→刀剑饥荒→但不使仇敌夸耀。
**最终的拯救（34-43节）**——这是在我库中收藏的→耶和华为百姓伸冤→我、唯有我是神→使人死使人活→万民当称赞他的百姓→他必报仇雪恨。

## Structure (English)

**Prologue (vv. 1-4)**—Heaven/earth called → teaching like rain → proclaim LORD's name → He is the Rock → perfect/just/upright.
**Israel's Betrayal (vv. 5-18)**—Crooked/perverse → foolish/unwise → LORD is your father → led to rich land → Jeshurun grew fat → forsook God → scorned the Rock → sacrificed to demons.
**God's Judgment (vv. 19-33)**—I will hide my face → provoke with no-people → bring disasters → sword/famine → but not let enemies boast.
**Final Salvation (vv. 34-43)**—Stored in my treasury → LORD vindicates his people → I, only I, am God → kill and make alive → nations praise his people → he avenges.

## 神学要点（中文）

**"他是磐石——作为完全"——神的完全性**
改革宗传统教导神的本质是绝对完全的。韦敏斯德信条第2章。"磐石"象征神的永恒性和不变性——"作为完全"表明神的每一个行动都是完美的。即使祂的审判看起来可怕——也是"无不公平"的。弟兄姐妹，信仰不是在不明白的时候说神不公——而是在不明白的时候说"他是磐石——他的作为完全"。

**"耶书仑肥胖踢跳"——繁荣的属灵危险**
改革宗传统教导物质繁荣可以成为属灵的危险。韦敏斯德大要理问答第105问。申命记28章的祝福成就之后——产生了申命记32章的背叛。这是人类罪性的普遍规律——受苦时寻求神——享乐时忘记神。申6:10-12已经预警了这一点。

**"我、唯有我是神"——唯一神论与神的主权**
改革宗传统教导神是唯一的主权者。韦敏斯德信条第2章。"我、唯有我"——排他性的宣告。弟兄姐妹，这不是排他主义的偏狭——而是现实的宣告：只有一位神——所有的生死、祸福都在祂手中。

## Theology (English)

**"He Is the Rock, His Work Is Perfect"—God's Perfection**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's nature is absolutely perfect. Westminster Confession chapter 2. "Rock" symbolizes God's eternality and immutability. Even His judgment—though terrifying—is "just." Faith isn't calling God unjust when incomprehensible—but saying "He is the Rock, His work is perfect."

**"Jeshurun Grew Fat and Kicked"—Prosperity's Spiritual Danger**
The Reformed tradition teaches material prosperity can become spiritual danger. WLC Q105. The blessings of Deuteronomy 28 produced the betrayal of Deuteronomy 32. Universal pattern of human sinfulness—seeking God in suffering—forgetting God in pleasure.

**"I, Only I, Am God"—Monotheism and Divine Sovereignty**
The Reformed tradition teaches God is the only sovereign. Westminster Confession chapter 2. Not exclusivist prejudice—but reality's declaration: only one God—all life/death, blessing/curse is in His hands.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"他是磐石"——基督是属灵的磐石**——哥林多前书10:4"他们都喝了从随着他们的灵磐石所出的水，那磐石就是基督。"以色列在旷野从磐石喝水——保罗说那磐石就是基督。弟兄姐妹，摩西之歌六次呼唤"磐石"——基督就是那磐石——是我们信仰和救恩的不动根基。

**"我使人死我使人活"——基督是复活**——约翰福音11:25"我就是复活，我就是生命。"申命记说神"使人死、使人活"——基督自己宣告"我就是复活和生命"。神在旧约中的主权在新约中通过基督得到了最高的彰显——祂死而复活——征服了死亡——成为一切人复活的初熟果子。

**"他必报仇雪恨"——基督的再来**——启示录19:2"他的判断是真实公义的；因为他判断了那用淫行败坏世界的大淫妇，并且为自己仆人的血，向她报了仇。"摩西之歌预言神要"报仇雪恨"——这个应验要等到基督的再来——当祂在荣耀中来审判所有人的时候——那时"万民当称赞他的百姓"（第43节）。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"He Is the Rock"—Christ Is the Spiritual Rock**—1 Corinthians 10:4: "They drank from the spiritual Rock that followed them, and the Rock was Christ." Israel drank from the rock in the wilderness—Paul says that rock was Christ. The Song of Moses calls "Rock" six times—Christ is that Rock—our faith's unmovable foundation.

**"I Kill and I Make Alive"—Christ Is the Resurrection**—John 11:25: "I am the resurrection and the life." Deuteronomy says God "kills and makes alive"—Christ declares "I am the resurrection and the life." God's OT sovereignty was supremely manifested in Christ's death and resurrection.

**"He Will Avenge"—Christ's Return**—Revelation 19:2. Moses' Song prophesies God "avenging"—fulfilled at Christ's return—when He comes in glory to judge—then "nations praise his people" (v. 43).
