## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第二十八章是整本申命记最长的一章——六十八节——也是整本圣经中最震撼的一章之一。前十四节是祝福——后面五十四节全是咒诅。弟兄姐妹，咒诅的篇幅是祝福的将近四倍——这不是因为神偏爱审判——而是因为摩西知道以色列人会走向悖逆。

**祝福的全面性（第1-14节）**

> "你若留意听从耶和华你神的话谨守遵行他的一切诫命就是我今日所吩咐你的，他必使你超乎天下万民之上。你若听从耶和华你神的话，这以下的福必追随你临到你身上。"

弟兄姐妹，"福必追随你临到你身上"——不是你去追福——而是福来追你！弟兄姐妹，顺服神的人不需要费力追求祝福——祝福会自己追来。

弟兄姐妹，祝福涵盖了生活的每一个领域——"你在城里必蒙福在田间也必蒙福。你身所生的后裔地所产的五谷牲畜所下的牛犊羊羔都必蒙福。你的筐子和你的抟面盆都必蒙福。你出也蒙福入也蒙福。"弟兄姐妹，从城市到乡村——从生育到农牧——从饮食到出行——全方位的祝福。弟兄姐妹，神的祝福不只是"属灵的"——而是覆盖你整个生活的。

**咒诅的可怕（第15-68节）**

弟兄姐妹，从第15节开始——同样的句式——但内容完全翻转："你若不听从耶和华你神的话不谨守遵行他的一切诫命律例就是我今日所吩咐你的，这以下的咒诅都必追随你临到你身上。"

弟兄姐妹，咒诅不只是祝福的反面——它们层层递进越来越可怕。从经济困难到疾病瘟疫——从军事失败到精神崩溃——最终到了无法想象的恐怖：

> "你在仇敌围困窘迫之中必吃你本身所生的就是耶和华你神所赐给你的儿女之肉。"（第53节）

弟兄姐妹，这不是修辞——这在历史上真正发生了。公元70年罗马将军提多围困耶路撒冷时——约瑟夫斯记载了一个母亲吃自己婴儿的恐怖事件。弟兄姐妹，申命记28章在摩西口中是预言——在历史中是现实。

弟兄姐妹，最后几节更是令人颤抖——"耶和华必使你们分散在万民中从地这边到地那边。你在那些国中也不得安逸，脚掌也无安息之处。耶和华却使你在那里心中跳动眼目失明精神消耗。你的性命将悬悬无定你昼夜恐惧自料性命难保。"（第64-66节）

弟兄姐妹，"分散在万民中"——弟兄姐妹，犹太人从公元70年圣殿被毁后被分散到全世界——将近两千年没有自己的国家——直到1948年以色列复国。弟兄姐妹，申命记28章的每一个咒诅都在历史中精确应验了——这本身就是圣经是神的话语的有力证据。

弟兄姐妹，但这一章不只是历史预言——它是给你我的警告：离开神的道路——后果是什么。弟兄姐妹，不是神想降灾——而是离开神的保护就是进入咒诅的领域。弟兄姐妹，祝福的条件只有一个："留意听从"。咒诅的条件也只有一个："不听从"。弟兄姐妹，一切取决于你对神话语的态度。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 28 is the longest chapter in Deuteronomy—sixty-eight verses—and one of the most staggering in all of Scripture. The first fourteen verses are blessings—the remaining fifty-four are curses. Curses outnumber blessings nearly four to one—not because God favors judgment—but because Moses knew Israel would turn to rebellion.

**The Comprehensiveness of Blessings (vv. 1-14)**

"If you faithfully obey the LORD your God...all these blessings shall come upon you and overtake you." Not you chasing blessings—but blessings chasing you! Blessings cover every life domain—city and field, offspring and livestock, basket and kneading bowl, going out and coming in. God's blessing isn't just "spiritual"—it covers your entire life.

**The Horror of Curses (vv. 15-68)**

From verse 15—same pattern but completely reversed. Curses escalate progressively—from economic hardship to disease—from military defeat to mental collapse—ultimately reaching unimaginable horror:

"You shall eat the fruit of your womb, the flesh of your sons and daughters" (v. 53). This isn't rhetoric—it actually happened. During Titus's siege of Jerusalem in AD 70, Josephus recorded a mother eating her own infant. Deuteronomy 28 was prophecy in Moses' mouth—reality in history.

"The LORD will scatter you among all peoples, from one end of the earth to the other...Among these nations you shall find no respite" (vv. 64-65). Jews were scattered worldwide from AD 70—nearly two thousand years without a homeland—until Israel's 1948 restoration. Every curse in Deuteronomy 28 was precisely fulfilled in history—powerful evidence that the Bible is God's word.

But this chapter isn't just historical prophecy—it's a warning for us: leaving God's path—what are the consequences? Blessing's condition: "faithfully obey." Curse's condition: "do not obey." Everything depends on your attitude toward God's word.

## 历史背景（中文）

申命记28章的咒诅在以色列历史中有惊人的精确应验。公元前586年巴比伦围困耶路撒冷时——耶利米哀歌4:10"慈怜的妇人当我众民被毁灭的时候亲手煮自己的儿女作为食物。"公元70年罗马围困耶路撒冷时——约瑟夫斯（犹太战争6.3.4）记载了类似的恐怖事件。"分散在万民中"的咒诅从公元70年圣殿被毁开始应验——犹太人被分散到罗马帝国各地——中世纪在欧洲各国受到迫害——最终经历了二十世纪的大屠杀（600万人被杀）。祝福部分的"你必借给许多国民却不至向他们借贷"在犹太人历史上也有应验——中世纪欧洲犹太人因为被禁止从事大多数职业而从事金融借贷——成为"放贷人"。

## Historical Context (English)

Deuteronomy 28's curses have astonishingly precise historical fulfillment. During Babylon's 586 BC siege—Lamentations 4:10 records mothers cooking their own children. During Rome's AD 70 siege—Josephus (Jewish Wars 6.3.4) records similar horrors. "Scattered among all peoples" fulfilled from AD 70—Jews dispersed throughout the Roman Empire—persecuted across medieval Europe—culminating in the twentieth-century Holocaust (6 million killed). The blessing "you shall lend to many nations" was fulfilled as medieval Jews, barred from most professions, became moneylenders.

## 经文结构（中文）

**祝福的条件（1-2节）**——留意听从→祝福追随临到。
**六重祝福（3-6节）**——城里蒙福→田间蒙福→后裔蒙福→土产蒙福→筐子抟面盆蒙福→出入蒙福。
**祝福的扩展（7-14节）**——仇敌败退→仓房丰满→称为耶和华名下子民→借给人不借人→作首不作尾→在上不在下。
**咒诅的条件（15节）**——不听从→咒诅追随临到。
**六重咒诅（16-19节）**——与祝福对应的全面翻转。
**咒诅递进（20-46节）**——瘟疫疾病→天如铜地如铁→军事失败→疯狂瞎眼→妻子被占→牛羊被夺→为尾不为首。
**极端咒诅（47-57节）**——因不欢欢喜喜事奉神→仇敌来攻→吃儿女之肉。
**最终咒诅（58-68节）**——埃及的疾病→人数稀少→分散万民→心中跳动→性命悬悬→送回埃及。

## Structure (English)

**Blessing Condition (vv. 1-2)**—Faithfully obey → blessings overtake you.
**Six Blessings (vv. 3-6)**—Blessed in city → field → offspring → produce → basket/bowl → going/coming.
**Expanded Blessings (vv. 7-14)**—Enemies defeated → barns full → called by LORD's name → lend/not borrow → head/not tail → above/not beneath.
**Curse Condition (v. 15)**—Disobey → curses overtake you.
**Six Curses (vv. 16-19)**—Complete reversal of blessings.
**Escalating Curses (vv. 20-46)**—Plague/disease → bronze sky/iron ground → military defeat → madness/blindness → wife taken → livestock seized.
**Extreme Curses (vv. 47-57)**—For not serving God joyfully → enemy siege → eating children's flesh.
**Final Curses (vv. 58-68)**—Egyptian diseases → few in number → scattered → fearful heart → life hanging in doubt → sent back to Egypt.

## 神学要点（中文）

**约的两面——祝福与咒诅**
改革宗传统教导约包含应许和警告两个方面。韦敏斯德信条第7章。祝福与咒诅不是两个独立的东西——而是同一个约的两面。顺服带来祝福——悖逆带来咒诅。弟兄姐妹，神的约不是无条件的溺爱——而是有结构的关系。

**律法的功用——显明罪和绝望**
改革宗传统教导律法的第一功用是显明罪。韦敏斯德信条第19章。28章的咒诅如此漫长而可怕——目的是让人意识到靠自己不可能完全遵守律法——从而寻求恩典。律法不是为了让你成功——而是为了让你绝望——然后转向基督。

**历史的精确应验——神的主权**
改革宗传统教导神掌管历史的每一个细节。韦敏斯德信条第5章。28章的咒诅在历史中精确应验——从巴比伦之囚到罗马毁灭圣殿到中世纪迫害到现代大屠杀——证明了神话语的可靠性和神对历史的绝对主权。

## Theology (English)

**Covenant's Two Sides—Blessings and Curses**
The Reformed tradition teaches covenant includes both promise and warning. Westminster Confession chapter 7. God's covenant isn't unconditional indulgence—but structured relationship.

**Law's Function—Revealing Sin and Despair**
The Reformed tradition teaches law's first use is revealing sin. Westminster Confession chapter 19. Chapter 28's extensive, terrifying curses aim to show the impossibility of perfect law-keeping—driving people to seek grace. Law isn't for success—but for despair—then turning to Christ.

**Precise Historical Fulfillment—God's Sovereignty**
The Reformed tradition teaches God governs every historical detail. Westminster Confession chapter 5. Chapter 28's curses precisely fulfilled across history—proving God's word's reliability and absolute sovereignty over history.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"福必追随你"——在基督里祝福追随我们**——以弗所书1:3"愿颂赞归与我们主耶稣基督的父神！他在基督里曾赐给我们天上各样属灵的福气。"旧约的祝福是地上的（城里田间后裔土产）——新约的祝福是"天上各样属灵的福气"——在基督里——祝福追随每一个信徒。

**咒诅的极致——基督在十字架上经历了一切**——弟兄姐妹，28章的每一个咒诅——疾病、被弃、黑暗、孤独、口渴、赤身——基督在十字架上都经历了。"我的神我的神为什么离弃我"（太27:46）——这是28章64节"耶和华使你分散"的终极应验——基督被父神"分散"——被弃绝——为了使我们永远不被弃绝。

**"送回埃及"——基督带我们出埃及**——28章最后一个咒诅是"送回埃及"——回到奴役之地。弟兄姐妹，罪使我们"回到埃及"——回到捆绑——但基督再次带我们"出埃及"——加拉太书5:1"基督释放了我们叫我们得以自由所以要站立得稳不要再被奴仆的轭挟制。"

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"Blessings Shall Overtake You"—In Christ Blessings Pursue Us**—Ephesians 1:3: "Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed us in Christ with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly places." OT blessings were earthly—NT blessings are "every spiritual blessing in the heavenly places"—in Christ, blessings pursue every believer.

**The Curse's Extreme—Christ Experienced All on the Cross**—Every curse in chapter 28—disease, abandonment, darkness, loneliness, thirst, nakedness—Christ experienced on the cross. "And about the ninth hour Jesus cried with a loud voice, saying, Eli, Eli, lama sabachthani? that is to say, My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me?" (Matt 27:46)—the ultimate fulfillment of "the LORD will scatter you"—Christ was "scattered" from the Father—abandoned—so we would never be abandoned.

**"Sent Back to Egypt"—Christ Brings Us Out**—Chapter 28's final curse is being "sent back to Egypt"—back to slavery. Sin sends us "back to Egypt"—but Christ brings us "out of Egypt" again—Galatians 5:1: "For freedom Christ has set us free; stand firm therefore, and do not submit again to a yoke of slavery."
