## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第十五章讲的是安息年的经济制度——每七年豁免债务、善待穷人、释放奴仆。这一章的核心信息是：你蒙了恩——所以你要施恩。

**每七年豁免债务（第1-6节）**

> "每逢七年末一年你要施行豁免。豁免的定例乃是这样：凡债主要把所借给邻舍的豁免了；不可向邻舍和弟兄追讨，因为耶和华的豁免年已经宣告了。"

弟兄姐妹，"豁免"——希伯来文"shemitah"——放手、释放的意思。弟兄姐妹，每七年所有的债务一笔勾销。弟兄姐妹，从经济学的角度看这不合理——债务人赖账怎么办？弟兄姐妹，但神的经济学不是人的经济学——神的经济学的核心是：你不应该永远被债务压着——七年足够了——该释放了。弟兄姐妹，这个制度防止了永久性的贫困——没有人会因为一次的失败而终身为奴。

**不可硬着心（第7-11节）**

> "在耶和华你神所赐你的地上若有一个穷人——就是你的弟兄——你不可忍着心、揝着手不帮补穷乏的弟兄。总要向他松开手，照他所缺乏的借给他补他的不足。你要谨慎，不可心里起恶念说'第七年的豁免年快到了'，你便恶眼看你穷乏的弟兄不给他什么。"

弟兄姐妹，"忍着心揝着手"——弟兄姐妹，这两个词画了一幅生动的画面——心揪紧了、手握紧了——不想给。弟兄姐妹，你看见穷乏的弟兄——你的第一反应是什么？是松开手还是握紧手？

弟兄姐妹，"不可心里起恶念说第七年快到了"——弟兄姐妹，这是何等精准的心理描述！弟兄姐妹，假设现在是第六年——有人来借钱——你心里想："明年就是豁免年了——他借了不用还——我不借。"弟兄姐妹，这在经济上完全合理——但在神的眼中是"恶念"。弟兄姐妹，神要你做一个在经济上"不合理"的决定——因为怜悯比精明更重要。

"你总要给他，给他的时候心里不可愁烦；因耶和华你的神必因此在你一切所行所办的事上赐福与你。"弟兄姐妹，"心里不可愁烦"——不只是外在地给——还要内心甘愿。弟兄姐妹，勉强地给和甘心地给——在神眼中完全不同。"捐得乐意的人是神所喜爱的"（林后9:7）。

"原来那地上的穷人永不断绝；所以我吩咐你说：总要向你地上困苦穷乏的弟兄松开手。"弟兄姐妹，"穷人永不断绝"——弟兄姐妹，这是现实主义——不是悲观主义。弟兄姐妹，你不能消灭贫穷——但你可以照顾穷人。你的责任不是解决整个世界的贫穷——而是"向你地上穷乏的弟兄松开手"——照顾你身边的人。

**释放奴仆（第12-18节）**

> "你弟兄中若有一个希伯来男人或希伯来女人被卖给你服事你六年到第七年就要任他自由出去。你任他自由的时候不可使他空手而去。要从你羊群禾场酒榨之中多多地给他，耶和华你的神怎样赐福与你你也要照样给他。"

弟兄姐妹，释放奴仆——不可"空手而去"——要"多多地给他"。弟兄姐妹，这不只是"放他走"——而是"装备他重新开始"。弟兄姐妹，真正的自由不只是去掉枷锁——还需要给他重新开始的资源。弟兄姐妹，如果你放一个奴仆走——但不给他任何东西——他明天就会因为贫穷而再次卖身——这不是真正的释放。

"你要记念你在埃及地作过奴仆，耶和华你的神将你救赎。因此我今日吩咐你这件事。"弟兄姐妹，"你在埃及作过奴仆"——弟兄姐妹，你为什么要释放奴仆？因为你自己曾经是奴仆。你为什么要慷慨？因为神曾经慷慨地救赎了你。弟兄姐妹，恩典不是止步于你——恩典要通过你流向别人。

弟兄姐妹，申命记第十五章的经济伦理可以总结为一句话：你蒙了恩所以你要施恩。神豁免了你的罪债——你也要豁免弟兄的钱债。神释放了你脱离罪的奴役——你也要释放被压迫的人。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 15 addresses the sabbatical year's economic system—debt release every seven years, generosity to the poor, freeing servants. The core message: you received grace—so you must give grace.

**Seven-Year Debt Release (vv. 1-6)**

"At the end of every seven years you shall grant a release. Every creditor shall release what he has lent to his neighbor." Every seven years, all debts cancelled. Economically unreasonable—but God's economics isn't human economics. The core principle: no one should be crushed by debt forever—seven years is enough—time to release.

**Don't Harden Your Heart (vv. 7-11)**

"If among you, one of your brothers should become poor...you shall not harden your heart or shut your hand against your poor brother." Two vivid images—heart tightened, hand clenched.

"Take care lest there be an unworthy thought in your heart...saying, 'The seventh year, the year of release is near,' and your eye look grudgingly on your poor brother." What precise psychology! Suppose it's year six—someone asks to borrow—you think: "Next year is release year—he won't repay—I won't lend." Economically rational—but in God's eyes, "an unworthy thought." God asks you to make an economically "irrational" decision—because mercy matters more than shrewdness.

"The poor will never cease out of the land"—realism, not pessimism. You can't eliminate poverty—but you can care for the poor. Your responsibility isn't solving world poverty—but "opening your hand to your brother" nearby.

**Freeing Servants (vv. 12-18)**

"You shall furnish him liberally...as the LORD your God has blessed you, you shall give to him." Not just "let him go"—but "equip him to restart." True freedom isn't just removing chains—it requires resources for a new beginning. If you free a servant empty-handed—he'll sell himself again tomorrow from poverty.

"You shall remember that you were a slave in Egypt, and the LORD your God redeemed you." Why free servants? Because you were once a servant. Why be generous? Because God generously redeemed you. Grace doesn't stop at you—grace flows through you to others.

Deuteronomy 15's economic ethic in one sentence: you received grace, so give grace.

## 历史背景（中文）

安息年（shemitah）制度与利未记25章的禧年制度相关但不同——安息年每七年一次豁免债务，禧年每五十年一次归还土地。关于"豁免"是永久取消还是暂时延期——拉比们有不同的解释——希勒尔（公元前1世纪）发明了"prozbul"制度来绕过安息年的豁免——因为到了他的时代人们在第六年不肯借钱出去。以色列人释放希伯来奴仆的规定与古代近东其他法典（如汉谟拉比法典§117）有相似之处——但以色列律法更加慷慨——不仅释放奴仆，还要"多多地给他"以防止再度陷入贫困。耶利米书34:8-22记载了西底家时代以色列人违反这条律法——先释放后又强迫奴仆回来——引来了神的审判。

## Historical Context (English)

The sabbatical year (shemitah) relates to but differs from Leviticus 25's Jubilee—sabbatical releases debts every seven years, Jubilee returns land every fifty. Whether "release" means permanent cancellation or temporary suspension—rabbis debated. Hillel (1st century BC) invented the "prozbul" to circumvent sabbatical release—because by his time people wouldn't lend in the sixth year. Hebrew servant release rules parallel other ANE codes (Hammurabi §117)—but Israel's law is more generous—not only freeing but "furnishing liberally." Jeremiah 34:8-22 records Zedekiah-era violation—freeing then re-enslaving—bringing God's judgment.

## Structure (English)

**Sabbatical Release (vv. 1-6)**—Every seven years grant release → don't exact from brother → may exact from foreigner → if obey, blessed → lend to nations, won't borrow.
**Caring for Poor (vv. 7-11)**—Don't harden heart or shut hand → open hand, lend → don't think "release year is near" → give freely without grudging → blessed for this → poor never cease.
**Freeing Servants (vv. 12-18)**—Serve six years, free seventh → don't send empty-handed → furnish liberally → you were slave in Egypt → willing service → if unwilling to leave, serve permanently.
**Firstborn Livestock (vv. 19-23)**—Firstborn males holy to LORD → don't work or shear → if blemished, don't offer → don't eat blood.

## 神学要点（中文）

**恩典的经济学——蒙恩者施恩**
改革宗传统教导恩典产生感恩和慷慨。韦敏斯德小要理问答第1问："（一）荣耀神；（二）以祂为乐，直到永远。""你在埃及作过奴仆耶和华将你救赎"——你的慷慨不是基于穷人的配得——而是基于你自己曾经被神慷慨地拯救。恩典的逻辑：因为你白白得到了→你也要白白给出。

**不可忍心揝手——怜悯是命令不是选项**
改革宗传统教导基督徒有照顾穷人的责任。韦敏斯德大要理问答第141问论第八条诫命：要求给人所当得的……按我们的能力救济穷困者的需要。"不可忍心揝手"——帮助穷人不是建议——是命令。

**"穷人永不断绝"——现实主义与责任**
改革宗传统教导罪的存在使贫穷不可完全消除——但这不免除我们照顾穷人的责任。耶稣引用这节经文（太26:11"因为常有穷人和你们同在"）不是说不要关心穷人——而是说照顾穷人是持续的责任——不是一次性的。

## Theology (English)

**Economics of Grace—Recipients Give Grace**
The Reformed tradition teaches grace produces gratitude and generosity. WSC Q1. "You were a slave in Egypt, the LORD redeemed you"—your generosity isn't based on the poor's merit—but on God's generous rescue of you.

**Don't Harden Heart—Mercy Is Command, Not Option**
The Reformed tradition teaches Christians must care for the poor. WLC Q141 on the eighth commandment. "Don't harden your heart"—helping the poor isn't suggestion—it's command.

**"The Poor Will Never Cease"—Realism and Responsibility**
The Reformed tradition teaches sin makes poverty ineradicable—but this doesn't exempt our responsibility. Jesus quoted this verse (Matt 26:11) not to dismiss caring for the poor—but to say caring is an ongoing responsibility.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**安息年豁免——基督豁免我们的罪债**——马太福音6:12"免我们的债，如同我们免了人的债。"安息年每七年豁免经济债务——基督一次性豁免了我们全部的罪债。我们欠神的债是我们永远还不起的——但基督"撤去"了那"有碍于我们的字据"（西2:14）。

**释放奴仆——基督释放我们脱离罪的奴役**——约翰福音8:36"所以天父的儿子若叫你们自由，你们就真自由了。"安息年释放希伯来奴仆——基督释放所有被罪捆绑的人。而且基督不是让我们"空手而去"——祂赐给我们圣灵、赐给我们永生、赐给我们在基督里一切属灵的福气——这是"多多地给"的终极实现。

**"你在埃及作过奴仆"——记念恩典是怜悯的动力**——以弗所书2:11-13"你们应当记念……那时你们与基督无关……如今却在基督耶稣里，靠着他的血，已经得亲近了。"你为什么要怜悯别人？因为"你应当记念"你曾经也在那里——与基督无关、没有指望——但神怜悯了你。这个记忆就是你怜悯别人的动力。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**Sabbatical Release—Christ Cancels Our Sin-Debt**—Matthew 6:12: "Forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors." The sabbatical year cancels economic debts every seven years—Christ cancels all our sin-debt once for all. Colossians 2:14: He "canceled the record of debt that stood against us."

**Freeing Servants—Christ Frees Us from Sin's Slavery**—John 8:36: "So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed." Christ doesn't send us away "empty-handed"—He gives us the Spirit, eternal life, every spiritual blessing in Christ—the ultimate "furnishing liberally."

**"You Were a Slave in Egypt"—Remembering Grace Motivates Mercy**—Ephesians 2:11-13: "Remember that you were at that time separated from Christ...But now in Christ Jesus you...have been brought near by the blood of Christ." Why show mercy? Because "remember"—you were once there too. This memory is your motivation for mercy.
