## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第十九章规定了逃城制度和见证人制度——两者都在保护无辜者。逃城保护误杀者免受血亲复仇；见证人制度防止人被假见证陷害。

**逃城的设立（第1-13节）**

> "耶和华你神将列国之民剪除，把地赐给你的时候，你要在所得的地上为自己分出三座城。你要量准你的地，将耶和华你神使你承受为业之地分为三段，使各处的人都可以逃到其中一座城去。"

弟兄姐妹，逃城——这是旧约中神对人性最温柔的理解之一。弟兄姐妹，在古代以色列——"血亲复仇"是合法的——死者的最近亲属（"血仇人"）有权追杀凶手。弟兄姐妹，但如果是"误杀"呢？比如和邻居一起砍树，斧头飞出去砍死了人——这不是蓄意谋杀。弟兄姐妹，对于这样的人，神设立了逃城——一个可以跑去避难的地方——不受血仇人的追杀。

弟兄姐妹，"误杀人的可以逃到那里存活"——弟兄姐妹，"存活"两个字是关键。逃城的目的是让无辜的人活下去——不是让真正的凶手逃避惩罚（第12节说蓄意杀人的要被交出来处死）。弟兄姐妹，神的公义很精准——祂区分"蓄意"和"误伤"——在审判中有这样的细腻是神恩典的体现。

弟兄姐妹，"你若再扩张耶和华你神所命给你的境界……就要再从其中加添三座城"——弟兄姐妹，随着以色列版图的扩大，逃城也要增加——确保每个人都能在合理的距离内到达一座逃城。弟兄姐妹，神不设立一座逃城——而是确保每个地区都有。神的恩典是普及的——不是只对离圣所近的人。

**地界石（第14节）**

> "在耶和华你神所赐你承受为业之地，不可挪移邻舍的地界，那是先人所定的。"

弟兄姐妹，"不可挪移地界石"——弟兄姐妹，地界石是古代社会确定土地所有权的标志——就像今天的产权证书。弟兄姐妹，挪移地界石——悄悄把邻居的地圈进自己的——这是贪婪和欺骗的行为。弟兄姐妹，箴言22:28"你不可挪移古时所立的地界，那是你列祖所定的"——这个禁令不是细节——而是对财产正义的根本要求。

**见证人的公义（第15-21节）**

> "无论何事，一个见证人不可以单独起来，见证那人的任何罪，或任何过犯，或任何罪恶；总要凭两三个见证人的口才可以定案。"

弟兄姐妹，这是第十七章原则的重申——"两三个见证人才可定案"。弟兄姐妹，这个原则之所以反复强调——是因为虚假的指控是对生命的威胁。弟兄姐妹，"若有凶恶的见证人起来见证某人有悖逆的罪，这两个争论的人就要站在耶和华面前，并当时的祭司文士面前……见证人若是作假见证，以谎言攻击弟兄，你们就要待他如同他想待弟兄一样。"

弟兄姐妹，"以眼还眼以牙还牙以手还手以脚还脚"——弟兄姐妹，"以眼还眼"在现代人耳中是残酷的——但在古代法律背景中——它实际上是对"过分复仇"的限制！弟兄姐妹，古代社会没有限制的话——为了一颗牙可能杀一个人（参创4:23拉麦的歌）。"以牙还牙"说：惩罚必须与罪行相称——不多也不少。弟兄姐妹，这是比例原则的最早表述——是现代法律体系的重要原则。

弟兄姐妹，申命记第十九章教导我们：神的公义是精准的、有程序保护的、有比例原则的。逃城保护无辜者、地界石保护财产、见证人制度保护名誉——神的律法全面地保护了人的尊严。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 19 establishes cities of refuge and witness laws—both protecting the innocent. Cities of refuge protect accidental killers from blood-revenge; witness laws prevent entrapment by false testimony.

**Establishing Cities of Refuge (vv. 1-13)**

> "You shall set apart three cities for yourselves in the land...so that any manslayer can flee there. This is the provision for the manslayer...if anyone kills his neighbor unintentionally without having hated him in the past—he may flee to one of these cities and live."

Cities of refuge—one of the OT's most tender understandings of human nature. In ancient Israel, "blood vengeance" was legal—the nearest relative ("avenger of blood") could pursue a killer. But what about accidental death? Chopping wood with a neighbor, the axe head flies and kills someone—not murder. God established refuge cities—places to flee from the avenger.

> "The manslayer may flee there and live"—the goal is keeping the innocent alive—not letting actual murderers escape (v. 12: deliberate killers must be surrendered). God's justice is precise—distinguishing "intentional" from "accidental."

**Boundary Stones (v. 14)**

"You shall not move your neighbor's landmark, set up by the former generations." Boundary stones confirmed land ownership—like modern title deeds. Moving them—quietly absorbing a neighbor's land—is greed and deception. Not a minor rule—but a fundamental property justice requirement.

**Justice in Witness (vv. 15-21)**

> "A single witness shall not suffice...only on the evidence of two or three witnesses shall a charge be established." Repeated from chapter 17—because false accusations threaten lives. "If a malicious witness arises to accuse a person...the judges shall inquire diligently...then you shall do to him as he had meant to do to his brother."

"Eye for eye, tooth for tooth, hand for hand, foot for foot"—to modern ears this sounds cruel—but in ancient legal context it actually limited excessive revenge! Without limits, someone might kill over a tooth (see Gen 4:23 Lamech's song). "Tooth for tooth" means: punishment must be proportionate—no more, no less. This is the earliest statement of the proportionality principle—an important foundation of modern legal systems.

## 历史背景（中文）

逃城制度在民数记35章和约书亚记20章也有记载——实际设立的逃城有六座：约旦河西三座（基低斯、示剑、基列亚巴/希伯仑）和约旦河东三座（比悉、拉末、哥兰）。"血仇人"（go'el ha-dam，"血的救赎者"）制度是古代以色列的族权复仇传统。申命记19:15的"两三个见证人"原则后来成为犹太法律（哈拉卡）和早期基督教教会纪律（太18:16，林后13:1）的基础。"以眼还眼以牙还牙"（lex talionis，拉丁文"同态复仇法"）也出现在汉谟拉比法典（§196-197）中——显示这是古代近东普遍的法律原则——申命记的版本将其置于约的框架中。

## Historical Context (English)

Cities of refuge are also detailed in Numbers 35 and Joshua 20—six were actually established: three west of Jordan (Kedesh, Shechem, Kiriath-Arba/Hebron) and three east (Bezer, Ramoth, Golan). The "avenger of blood" (go'el ha-dam) system was Israel's clan vengeance tradition. Deuteronomy 19:15's "two or three witnesses" became Jewish law (halakha) and early church discipline (Matt 18:16, 2 Cor 13:1) foundation. "Eye for eye, tooth for tooth" (lex talionis) also appears in Hammurabi's Code (§196-197)—a common ANE legal principle—Deuteronomy places it within covenant framework.

## 经文结构（中文）

**逃城制度（1-13节）**——分三座逃城→道路要修好→误杀人的可逃存活→与邻舍素无仇恨→若仇恨蓄意：不可收留，交与血仇人→地扩大时再加三城→免得流无辜人的血。
**地界石（14节）**——不可挪移邻舍地界→先人所定的。
**见证人制度（15-21节）**——一个见证人不够→两三个才可定案→恶意见证人要被调查→若作假见证：以其人之道还治其身→以眼还眼以牙还牙→以此从你们中间除灭恶人。

## Structure (English)

**Cities of Refuge (vv. 1-13)**—Set aside three cities → keep roads maintained → accidental killer may flee and live → no prior hatred → if hatred/intentional: don't shelter, hand to avenger → expand to six cities as land grows → prevent innocent bloodshed.
**Boundary Stones (v. 14)**—Don't move neighbor's landmark → set by former generations.
**Witness System (vv. 15-21)**—One witness insufficient → two or three to establish charge → malicious witness investigated → if false: do to him what intended → eye for eye, tooth for tooth → purge evil.

## 神学要点（中文）

**逃城——神的庇护与公义并行**
改革宗传统教导神同时是公义的和慈爱的。韦敏斯德信条第2章。逃城制度完美地呈现了神性情的两面——对蓄意杀人者，公义要求处死；对误杀者，慈爱提供庇护。公义与慈爱不是矛盾的——而是在同一个制度中同时表达。

**"以眼还眼"——比例原则与神的公义**
改革宗传统教导惩罚必须与罪行相称。韦敏斯德信条第5章。"以眼还眼"不是野蛮——而是精准。惩罚过轻是对受害者的不公——惩罚过重是对被告的不公。神的公义是准确的、有比例的。

**两三个见证人——程序公义的神圣性**
改革宗传统教导神的公义包括程序公义。韦敏斯德信条第23章。程序公义不是技术性的障碍——而是保护无辜者的重要机制。当程序被绕过时——即使结果"正确"——也是对神公义标准的违背。

## Theology (English)

**Cities of Refuge—God's Shelter and Justice Coexist**
The Reformed tradition teaches God is simultaneously just and loving. Westminster Confession chapter 2. The refuge city system perfectly presents both—for intentional killers: justice demands death; for accidental killers: love provides shelter.

**"Eye for Eye"—Proportionality Principle and God's Justice**
The Reformed tradition teaches punishment must be proportionate to the crime. Westminster Confession chapter 5.: Eye for eye isn't barbaric—it's precise. Under-punishment wrongs the victim—over-punishment wrongs the accused.

**Two or Three Witnesses—Procedural Justice's Sanctity**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's justice includes procedural justice. Westminster Confession chapter 23. Procedural justice isn't a technical obstacle—it's an important protection for the innocent.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**逃城——基督是我们的避难所**——希伯来书6:17-18"神……使我们这逃往避难所、持定摆在我们前头指望的人，可以大得勉励。"旧约的逃城是空间上的避难所——基督是属灵的避难所。弟兄姐妹，我们都是罪的"逃亡者"——在神的公义审判下我们需要一个避难所——基督就是那避难所。不是因为我们无罪——而是因为祂承担了我们的罪。

**"以眼还眼"与基督的登山宝训**——马太福音5:38-39"你们听见有话说'以眼还眼以牙还牙'，只是我告诉你们：不要与恶人作对；有人打你的右脸，连左脸也转过来由他打。"基督没有废除比例原则——而是把它提升到了更高的层次：在基督里，不是"刚好公平"——而是"超越公平的恩典"。

**假见证——基督被假见证所杀**——马太福音26:59-60"祭司长和全公会寻找假见证控告耶稣，要治死他……虽有许多人来作假见证，总得不着实据。"申命记19章警告假见证——但基督却是假见证的受害者。神的公义要求：假见证要受被告本该受的刑罚——基督受了死亡——是为了假见证他的人。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**Cities of Refuge—Christ Is Our Refuge**—Hebrews 6:17-18: "God...has strongly encouraged us who have fled for refuge to hold fast to the hope set before us." OT refuge cities are spatial shelters—Christ is our spiritual refuge. We are all sin's "fugitives"—under God's just judgment we need refuge—Christ is that refuge. Not because we're innocent—but because He bore our sin.

**"Eye for Eye" and Christ's Sermon on the Mount**—Matthew 5:38-39: "You have heard that it was said, 'An eye for an eye'...But I say to you, Do not resist the one who is evil." Christ didn't abolish the proportionality principle—He elevated it: in Christ, not "exactly fair"—but "grace beyond fairness."

**False Witness—Christ Was Killed by False Witness**—Matthew 26:59-60: "The chief priests and the whole council were seeking false testimony against Jesus...though many false witnesses came forward." Deuteronomy warns false witnesses—but Christ was false witness's victim. God's justice requires: false witnesses receive the accused's due punishment—Christ received death—for those who falsely witnessed against Him.
