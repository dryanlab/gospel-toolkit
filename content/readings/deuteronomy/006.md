## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第六章包含了以色列信仰最核心的宣告——"以色列啊，你要听！耶和华我们神是独一的主。你要尽心、尽性、尽力爱耶和华你的神。"这段经文被称为"示玛"（Shema，希伯来文"听"的意思），是犹太信仰的核心，也是耶稣所说的"最大的诫命"。

**示玛——以色列的信仰告白（第4-5节）**

> "以色列啊，你要听！耶和华我们神是独一的主。你要尽心、尽性、尽力爱耶和华你的神。"

弟兄姐妹，"以色列啊你要听"——不是"看"不是"想"——而是"听"。弟兄姐妹，信仰始于"听"——"信道是从听道来的"（罗10:17）。在所有感官中，神选择了"听"作为信仰的入口——因为"听"需要你安静下来、注意力集中、接受外来的话语。

"耶和华我们神是独一的主"——弟兄姐妹，"独一的"（echad）在希伯来文中有"合一"的含义——不只是数量上的"一个"——而是本质上的"合一"。弟兄姐妹，三位一体的神不是三个神——而是"独一的主"——圣父、圣子、圣灵在本质上是"合一的"。

"你要尽心、尽性、尽力爱耶和华你的神"——弟兄姐妹，三个"尽"——心（leb，意志和思想的中心）、性（nephesh，灵魂/生命）、力（meod，极度/力量）。弟兄姐妹，不是"部分地爱"——不是"有空的时候爱"——而是"尽"——用你全部的心、全部的灵魂、全部的力量。弟兄姐妹，神要求的爱是全人的、是极致的、是不保留的。

**教导你的儿女（第6-9节）**

> "我今日所吩咐你的话都要记在心上，也要殷勤教训你的儿女。无论你坐在家里，行在路上，躺下，起来，都要谈论。也要系在手上为记号，戴在额上为经文。又要写在你房屋的门框上，并你的城门上。"

弟兄姐妹，"殷勤教训你的儿女"——弟兄姐妹，信仰的传承不是学校的责任——不是教会的责任——首先是家庭的责任。"无论你坐在家里行在路上躺下起来都要谈论"——弟兄姐妹，这不是"每天固定一个小时的圣经课"——而是"生活中随时随地"。吃饭的时候、走路的时候、睡觉前、起床后——信仰要渗透在生活的每一个角落。

弟兄姐妹，犹太人到今天仍然实践这段经文——他们在门框上钉一个小盒子（mezuzah），里面放着示玛的经文。弟兄姐妹，形式可以不同——但原则不变：让神的话语充满你的生活空间——让你的孩子在每一个转身都遇到神的话。

**不要忘记（第10-15节）**

> "耶和华你的神领你进他向你列祖亚伯拉罕、以撒、雅各起誓应许给你的地。那里有城邑，又大又美，非你所建造的；有房屋，装满各样美物，非你所装满的；有凿成的水井，非你所凿成的；还有葡萄园、橄榄园，非你所栽种的；你吃了而且饱足。那时你要谨慎，免得你忘记将你从埃及地为奴之家领出来的耶和华。"

弟兄姐妹，"你吃了而且饱足那时你要谨慎免得忘记"——弟兄姐妹，这是人类最常见的属灵疾病之一——富足中忘记神。贫穷的时候你祷告——饱足的时候你忘记。困难的时候你寻求——顺利的时候你骄傲。弟兄姐妹，摩西警告你：最危险的时候不是你穷的时候——而是你富的时候。弟兄姐妹，"非你所建造的城""非你所装满的房屋""非你所凿的井"——一切都是神给的——但人的本性就是接受了恩典后忘记恩典的来源。

**不可试探耶和华（第16节）**

> "你们不可试探耶和华你们的神，像你们在玛撒试探他一样。"

弟兄姐妹，耶稣在旷野受试探时引用了这句话——"不可试探主你的神"（太4:7）。弟兄姐妹，试探神是什么？就是故意把自己放在危险中、然后要求神来救你。弟兄姐妹，信心不是冒险——信心是信靠。

弟兄姐妹，申命记第六章给了我们信仰生活的总纲——听、爱、教导、记住、不要忘记。这五个动词构成了信仰生活的完整框架。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 6 contains Israel's most core confession—"Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one. You shall love the LORD your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your might." Known as the "Shema" (Hebrew for "hear"), this is Judaism's core confession and what Jesus called "the greatest commandment."

**The Shema—Israel's Confession (vv. 4-5)**

> "Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one. You shall love the LORD your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your might."

Brothers and sisters, "Hear, O Israel"—not "see," not "think"—but "hear." Faith begins with "hearing"—"So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God." (Rom 10:17). "One" (echad) in Hebrew carries the meaning of "unity"—not just numerical "one" but essential "unity." The triune God is not three gods—but "one Lord."

"With all your heart, soul, and might"—three "alls"—heart (leb, center of will and thought), soul (nephesh, life), might (meod, extreme/strength). Not "partially love"—not "love when convenient"—but "all"—your entire heart, entire soul, entire strength. God demands love that is whole-person, extreme, unreserved.

**Teach Your Children (vv. 6-9)**

> "These words...you shall teach them diligently to your children, and shall talk of them when you sit in your house, and when you walk by the way, and when you lie down, and when you rise."

Brothers and sisters, "teach diligently to your children"—faith transmission is first the family's responsibility. "When you sit, walk, lie down, rise"—not "one fixed Bible hour daily"—but "anytime, anywhere in life." Faith must permeate every corner of life.

**Don't Forget (vv. 10-15)**

> "When the LORD your God brings you into the land...with great and good cities that you did not build, and houses full of all good things that you did not fill...then take care lest you forget the LORD."

Brothers and sisters, "when you eat and are full, take care lest you forget"—humanity's most common spiritual disease—forgetting God in abundance. When poor you pray—when full you forget. Moses warns: the most dangerous time isn't when you're poor—but when you're rich.

Brothers and sisters, Deuteronomy 6 gives faith life's framework—hear, love, teach, remember, don't forget. These five verbs form the complete structure of the faith life.

## 历史背景（中文）

申命记6:4-5的"示玛"（Shema）是犹太信仰最核心的经文——犹太人每天早晚各诵读一次。"以色列啊你要听"是犹太教礼拜仪式的开始语。耶稣在马可福音12:29-30被问到"最大的诫命是什么"时，直接引用了示玛。"系在手上为记号戴在额上为经文"（第8节）后来发展成为犹太人的"经文匣"（tefillin/phylacteries）——小皮盒里装着羊皮纸上的经文，绑在手臂和额头上。门框上的"mezuzah"（门柱经文盒）直到今天仍然是犹太家庭的标志。耶稣在马太福音23:5批评法利赛人"将佩戴的经文做宽了"——说明到了新约时代这些外在的象征已经被形式主义化了。

## Historical Context (English)

Deuteronomy 6:4-5's "Shema" is Judaism's most core text—Jews recite it twice daily, morning and evening. "Hear, O Israel" opens Jewish worship. When asked "what is the greatest commandment" in Mark 12:29-30, Jesus directly quoted the Shema. "Bind them as a sign on your hand, as frontlets between your eyes" (v. 8) developed into Jewish "tefillin/phylacteries"—small leather boxes containing parchment Scripture, bound on arm and forehead. The "mezuzah" (doorpost Scripture box) remains a Jewish household marker today. Jesus in Matthew 23:5 criticized Pharisees for making phylacteries broad—showing by NT times these external symbols had been formalized.

## Structure (English)

**Purpose of Statutes (vv. 1-3)**—Fear the LORD → keep statutes → that it may go well → land of milk and honey.
**The Shema—Core Faith (vv. 4-5)**—Hear O Israel → LORD is one → love with all heart/soul/might.
**Diligent Teaching (vv. 6-9)**—Keep in heart → teach diligently to children → talk sitting/walking/lying/rising → bind/wear/write.
**Don't Forget (vv. 10-15)**—Enter good land → cities/houses/wells not yours → when full take care → lest forget LORD.
**Don't Test (vv. 16-19)**—Don't test the LORD → do what is right and good → drive out enemies.
**Future Teaching (vv. 20-25)**—When your son asks → tell the Exodus story → this will be our righteousness.

## 神学要点（中文）

**"尽心尽性尽力"——全人的爱**
改革宗传统教导爱神是最大的诫命。韦敏斯德小要理问答第42问："十诫的总纲是什么？"答："十诫的总纲就是：要尽心、尽性、尽意、尽力爱主──我们的神，并且爱邻舍如己。"三个"尽"描述的不是三种不同的爱——而是同一种爱的三个维度：心灵的（情感）、灵魂的（本质的）、力量的（行动的）。爱神不是部分的奉献——而是全人的委身。

**家庭门训——信仰传承的首要场所**
改革宗传统高度重视家庭敬拜和门训。韦敏斯德大要理问答第156问论读经的责任。"殷勤教训你的儿女"是父母——尤其是父亲——的首要属灵责任。清教徒传统特别强调"家庭是小教会"——父亲是家庭的牧师。

**"独一的主"与三位一体**
改革宗传统教导"echad"（独一的）与三位一体不矛盾。韦敏斯德信条第2章：在神的统一性之中有三个位格。"独一的"描述的是神的本质（一个本质）——三位一体描述的是神的位格（三个位格）。两者和谐共存——这是基督教信仰最深奥的奥秘之一。

## Theology (English)

**"All Heart, Soul, Might"—Whole-Person Love**
The Reformed tradition teaches loving God is the greatest commandment. Westminster Shorter Catechism Q42: "The sum of the ten commandments is to love the Lord our God with all our heart, mind, and strength." Three "alls" describe not three different loves but three dimensions of the same love.

**Family Discipleship—Faith Transmission's Primary Venue**
The Reformed tradition highly values family worship and discipleship. WLC Q156. "Teach diligently to your children" is parents'—especially fathers'—primary spiritual responsibility. Puritan tradition especially emphasized "the family is a little church."

**"One LORD" and the Trinity**
The Reformed tradition teaches "echad" (one) doesn't contradict the Trinity. Westminster Confession chapter 2: In the unity of the Godhead there are three persons. "One" describes God's essence (one essence)—Trinity describes God's persons (three persons). Both coexist harmoniously.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**示玛与最大的诫命——基督的诠释**——马可福音12:29-31"耶稣回答说：第一要紧的……就是说：以色列啊，你要听，主我们神是独一的主。你要尽心、尽性、尽意、尽力爱主你的神。其次就是说：要爱人如己。再没有比这两条诫命更大的了。"基督引用示玛为最大的诫命——并加上"爱人如己"——将十诫的两块石版（爱神和爱人）总结为两条诫命。

**"不可试探主你的神"——基督在旷野得胜**——马太福音4:5-7撒但把耶稣带到殿顶说"你若是神的儿子就跳下去"。耶稣引用申命记6:16回答："经上又记着说'不可试探主你的神。'"基督用示玛的章节（申6）击败了撒但——祂亲身实践了"尽心尽性尽力爱神"的诫命。

**"殷勤教训你的儿女"——基督是最伟大的教师**——马太福音28:20"凡我所吩咐你们的，都教训他们遵守。"大使命是"殷勤教训"的终极表达——不只是教自己的儿女——而是使"万民"作主的门徒。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**The Shema and the Greatest Commandment—Christ's Interpretation**—Mark 12:29-31: Jesus answered, "The most important is, 'Hear, O Israel: The Lord our God, the Lord is one. And you shall love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind and with all your strength.' The second is this: 'You shall love your neighbor as yourself.'" Christ quoted the Shema as the greatest commandment—adding "love your neighbor"—summarizing both tablets into two commands.

**"Do Not Test the Lord"—Christ's Victory in the Wilderness**—Matthew 4:5-7: Satan took Jesus to the temple pinnacle saying "throw yourself down." Jesus quoted Deuteronomy 6:16: "It is written, 'You shall not put the Lord your God to the test.'" Christ defeated Satan using the Shema's chapter (Deut 6)—personally fulfilling the command to love God with all heart, soul, and might.

**"Teach Diligently"—Christ Is the Greatest Teacher**—Matthew 28:20: "teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you." The Great Commission is "diligent teaching's" ultimate expression—not just teaching one's own children—but making "all nations" disciples.
