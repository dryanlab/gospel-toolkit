## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第三十章是整本申命记中最温暖、最感人的一章——在二十八章的可怕咒诅和二十九章的严厉警告之后——第三十章像一缕阳光穿透乌云。弟兄姐妹，这一章说的是：即使你犯了罪被分散到万国——只要你回转归向耶和华——祂必再次接纳你。

**悔改的应许（第1-10节）**

> "我所陈明在你面前的这一切咒诅都临到你身上——你在耶和华你神追赶你到的万国中必心里追念祝福的话——你和你的子孙若尽心尽性归向耶和华你的神，照着我今日一切所吩咐的听从他的话——那时耶和华你的神必怜恤你——救回你这被掳的子民——耶和华你的神要回转过来——从分散你到的万民中将你招聚回来。"

弟兄姐妹，"耶和华你的神必怜恤你"——弟兄姐妹，在五十四节的咒诅之后——这句话是何等的甘甜！弟兄姐妹，神没有说"如果你犯罪就完了"——祂说"即使咒诅都临到了——只要你回转——我必怜恤你。"弟兄姐妹，这就是恩典的本质——不是你配得——而是祂愿意。

弟兄姐妹，"耶和华你的神要回转过来"——弟兄姐妹，注意这个表达——不只是你回转——神也"回转"！弟兄姐妹，希伯来文"shub"（回转）在这段经文中同时用在人和神身上——你回转向神——神也回转向你。弟兄姐妹，悔改不只是人的行动——而是神的恩典使之成为可能。

> **"这诫命不是难行的"（第11-14节）**

> "我今日所吩咐你的诫命不是你难行的，也不是离你远的。不是在天上使你说'谁替我们上天取下来使我们听见可以遵行呢？'也不是在海外使你说'谁替我们过海取了来使我们听见可以遵行呢？'这话却离你甚近就在你口中就在你心里使你可以遵行。"

弟兄姐妹，"就在你口中就在你心里"——弟兄姐妹，保罗在罗马书10:6-8引用了这段话来论证信心的义："你若口里认耶稣为主心里信神叫他从死里复活就必得救。"弟兄姐妹，摩西说律法"不是难行的"——保罗说信心也"不是难行的"——就在你口中就在你心里。弟兄姐妹，救恩不在天上（不需要你做什么不可能的事）——不在海外（不需要你去什么遥远的地方）——就在你的口和你的心——认耶稣为主就够了。

**生死的选择（第15-20节）**

> "看哪我今日将生与福死与祸陈明在你面前。吩咐你爱耶和华你的神遵行他的道谨守他的诫命律例典章使你可以存活人数增多——你若心里偏离不肯听从却被勾引去敬拜事奉别神——我今日明明告诉你们你们必要灭亡——我今日呼天唤地向你作见证：我将生死祸福陈明在你面前，所以你要拣选生命使你和你的后裔都得存活——且爱耶和华你的神听从他的话紧紧地与他联结因为他是你的生命也是你日子长久的原因。"

弟兄姐妹，"我今日呼天唤地向你作见证"——弟兄姐妹，摩西把天和地都叫来做证人！弟兄姐妹，这不是私下的建议——而是宇宙性的公开宣告。

弟兄姐妹，"所以你要拣选生命"——弟兄姐妹，这是整本申命记的高潮——也是整个摩西五经的高潮之一。弟兄姐妹，神不是说"我替你选好了"——虽然祂在永恒中确实已经选了——但在历史中祂对你说"你要拣选"。弟兄姐妹，"拣选生命"——不是抽象的哲学选择——而是具体的："爱耶和华你的神——听从他的话——紧紧地与他联结。"弟兄姐妹，"因为他是你的生命"——不是说神给你生命——而是说神就是你的生命。弟兄姐妹，离开神不是失去一些好处——而是失去生命本身。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 30 is the warmest, most moving chapter in all of Deuteronomy—after chapter 28's terrifying curses and chapter 29's stern warnings—chapter 30 is sunlight piercing storm clouds. Even if you sin and are scattered among nations—if you return to the LORD—He will accept you again.

**The Promise of Repentance (vv. 1-10)**

> "When all these things come upon you...and you return to the LORD your God...then the LORD your God will restore your fortunes and have mercy on you." After fifty-four verses of curses—"have mercy on you"—how sweet! God doesn't say "if you sin, it's over"—He says "even after all curses fall—just return—I will have mercy."

"The LORD your God will return"—not only you return—God also "returns"! Hebrew "shub" (return) applies to both human and God here. Repentance isn't just human action—but made possible by God's grace.

> **"This Commandment Is Not Too Hard" (vv. 11-14)**

"It is not in heaven...It is not beyond the sea...But the word is very near you. It is in your mouth and in your heart." Paul quoted this in Romans 10:6-8 for the righteousness of faith: "If you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart...you will be saved." Salvation isn't in heaven (no impossible feats needed)—not beyond the sea (no distant journeys required)—it's in your mouth and heart—confessing Jesus as Lord is enough.

**The Choice of Life (vv. 15-20)**

"I have set before you today life and good, death and evil...I call heaven and earth to witness against you today, that I have set before you life and death, blessing and curse. Therefore choose life." The climax of Deuteronomy—and the entire Pentateuch. "Choose life"—not abstract philosophy—but specific: "Love the LORD your God—obey his voice—hold fast to him." "For he is your life"—not that God gives you life—God IS your life. Leaving God isn't losing some benefits—it's losing life itself.

## 历史背景（中文）

"从分散你到的万民中将你招聚回来"在犹太历史中有双重应验——第一次是公元前539年波斯居鲁士王允许犹太人从巴比伦回归（以斯拉记1:1-4），第二次是1948年以色列建国后犹太人从世界各地回归。锡安主义运动的创始者赫茨尔(Theodor Herzl)虽然是世俗犹太人——但许多支持以色列复国的人引用申命记30:3-5作为圣经根据。保罗在罗马书10:6-8对申命记30:12-14的引用是新约引用旧约最重要的释经实例之一——保罗将摩西的话重新诠释为指向基督的信心之义。

## Historical Context (English)

"Gather you again from all the peoples" has dual historical fulfillment—first in 539 BC when Cyrus allowed Jews to return from Babylon (Ezra 1:1-4), second in the post-1948 Jewish return to Israel. Paul's quotation of Deuteronomy 30:12-14 in Romans 10:6-8 is one of the NT's most important OT interpretive examples—Paul reinterprets Moses' words as pointing to Christ's righteousness by faith.

## 经文结构（中文）

**悔改的条件（1-2节）**——咒诅都临到→在万国中追念祝福→归向耶和华。
**神的怜恤（3-7节）**——神必怜恤→救回被掳→招聚回来→即使在天涯→神也从那里招聚→领你进列祖之地→使你享福增多→将咒诅加在仇敌身上。
**再次的顺服（8-10节）**——你必再听从→遵行诫命→神必喜悦→你尽心尽性归向他。
**诫命不是难行的（11-14节）**——不在天上→不在海外→就在你口中就在你心里。
**生死的选择（15-20节）**——生与福死与祸→爱耶和华→遵行他的道→心若偏离→必要灭亡→呼天唤地作见证→拣选生命→爱神听他话→紧紧联结→他是你的生命。

## Structure (English)

**Repentance's Condition (vv. 1-2)**—Curses fulfilled → remember blessings among nations → return to the LORD.
**God's Mercy (vv. 3-7)**—God will have mercy → restore fortunes → gather back → even from heaven's farthest end → bring to ancestors' land → prosper and multiply → put curses on enemies.
**Renewed Obedience (vv. 8-10)**—You shall again obey → keep commandments → God will delight → when you return wholeheartedly.
**Commandment Not Too Hard (vv. 11-14)**—Not in heaven → not beyond the sea → in your mouth and heart.
**Life and Death Choice (vv. 15-20)**—Life/good, death/evil → love the LORD → walk in His ways → if heart turns → you will perish → heaven and earth as witnesses → choose life → love God, obey, hold fast → He is your life.

## 神学要点（中文）

**悔改的恩典——先于人的回转**
改革宗传统教导悔改本身是神恩典的工作。韦敏斯德信条第15章论悔改。申命记30章的次序是关键的：不是"你先悔改然后神才怜恤"——而是"神的怜恤使你能够悔改"。第6节"耶和华你神必将你心里和你后裔心里的污秽除掉使你尽心尽性爱耶和华你的神"——是神先割你的心——然后你才能爱神。

**"拣选生命"——神的主权与人的责任**
改革宗传统同时教导神的主权预定和人的责任选择。韦敏斯德信条第3章与第9章。"拣选生命"是真实的命令——人被要求做出选择。但同时——没有神的恩典——没有人会选择生命。弟兄姐妹，这不是矛盾——而是奥秘。

**"他是你的生命"——神不只是给予者更是生命本身**
改革宗传统教导与神的联合是救恩的核心。韦敏斯德信条第26章论与基督联合。"他是你的生命"——不是"他给你生命"——是"他就是你的生命"。约翰福音14:6"我就是道路真理生命。"神不是手段——神是目的。

## Theology (English)

**Repentance's Grace—Prior to Human Turning**
The Reformed tradition teaches repentance itself is God's gracious work. Westminster Confession chapter 15. The order in Deuteronomy 30 is key: not "you repent then God has mercy"—but "God's mercy enables repentance." Verse 6: "The LORD will circumcise your heart...so that you will love the LORD"—God first circumcises your heart—then you can love Him.

**"Choose Life"—God's Sovereignty and Human Responsibility**
The Reformed tradition teaches both divine sovereignty and human responsibility. Westminster Confession chapters 3 and 9.: Choose life is a genuine command—humans are required to choose. Yet without God's grace—no one would choose life. Not contradiction—but mystery.

**"He Is Your Life"—God Isn't Just Giver but Life Itself**
The Reformed tradition teaches union with God is salvation's core. Westminster Confession chapter 26.: He is your life—not "He gives you life"—"He IS your life." John 14:6. God isn't the means—God is the end.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"就在你口中就在你心里"——信心的义指向基督**——罗马书10:8-10"这话却离你甚近就在你口中就在你心里——就是我们所传信主的道——你若口里认耶稣为主心里信神叫他从死里复活就必得救。因为人心里相信就可以称义口里承认就可以得救。"弟兄姐妹，摩西说律法在你口和心里——保罗说信基督也在你口和心里。救恩从来不是远在天边——而是近在咫尺——"口里认耶稣为主"——就这么近。

**"拣选生命"——基督就是生命**——约翰福音11:25"耶稣对她说我就是复活我就是生命信我的人虽然死了也必复活。"摩西说"拣选生命"——耶稣说"我就是生命"。弟兄姐妹，拣选生命就是拣选基督——拣选基督就是拣选生命。两者是同一个选择。

**"耶和华必将你心里的污秽除掉"——基督赐下新心**——以西结书36:26"我也要赐给你们一个新心将新灵放在你们里面又从你们的肉体中除掉石心赐给你们肉心。"摩西预言了"心的割礼"——基督成就了它——赐给我们"新心"和"新灵"。旧约的应许在新约中成就了。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"In Your Mouth and Heart"—Faith's Righteousness Points to Christ**—Romans 10:8-10: "The word is near you, in your mouth and in your heart...if you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." Moses said the law is in mouth and heart—Paul said faith in Christ is in mouth and heart. Salvation was never far away—"confess Jesus as Lord"—that close.

**"Choose Life"—Christ IS Life**—John 11:25: "I am the resurrection and the life." Moses said "choose life"—Jesus said "I am the life." Choosing life is choosing Christ—choosing Christ is choosing life. The same choice.

**"The LORD Will Circumcise Your Heart"—Christ Gives New Hearts**—Ezekiel 36:26: "I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you." Moses prophesied "heart circumcision"—Christ fulfilled it—giving us "new hearts" and "new spirits." OT promises fulfilled in the NT.
