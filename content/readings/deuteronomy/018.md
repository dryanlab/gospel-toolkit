## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第十八章是旧约先知职分的规范——利未人祭司的供养、禁止巫术占卜，以及对一位伟大先知降临的应许。第15节和第18节是旧约中最重要的弥赛亚预言之一。

**利未人的供养（第1-8节）**

> "祭司利未人和利未全支派，必在以色列中无份无业；他们所吃用的就是献给耶和华的火祭和一切所捐的。他们在弟兄中没有产业，耶和华就是他们的产业，正如耶和华所应许他们的。"

弟兄姐妹，"耶和华就是他们的产业"——弟兄姐妹，利未人没有土地——他们的"产业"是神自己。弟兄姐妹，多少人羡慕有稳定的资产、固定的财产——但利未人被神设计为没有土地——因为他们的产业是神自己。弟兄姐妹，当你手里抓着的东西越来越多的时候——你的心向神的开放就越来越少。利未人的无地恰恰是一种蒙福——强迫他们完全依靠神。

弟兄姐妹，"凡从以色列来的你所贡献给耶和华的，就是各种头生的、各种举祭，以及各种奉献物、还愿物，都要归给祭司"——神设计了一套经济体系来供养利未人——以色列人献的祭有一部分归祭司吃用。弟兄姐妹，这是新约"应当养活传道人"原则的旧约根基（提前5:18，林前9:14）。

**禁止巫术占卜（第9-14节）**

> "你到了耶和华你神所赐之地，那些国民所行可憎恶的事，你不可学着行。你们中间不可有人使儿女经火，也不可有占卜的、观兆的、用法术的、行邪术的、用迷术的、交鬼的、行巫术的、过阴的。凡行这些事的都是耶和华所憎恶的。"

弟兄姐妹，这份禁止名单几乎涵盖了所有形式的超自然咨询——占星、预言、巫术、招魂……弟兄姐妹，为什么禁止？不是因为这些东西不存在——而是因为这些都是试图绕过神去获取信息和力量。弟兄姐妹，当你转向占卜的时候——你是在说"我不相信神足够了解我的未来"。弟兄姐妹，神禁止这些不是要把你蒙在鼓里——而是说：你的一切都在我手中——你来问我就够了。

**那将要来的先知（第15-22节）**

> "耶和华你的神要从你们弟兄中间给你兴起一位先知像我，你们要听从他……我要将当说的话传给他，他要将我一切所吩咐的都传给他们。"

弟兄姐妹，"兴起一位先知像我"——这是申命记中最重要的弥赛亚预言之一。弟兄姐妹，注意"像我"——"像摩西"——摩西是谁？以色列最伟大的先知——与神"面对面"说话的人（申34:10）——带领百姓出埃及、立新约、做中保。弟兄姐妹，"像摩西的先知"不是寻常的先知——而是一位终极的先知——祂不只传达神的话，祂就是神的话。

弟兄姐妹，使徒约翰在约翰福音1章记载施洗约翰被问到"你是那先知吗？"（约1:21）——"那先知"就是指申命记18:15-18的先知。弟兄姐妹，五旬节之后彼得在所罗门廊引用这节经文说："摩西曾说：主神要从你们弟兄中间给你们兴起一位先知像我，凡他向你们所说的话，你们都要听从他"（徒3:22）——直接应用在耶稣身上。

弟兄姐妹，"凡不听从那先知之话的，我必讨他的罪"——弟兄姐妹，不听那先知的是死罪——这说明那先知的权柄是至高的。弟兄姐妹，耶稣基督就是这位先知——不听祂的将面临神的审判。弟兄姐妹，你听从基督的话吗？

**分辨真假先知（第20-22节）**

> "若有先知擅敢托我的名说我所未曾吩咐他说的话，或是奉别神的名说话，那先知就必治死。你心里若说：'耶和华所未曾吩咐的话我们怎能知道呢？'先知托耶和华的名说话，所说的若不成就，也无效验，这就是耶和华所未曾吩咐的，是那先知擅自说的，你不要怕他。"

弟兄姐妹，"若不成就也无效验"——弟兄姐妹，这是分辨真假先知的一个测试——预言有没有应验。弟兄姐妹，当然不是所有的预言都可以立即验证——但这个原则的核心是：真先知的话是有信度的、有历史记录的、是神来的。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 18 establishes prophetic regulations—Levitical support, prohibiting divination, and the promise of a great prophet's coming. Verses 15 and 18 are among the OT's most important messianic prophecies.

**Levitical Support (vv. 1-8)**

"The LORD is their inheritance." Levites had no land—their "inheritance" is God Himself. The more you clutch in your hands, the less your heart opens to God. The Levites' landlessness was actually a blessing—forcing complete dependence on God.

**Prohibiting Divination (vv. 9-14)**

This prohibition list covers virtually all forms of supernatural consultation. Why forbidden? Not because these things don't exist—but they all attempt to bypass God to obtain information and power. When you turn to divination—you're saying "I don't trust God to sufficiently know my future."

**The Coming Prophet (vv. 15-22)**

> "The LORD your God will raise up for you a prophet like me from among you, from your brothers—it is to him you shall listen."

"A prophet like me"—like Moses—Israel's greatest prophet, who spoke with God "face to face," led people from Egypt, established covenant, served as mediator. A "prophet like Moses" is not an ordinary prophet—but an ultimate Prophet—who doesn't just convey God's word—He is God's word.

John records the Baptist being asked "And they asked him, What then? Art thou Elias? And he saith, I am not. Art thou that prophet? And he answered, No." (John 1:21)—"the Prophet" refers to Deuteronomy 18:15-18. After Pentecost, Peter quoted this directly applying it to Jesus (Acts 3:22).

"Whoever will not listen to my words that he shall speak in my name, I myself will require it of him." Not listening to that prophet is a capital offense—showing that prophet's authority is supreme. Jesus Christ is that Prophet.

## 历史背景（中文）

申命记18:15-18是新约中被引用来证明耶稣是弥赛亚的重要经文之一。使徒行传3:22-23彼得引用它，7:37司提反引用它。死海古卷（《公会规章》1QS 9:11）也期待"那先知"的降临——说明第一世纪的犹太人确实在等待这位特定的先知。"像我"（kamoni）在希伯来文中有"以我为模型"的含义——暗示那先知不只是"类似摩西"——而是在摩西的模式上更完全的实现。禁止巫术占卜的律法与古代近东的背景相关——美索不达米亚和埃及都有高度发展的占卜体系，巴比伦的占星术在古代世界非常盛行。

## Historical Context (English)

Deuteronomy 18:15-18 is one of the NT's key texts proving Jesus is Messiah. Acts 3:22-23 (Peter) and 7:37 (Stephen) quote it. Dead Sea Scrolls (1QS 9:11) also anticipate "the Prophet"—showing first-century Jews genuinely awaited this specific prophet. "Like me" (kamoni) in Hebrew implies "modeled on me"—the prophet isn't merely "similar to Moses" but a more complete realization in Moses' pattern. Divination prohibition relates to ANE background—Mesopotamia and Egypt had highly developed divination systems.

## 经文结构（中文）

**利未人无业靠神（1-8节）**——无份无业→火祭为食→耶和华是他们的产业→从以色列得首生举祭奉献物。
**禁止迷信巫术（9-14节）**——不可学列国可憎的事→不可使儿女经火→不可占卜观兆用法术行巫术→这些都是神所憎恶的。
**那位先知（15-22节）**——耶和华要兴起一位先知→像摩西一样→你们要听他→在何烈山你们求过→我要将话传给他→不听的必被讨罪→假先知的标志：所说无效验。

## Structure (English)

**Levites Have LORD as Inheritance (vv. 1-8)**—No portion or inheritance → fire offerings as food → LORD is their inheritance → receive firstborn, heave offerings.
**Prohibiting Occult (vv. 9-14)**—Don't learn nations' abominations → no child sacrifice → no divination/omens/sorcery → abominations to LORD.
**The Prophet (vv. 15-22)**—LORD will raise a prophet → like Moses → listen to him → as you asked at Horeb → I'll put words in his mouth → whoever won't listen: I'll require it → false prophet: words don't come true.

## 神学要点（中文）

**先知职分——神话语的传递**
改革宗传统教导神通过祂的话语与人沟通。韦敏斯德信条第1章。先知是神话语的传递者——不是神话语的来源。"我要将当说的话传给他"——先知只传达神给的话。这个原则区分了真先知（传神的话）和假先知（传自己的话或别神的话）。

**禁止占卜——神是启示的唯一来源**
改革宗传统教导神的话语是唯一可靠的启示。韦敏斯德信条第1章。占卜是试图从神以外的来源获得关于未来的知识——这是对神主权的挑战。神禁止占卜不是因为他要隐瞒信息——而是因为所有真实的启示都从祂而来——祂已经在祂的话语中给了我们所需要的。

**"像摩西的先知"——先知、祭司、王三职**
改革宗传统教导基督成全了先知、祭司、王三重职分。韦敏斯德小要理问答第23-26问。申命记18章的"先知"代表了先知职分——基督是最终的先知（约1:18他将神表明出来）、祭司（来7:25代祷）、王（启19:16万王之王）。

## Theology (English)

**Prophetic Office—Transmission of God's Word**
The Reformed tradition teaches God communicates through His word. Westminster Confession chapter 1. Prophets transmit God's word—they don't originate it. "I will put my words in his mouth."

**Prohibiting Divination—God Is the Sole Source of Revelation**
The Reformed tradition teaches God's word is the only reliable revelation. Westminster Confession chapter 1. Divination attempts to get future knowledge from non-divine sources—a challenge to God's sovereignty.

**"A Prophet Like Moses"—Prophet, Priest, King Three Offices**
The Reformed tradition teaches Christ fulfills prophet, priest, king threefold offices. WSC Q23-26. Deuteronomy 18's "prophet" represents the prophetic office—Christ is the ultimate Prophet (John 1:18), Priest (Heb 7:25), King (Rev 19:16).

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"兴起一位先知像摩西"——这就是耶稣**——约翰福音6:14"众人看见耶稣所行的神迹就说'这真是那要到世间来的先知。'"约翰福音1:45"腓力找着拿但业，对他说：摩西在律法上所写的，和众先知所记的那一位，我们遇见了。"弟兄姐妹，那将要来的先知——他们找到了——就是拿撒勒人耶稣。

**摩西与基督的对比**——希伯来书3:3-5"耶稣比摩西算是更配多得荣耀……摩西为仆人，在神的全家诚然尽忠，为要证明将来必传说的事；但基督为儿子，治理神的家。"摩西是仆人——基督是儿子。摩西传递话语——基督自己就是话语（约1:1）。摩西是先知中最大的——基督是先知职分的终极成全。

**"不听他的必被讨罪"——基督是判断的标准**——约翰福音12:48"弃绝我不领受我话的人，有审判他的；就是我所讲的道，在末日要审判他。"申命记说不听那先知必被讨罪——基督说不领受我话的有末日的审判。弟兄姐妹，那位先知的话是审判的标准——基督的话就是那终极的标准。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"A Prophet Like Moses"—This Is Jesus**—John 6:14: "When the people saw the sign that he had done, they said, 'This is indeed the Prophet who is to come into the world!'" John 1:45: "We have found him of whom Moses in the Law and also the prophets wrote, Jesus of Nazareth."

**Moses vs. Christ**—Hebrews 3:3-5: "Jesus has been counted worthy of more glory than Moses...Moses was faithful in all God's house as a servant...but Christ is faithful over God's house as a son." Moses was servant—Christ is Son. Moses transmitted the word—Christ is the Word (John 1:1).

**"Whoever Won't Listen Will Be Judged"—Christ Is Judgment's Standard**—John 12:48: "The one who rejects me and does not receive my words has a judge; the word that I have spoken will judge him on the last day." Deuteronomy says not listening to the prophet brings judgment—Christ says not receiving my words brings final judgment.
