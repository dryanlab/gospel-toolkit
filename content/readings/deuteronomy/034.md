## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第三十四章是整本摩西五经的结局——我的死。弟兄姐妹，整本摩西五经从创世记1:1"起初神创造天地"开始——到这里结束。从创造到死亡——从伊甸园到尼波山——从起初到终结。弟兄姐妹，这是旧约最庄严的一幕。

**尼波山上的眺望（第1-4节）**

> "摩西从摩押平原登尼波山，上了那与耶利哥相对的毗斯迦山顶。耶和华把基列全地直到但，拿弗他利全地，以法莲、玛拿西的地，犹大全地直到西海，南地和棕树城耶利哥的平原直到琐珥，都指给他看。"

弟兄姐妹，我站在山顶上——看见了整片应许之地——从北到南——从东到西——一切都在我眼前展开。弟兄姐妹，这是一种奇特的滋味——看见了——但不能进去。弟兄姐妹，"耶和华对他说这就是我向亚伯拉罕以撒雅各起誓说我要赐给你后裔之地。我使你眼睛看见了，你却不得过到那里去。"

弟兄姐妹，"你却不得过到那里去"——弟兄姐妹，我在摩押地曾经错打磐石（民20章）——神说那时"你也不得过到那里去"。弟兄姐妹，一个领袖的一次罪——改变了他的整个命运。弟兄姐妹，但神没有让我在绝望中死去——祂让我先看见——然后才离去。弟兄姐妹，看见了就够了——愿景比实现更重要——不是每一个人都要进入他所看见的——但看见了就算得着了。

**神亲手埋葬（第5-6节）**

> "于是耶和华的仆人摩西死在摩押地，正如耶和华所说的。他被埋葬在摩押地、伯毗珥对面的谷中，只是到今日没有人知道他的坟墓在哪里。"

弟兄姐妹，"没有人知道他的坟墓在哪里"——弟兄姐妹，为什么神要把我的坟墓隐藏起来？弟兄姐妹，我知道我们的本性——如果知道了摩西的坟墓在哪里——那个地方早就成了朝圣地——有人开始供奉我的骨头——把我当成神来崇拜。弟兄姐妹，神保护我死后的荣誉——不让我成为偶像。弟兄姐妹，真正的先知和领袖的荣耀是荣耀神——不是荣耀自己——即使在死后也要防止被神化。

**摩西的身体与眼力（第7节）**

> "摩西死的时候年一百二十岁；眼目没有昏花，精神没有衰败。"

弟兄姐妹，"眼目没有昏花，精神没有衰败"——弟兄姐妹，一百二十岁！以人类正常的衰老——我应该早就眼花耳聋了。但神保守了我——让我到生命的最后一天仍然清晰地看见、清晰地思考。弟兄姐妹，神不只是让你活着——祂让你有尊严地活着——让你清醒地完成祂给你的使命。

**以色列人的哀哭（第8节）**

> "以色列人在摩押平原为摩西哀哭了三十天，为摩西居丧哀哭的日子就满了。"

弟兄姐妹，三十天——弟兄姐妹，这是以色列历史上最长的国丧。亚伦死的时候也是哭了三十天（民20:29）。弟兄姐妹，好的领袖死去——百姓哭泣——不是因为礼节——而是因为真实地失去了。弟兄姐妹，你会不会被人这样哀哭——取决于你有没有真实地爱过他们。

**约书亚的继承（第9节）**

> "嫩的儿子约书亚满有智慧的灵，因为摩西曾按手在他身上；以色列人就听从他，照着耶和华吩咐摩西的行了。"

弟兄姐妹，"满有智慧的灵"——弟兄姐妹，约书亚的领导力不是来自他的才能——而是来自神所赐的灵。弟兄姐妹，伟大领袖的继承人需要神的灵——不只是人的委任。

**摩西的无与伦比（第10-12节）**

> "以后以色列中再没有兴起先知像摩西的；他是耶和华面对面所认识的，耶和华差遣他在埃及地向法老和他众臣仆并他全地行各样神迹奇事，又在以色列众人眼前显大能的手行一切大而可畏之事。"

弟兄姐妹，"以后以色列中再没有兴起先知像摩西的"——弟兄姐妹，这句话是整个旧约最高的历史评价。弟兄姐妹，摩西的独特性在于"面对面所认识的"——神与他面对面说话——不是在异梦中——不是在异象中——而是直接地、清晰地。弟兄姐妹，但这句话也是一个悬念——旧约的结束是一个问题：那位像摩西的先知在哪里？弟兄姐妹，申命记18:15摩西自己说"耶和华你的神要从你们弟兄中间给你兴起一位先知像我——你们要听从他。"弟兄姐妹，那位"像摩西"的先知就是耶稣。

弟兄姐妹，摩西五经就以这个悬念结束——以色列在等待——世界在等待——那位更大的先知、更伟大的立法者、更完全的中保要来了。弟兄姐妹，那就是耶稣基督——祂来了——"律法是藉着摩西传的，恩典和真理都是由耶稣基督来的"（约1:17）。

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 34 is the conclusion of the entire Pentateuch—my death. From Genesis 1:1 "In the beginning God created" to here—from creation to death—from Eden to Nebo—from beginning to end. The most solemn scene in the OT.

**The View from Mount Nebo (vv. 1-4)**

From the summit—I saw the entire promised land—north to south, east to west. A peculiar experience—seeing but unable to enter. "I have let you see it with your eyes, but you shall not go over there." One leader's sin—changed his entire destiny. But God didn't let me die in despair—He let me see first. Seeing is enough—vision matters more than achievement—not everyone enters what they see—but seeing is having received it.

**God Himself Buries Moses (vv. 5-6)**

"And he buried him...but no one knows the place of his burial to this day." Why hide my tomb? God knows our nature—if known, that place would become a pilgrimage site—people worshiping my bones. God protects my posthumous honor—preventing me from becoming an idol. A prophet's glory is to glorify God—not oneself—even in death, protected from being deified.

**Moses' Physical Condition (v. 7)**

"Moses was 120 years old when he died. His eye was undimmed, and his vigor was unabated." At 120—still clear sight, clear mind. God doesn't just let you live—He lets you live with dignity—alert to complete your mission.

**Israel's Mourning and Joshua's Rise (vv. 8-9)**

Thirty days of national mourning—the longest in Israel's history. Good leaders' deaths cause genuine weeping—not protocol. Whether you're mourned this way depends on whether you truly loved them.

**Moses' Incomparability (vv. 10-12)**

"And there has not arisen a prophet since in Israel like Moses, whom the LORD knew face to face." The highest historical assessment in the OT. But this is also a cliffhanger—the OT ends with a question: where is that prophet like Moses? Deuteronomy 18:15 Moses said: "The LORD your God will raise up for you a prophet like me." That prophet is Jesus.

The Pentateuch ends with this suspense—Israel waiting—the world waiting—that greater prophet, greater lawgiver, more perfect mediator was coming. That is Jesus Christ—"For the law was given by Moses, but grace and truth came by Jesus Christ." (John 1:17).

## 历史背景（中文）

尼波山（Mount Nebo）在今天约旦境内，从考古发现看，早期基督教在这里建有拜占庭教堂纪念摩西。摩西坟墓的"隐藏"在犹太传统中有丰富的解经——犹大书9节提到天使长米迦勒与魔鬼争夺摩西的身体：摩西死了三十天的国丧与亚伦死后相同（民20:29），与后来大卫王死的国丧也是三十天。申命记34:10的"以后以色列中再没有兴起先知像摩西的"与申命记18:15的预言形成了圣经文学上的张力——暗示有一位"更像摩西的先知"还未出现。早期基督教将这一预言指向基督——约翰福音1:21法利赛人问施洗约翰"你是那先知吗？"——就是指申命记18:15所预言的那位。

## Historical Context (English)

Mount Nebo is in modern Jordan; archaeological evidence shows early Christians built a Byzantine church here commemorating Moses. Moses' hidden tomb is richly interpreted in Jewish tradition—Jude verse 9 mentions archangel Michael contending with the devil over Moses' body. Moses' thirty-day mourning matches Aaron's (Num 20:29) and later King David's. Deuteronomy 34:10's "no prophet like Moses" creates literary tension with Deuteronomy 18:15's prophecy—implying a "more Moses-like prophet" is yet to come. Early Christianity applied this to Christ—John 1:21's Pharisees asking John the Baptist "Are you the Prophet?"—referring to Deuteronomy 18:15.

## Structure (English)

**View from Mount Nebo (vv. 1-4)**—Climbed Nebo → saw all the land → Gilead to Dan → Naphtali → Ephraim/Manasseh → Judah → Negeb → plain → LORD said "this is the land" → you see with your eyes → you shall not go over.
**Moses' Death (vv. 5-7)**—Died in Moab → buried opposite Beth-peor → no one knows tomb → 120 years old → eye undimmed → vigor unabated.
**Israel's Mourning (v. 8)**—Thirty days mourning → mourning days complete.
**Joshua's Succession (v. 9)**—Full of wisdom's spirit → Moses laid hands → Israel listened to him.
**Moses' Incomparability (vv. 10-12)**—No prophet like Moses → known face to face → signs/wonders in Egypt → great and awesome deeds before Israel.

## 神学要点（中文）

**摩西看见却不能进入——成就的局限**
改革宗传统教导神有时允许我们"看见"而不"进入"——以塑造我们的信仰和谦卑。韦敏斯德信条第5章论护理。摩西看见了应许之地却不能进入——这不是神的残忍——而是神的目的。弟兄姐妹，人生中许多伟大的工作者没有亲眼看见工作的完成——但他们的劳苦没有徒然。"看见了"本身就是神的恩赐。

**神亲手埋葬摩西——神对仆人的尊荣**
改革宗传统教导神荣耀敬畏他的人。韦敏斯德信条第33章。神亲自埋葬了摩西——这是何等的荣耀。同时神隐藏了坟墓——保护摩西不被神化。弟兄姐妹，神既给仆人最高的荣耀——也保护仆人不被偶像化。

**"以后再没有像摩西的先知"——旧约的悬念与期待**
改革宗传统教导旧约指向基督。韦敏斯德信条第7章。申命记34:10是旧约最重要的"悬念"之一——"再没有"暗示"将来会有"——那位更伟大的摩西正在等待。弟兄姐妹，旧约以期待结束——新约以成就回答。

## Theology (English)

**Moses Saw But Couldn't Enter—Achievement's Limits**
The Reformed tradition teaches God sometimes allows us to "see" but not "enter"—to shape faith and humility. Westminster Confession chapter 5. Moses saw the promised land but couldn't enter—not God's cruelty—but God's purpose. Many great workers didn't see their work's completion—but their labor wasn't in vain. "Having seen" is itself God's gift.

**God Personally Buries Moses—God Honors His Servants**
The Reformed tradition teaches God honors those who fear Him. Westminster Confession chapter 33. God personally buried Moses—what honor. Yet God hid the tomb—protecting Moses from deification. God gives servants highest honor—while protecting them from idolization.

**"No More Prophets Like Moses"—OT's Cliffhanger and Expectation**
The Reformed tradition teaches the OT points to Christ. Westminster Confession chapter 7. Deuteronomy 34:10 is one of the OT's most important "cliffhangers"—"no more" implies "there will be"—that greater Moses is waiting. OT ends with expectation—NT answers with fulfillment.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"以后再没有像摩西的先知"——耶稣就是那位先知**——约翰福音6:14"众人看见耶稣所行的神迹，就说这真是那要到世间来的先知。"申命记34:10说"再没有"——约翰福音6:14说"这就是"——四十年前摩西在这里结束的悬念——在加利利海边被解答了。申命记18:15摩西说"耶和华要兴起一位先知像我"——耶稣就是应验了这个预言的那一位。

**摩西的死——基督的死与复活的比较**——约翰福音3:14"摩西在旷野怎样举蛇，人子也必照样被举起来。"摩西死去——但没有复活——他的坟墓是秘密。基督死去——但复活了——"为什么在死人中寻找活人呢？他不在这里，已经复活了"（路24:5-6）。摩西的死是结局——基督的死是开始。弟兄姐妹，在基督里没有最终的结局——只有永恒的开始。

**"恩典和真理都是由耶稣基督来的"——律法的完成**——约翰福音1:17"律法本是藉摩西传的，恩典和真理都是由耶稣基督来的。"整个摩西五经——创世记到申命记——都是指向基督的准备。弟兄姐妹，律法让你看见你的病——基督带来医治。律法让你看见应许之地（目的地）——基督带你进去。弟兄姐妹，摩西的五卷书读完了——但故事没有结束——因为一切的故事都指向那位"道成肉身居住在我们中间"的主。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"No More Prophets Like Moses"—Jesus Is That Prophet**—John 6:14: "The people, seeing the sign Jesus did, said 'This is indeed the Prophet who is to come!'" Deuteronomy 34:10 says "no more"—John 6:14 says "this is"—the cliffhanger Moses left was answered at the Sea of Galilee. Deuteronomy 18:15: "The LORD will raise up a prophet like me"—Jesus fulfilled this prophecy.

**Moses' Death Compared to Christ's Death and Resurrection**—John 3:14. Moses died—but didn't rise—his tomb is secret. Christ died—but rose—"And as they were afraid, and bowed down their faces to the earth, they said unto them, Why seek ye the living among the dead? He is not here, but is risen: remember how he spake unto you when he was yet in Galilee," (Luke 24:5-6). Moses' death was an ending—Christ's death was a beginning. In Christ there is no final ending—only eternal beginning.

**"Grace and Truth Came Through Jesus Christ"—Law's Completion**—John 1:17. The entire Pentateuch—Genesis through Deuteronomy—was preparation pointing to Christ. Law shows your disease—Christ brings healing. Law shows you the promised land—Christ brings you in. Moses' five books are complete—but the story isn't over—because all stories point to the One who "became flesh and dwelt among us."
