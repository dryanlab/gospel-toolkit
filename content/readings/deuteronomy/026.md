## 正文（中文）

弟兄姐妹，我是摩西。申命记第二十六章是律法部分（12-26章）的庄严结语——以两个美丽的仪式作为结束：献初熟果子时的信仰告白，以及十一奉献的声明。这一章告诉我们：敬拜不只是行动——还需要话语——用嘴巴说出你的信仰和感恩。

**初熟果子的信仰告白（第1-11节）**

> "你进去得了耶和华你神所赐你为业之地居住，就要从耶和华你神赐你的地上将所收的各种初熟的土产取些来放在筐子里……你要对祭司说：我今日向耶和华你的神明认我已来到耶和华向我们列祖起誓应许赐给我们的地。"

弟兄姐妹，每年收割的初熟果子要放在筐子里带到耶和华面前。弟兄姐妹，"初熟"——不是"剩余"——不是你吃完了把剩下的给神——而是第一份、最好的、还没有你自己享用的。弟兄姐妹，给神的必须是"初熟"——不是"剩余"。

弟兄姐妹，然后以色列人要说一段话——这段话被称为"以色列信经"——旧约中最早的信仰告白之一：

> "我祖原是一个将亡的亚兰人。他下到埃及寄居。人数稀少在那里却成了又大又强人数很多的国民。埃及人恶待我们苦害我们叫我们作苦工。于是我们哀求耶和华我们列祖的神。耶和华听见我们的声音……就用大能的手和伸出来的膀臂并大可畏的事与神迹奇事领我们出了埃及，将我们领进这地方，把这流奶与蜜之地赐给我们。"

弟兄姐妹，"我祖原是一个将亡的亚兰人"——弟兄姐妹，这是指雅各。弟兄姐妹，以色列的信仰告白从最低点开始——"将亡的"、"寄居的"、"受苦的"——然后讲到神的大能拯救——最后到了流奶与蜜之地。弟兄姐妹，这就是每一个信徒的故事：我曾经是将亡的——但神拯救了我——带我进入了恩典之地。

弟兄姐妹，每年献初熟果子的时候重复这段话——不是因为神需要被提醒——而是因为你需要被提醒。弟兄姐妹，丰收的时候最容易忘记——是谁给你的。

**十一奉献的宣告（第12-15节）**

> "每逢三年就是十分取一之年……你就要在耶和华你神面前说：我已将圣物从我家里拿出来给了利未人和寄居的与孤儿寡妇……我没有违背你的诫命也没有忘记。"

弟兄姐妹，每三年的特别十一奉献后——你要在神面前做一个声明："我按照你的吩咐做了。"弟兄姐妹，这不是骄傲——而是负责任地向神报告。弟兄姐妹，神信任你管理祂托付给你的财产——然后祂要你报告。

**你今日认耶和华为你的神（第16-19节）**

> "耶和华你的神今日吩咐你行这些律例典章。所以你要尽心尽性谨守遵行。你今日认耶和华为你的神，应许遵行他的道……耶和华今日照他所应许你的也认你为他的子民……使你得称赞、美名、尊荣，超乎他所造的万民之上。"

弟兄姐妹，"你今日认耶和华为你的神"和"耶和华今日认你为他的子民"——弟兄姐妹，这是双向的约！弟兄姐妹，不只是你承认神——神也承认你。弟兄姐妹，在这个世上你可能默默无闻——但万有的主宰说："你是我的子民——我赐给你称赞、美名、尊荣。"弟兄姐妹，这是何等的恩典！

弟兄姐妹，申命记第二十六章用信仰告白和庄严的宣告为律法部分画上句号。弟兄姐妹，律法不是冰冷的规条——律法的核心是约——约的核心是关系——"你是我的——我是你的。"

## Content (English)

Brothers and sisters, I am Moses. Deuteronomy 26 is the solemn conclusion of the law section (chs. 12-26)—ending with two beautiful ceremonies: the faith confession when offering firstfruits, and the tithe declaration. Worship isn't just action—it needs words—spoken faith and gratitude.

**Firstfruits Confession (vv. 1-11)**

"When you come into the land...you shall take some of the first of all the fruit of the ground...and put it in a basket..." Then declare: "A wandering Aramean was my father. And he went down into Egypt and sojourned there, few in number, and there he became a nation, great, mighty, and populous. And the Egyptians treated us harshly...Then we cried to the LORD...and the LORD brought us out of Egypt with a mighty hand...and he brought us into this place and gave us this land."

"A wandering Aramean"—referring to Jacob. Israel's confession starts at rock bottom—"wandering," "sojourning," "suffering"—then God's mighty rescue—finally the land flowing with milk and honey. Every believer's story: I was once perishing—but God saved me—brought me into the land of grace.

Repeating this yearly—not because God needs reminding—but because you do. Harvest time is when you most easily forget—who gave it to you.

**Mutual Recognition (vv. 16-19)**

> "You have declared today that the LORD is your God" and "the LORD has declared today that you are his treasured people." A two-way covenant! Not only you acknowledge God—God acknowledges you. In this world you may be nobody—but the universe's sovereign says: "You are my people—I give you praise, fame, and honor."

Deuteronomy 26 closes the law section with confession and solemn declaration. Law isn't cold regulation—law's core is covenant—covenant's core is relationship—"you are mine—I am yours."

## 历史背景（中文）

"我祖原是一个将亡的亚兰人"被学者称为"小历史信经"（kleines historisches Credo）——是以色列最古老、最简洁的信仰告白之一。"将亡的亚兰人"（arami oved）——"oved"可以翻译为"将亡的"或"漂泊的"——指雅各在亚兰（叙利亚）地区的寄居经历。犹太逾越节家宴（Seder）的"哈加达"（Haggadah）就以这段经文为核心——每年逾越节晚宴上诵读和解释这段经文。"初熟的果子"（bikkurim）的献祭在犹太传统中是七七节（五旬节）的重要仪式——朝圣者带着装饰精美的果篮前往耶路撒冷圣殿。

## Historical Context (English)

"A wandering Aramean was my father" is called the "small historical creed" (kleines historisches Credo) by scholars—one of Israel's oldest, most concise faith confessions. "Wandering Aramean" (arami oved)—refers to Jacob's sojourning in Aram (Syria). The Jewish Passover Seder's Haggadah centers on this text—read and explained every Passover eve. "Firstfruits" (bikkurim) offering was an important Feast of Weeks (Pentecost) ceremony—pilgrims brought beautifully decorated fruit baskets to Jerusalem's temple.

## 经文结构（中文）

**初熟果子的仪式（1-11节）**——进入应许之地→取初熟土产放在筐子里→交给祭司→宣告信仰告白："我祖原是将亡的亚兰人"→述说出埃及→耶和华领我们进美地→将果子供在耶和华面前→你和利未人寄居的一同欢乐。
**十一奉献的声明（12-15节）**——第三年的十一奉献→给利未人寄居的孤儿寡妇→向耶和华声明：我按你的话行了→没有违背也没有忘记→求你眷顾你的百姓。
**双向的约（16-19节）**——今日你认耶和华为你的神→今日耶和华认你为他的子民→使你得称赞美名尊荣→超乎万民→你是归耶和华为圣的子民。

## Structure (English)

**Firstfruits Ceremony (vv. 1-11)**—Enter promised land → take first produce in basket → give to priest → declare creed: "wandering Aramean" → recount Exodus → LORD brought us to good land → set fruit before LORD → rejoice with Levite and sojourner.
**Tithe Declaration (vv. 12-15)**—Third-year tithe → give Levites/sojourners/orphans/widows → declare before LORD: I obeyed → didn't violate or forget → look down and bless.
**Mutual Covenant (vv. 16-19)**—Today you declare LORD is your God → Today LORD declares you His people → gives you praise/fame/honor → above all nations → you are holy to the LORD.

## 神学要点（中文）

**信仰告白——话语的重要性**
改革宗传统教导信仰需要公开的告白。韦敏斯德信条第21章论敬拜。"你要……说"——敬拜不只是内心的——还需要口头的宣告。罗马书10:9-10"你若口里认耶稣为主心里信神叫他从死里复活就必得救。因为人心里相信就可以称义口里承认就可以得救。"信心与告白是不可分的。

**初熟——不是剩余而是首先**
改革宗传统教导奉献应当是优先的而非剩余的。韦敏斯德信条第21章。"初熟"——第一份给神——不是剩下的。这反映了优先次序：神不是生活的附加——而是生活的首位。箴言3:9"你要以财物和一切初熟的土产尊荣耶和华"。

**双向的约——关系性的信仰**
改革宗圣约神学的核心是关系。韦敏斯德信条第7章。"你认耶和华为你的神""耶和华认你为他的子民"——约不是单方面的——而是双向的承诺。这不是合同——而是关系——"你是我的——我是你的"。

## Theology (English)

**Faith Confession—The Importance of Words**
The Reformed tradition teaches faith requires public confession. Westminster Confession chapter 21. Romans 10:9-10. Faith and confession are inseparable.

**Firstfruits—Not Leftovers but First**
The Reformed tradition teaches giving should be priority, not surplus. Westminster Confession chapter 21.: Firstfruits—the first portion to God—not what's left. Proverbs 3:9.

**Mutual Covenant—Relational Faith**
Reformed covenant theology's core is relationship. Westminster Confession chapter 7.: You declare the LORD / "the LORD declares you"—covenant isn't unilateral—but mutual commitment. Not contract—but relationship.

## 基督的影子（中文）

**"我祖原是将亡的"——基督拯救将亡的人**——以弗所书2:1-5"你们死在过犯罪恶之中……然而神既有丰富的怜悯……便叫我们与基督一同活过来。"每个以色列人都承认"我祖原是将亡的"——每个基督徒也承认"我原是死在罪恶中的"——但神拯救了我们。

**初熟的果子——基督是初熟的果子**——哥林多前书15:20"但基督已经从死里复活成为睡了之人初熟的果子。"以色列人献初熟的果子给神——基督是从死里复活的"初熟果子"——祂的复活保证了我们将来的复活。

**双向的约——基督与教会的联合**——雅歌2:16"良人属我我也属他。"申命记的"你认耶和华为神耶和华认你为子民"在基督里成为了最亲密的联合——基督属教会教会属基督。这不只是法律上的约——而是爱的联合。

## Christ's Shadow (English)

**"My Father Was a Wandering Aramean"—Christ Saves the Perishing**—Ephesians 2:1-5: "You were dead in trespasses...But God, being rich in mercy...made us alive together with Christ." Every Israelite confesses "my father was perishing"—every Christian confesses "I was dead in sin"—but God saved us.

**Firstfruits—Christ Is the Firstfruits**—1 Corinthians 15:20: "But in fact Christ has been raised from the dead, the firstfruits of those who have fallen asleep." Israel offered firstfruits to God—Christ is the "firstfruits" of resurrection—His resurrection guarantees our future resurrection.

**Mutual Covenant—Christ and Church's Union**—Song of Solomon 2:16: "My beloved is mine, and I am his." Deuteronomy's "you declare the LORD" / "the LORD declares you" becomes in Christ the most intimate union—Christ belongs to the church, the church belongs to Christ. Not just legal covenant—but love's union.
